LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  ****************************************************************
   * USERS AFFECTED:                                              *
   * ALL IMPACT USERS                                             *
   ****************************************************************
   * PROBLEM DESCRIPTION:                                         *
   * 1. derby.log deleted when copied from Primary to Secondary   *
   * (when secondary starts)                                      *
   *                                                              *
   * db/<server>/derbyderby.log should exist for all started      *
   * Derby instances (even if in slave mode).                     *
   *                                                              *
   * 2. If host names do not match those in the ImpactDB.ds file, *
   * Impact cannot start up                                       *
   *                                                              *
   *                                                              *
   * The issue here is that it is not obvious what the server is  *
   * reporting the servername as. So it's not easy for customers  *
   * to fix.                                                      *
   ****************************************************************
   * RECOMMENDATION:                                              *
