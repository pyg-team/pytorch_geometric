Title: IBM WebSphere Portal site is accessible, however no content is displayed. - United States

Text:
icm.properties; IConnectionManager.logon() TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM
The portal site is loading but it is blank , with no content. We have
tailed the logs and whenever the site loads it throws jdbc and ORA
errors.

SYMPTOM
the website does not display any WCM content.


CAUSE
there is a permissions issue for JCR during startup of the JavaContentRepository (JCR) and WCM applications

ENVIRONMENT
WebSphere Portal Server 8.0.0

have no values assigned.



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Update the following parameters in icm.properties:

jcr.database.name=
jcr.database.userid=
jcr.database.password=
jcr.database.schema=

save the changes, and restart WebSphere_Portal.
