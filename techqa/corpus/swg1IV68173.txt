   USERS AFFECTED:

   Platforms affected:
   Windows

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  When an XMS.NET application is running, XMS Common Services get
   initialized everytime when the trace call is made,rather
   commonservices should be initialized only once during the
   lifetime of an application
