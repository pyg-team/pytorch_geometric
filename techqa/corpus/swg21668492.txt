Title: IBM Approver not receiving emails when a hold notice is sent for approval - United States

Text:
Approver Hold Notices Preferences Alerts Pending Approval Atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does the approver not receive any email notifications when a hold Notice is sent for approval in Atlas?, 

CAUSE
Under Preferences for the approver, "Send copy of My Alerts to my email address" option is set to No

ANSWER
 

 1. Edit the Preferences for the approver 
 2. Select the check box for "Send copy of My Alerts to my email address" option. 
 3.  Save and Close.
