Title: IBM SPSS Statistics on Mac OS displaying "JVM Not Found" when starting. - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 You have installed IBM SPSS Statistics on a Mac OS host machine. When starting Statistics, an error message including "JVM Not Found" or 'JVMNotFound' is displayed. 

CAUSE
This behavior is most frequently causes by deployment of Statistics to a unsupported/incompatible version of Mac OS. 

ANSWER
Check the Product Compatibility Report for the installed version of Statistics to determine if the version of Statistics is compatible with the version of Mac OS:
