Title: IBM Viewing Policy Execution Reports in StoredIQ - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do we view Policy Execution Reports in StoredIQ? 

CAUSE
Unable to find the location to view generated Policy Execution Reports

ANSWER
 

 1. Login to StoredIQ Dataserver 
 2. Navigate to the Inbox link located next to help icon on the top right corner 
 3. Clicking the link brings up a list with links to the reports 
 4. Clicking on one of the report links will allow you to download the report
