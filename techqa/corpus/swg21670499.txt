Title: IBM Education: Webcast on Basic Rulerunner Configuration & Troubleshooting for IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture - United States

ABSTRACT
 On Thursday, May 15, 2014, a Connect With the Experts presentation on Basic Rulerunner Configuration & Troubleshooting was delivered by Rick Wilson with an open question and answer session following the presentation. 

CONTENT
The presentation provided the initial steps needed to set up a Rulerunner Server so that it will reliably process batches. Also included were some basic Rulerunner troubleshooting steps. Optimizing Rulerunner to increase throughput will be covered in a separate session.

Level of Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate

Presenter: 
Rick Wilson, Datacap Technical Support Engineer and Datacap 8.1 Certified Solution Designer
