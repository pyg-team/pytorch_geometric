ABSTRACT
 The IBM License Metric Tool technical support team shared information on how to effectively get help and be helpful in the dW Answers forum at IBM developerWorks. They also told about the IBM Security Learning Academy and toured the IBM Security Support channel on YouTube 

CONTENT
For this Open Mic session, Sherrie Clarke and Marla Morris showed the registration process and how to get started as a dW Answers user, as well as best practices for using the forum effectively.


Sherrie also explained how to find other self-help materials, including courses in the Security Learning Academy and videos on the IBM Security Support YouTube channel.
