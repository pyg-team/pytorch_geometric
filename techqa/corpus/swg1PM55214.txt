LOCAL FIX
 *  As a temporary workaround, customers can make the following
   
   changes after backing up their files:
   
   
   
   
   
   * in their custom theme, find the Plain.jsp file
   
   
   
   
   
   <profile_root>/installedApps/<node>/<customtheme&
   gt;.ear/<customtheme>
   
   .war/themes/html/Plain.jsp
   
   
   
   
   
   * modify it as follows (remove the extra } symbol)
   
   
   
   
   
   BEFORE
   
   
   --%><c:otherwise><c:set var="themeTemplateURI"
   
   
   value="${themeWebDAVBaseURI}Plain.html}"
   /></c:otherwise><%--
   
   
   
   
   AFTER
   
   
   --%><c:otherwise><c:set var="themeTemplateURI"
   
   
   value="${themeWebDAVBaseURI}Plain.html"
   /></c:otherwise><%--
   
   
   
   
   * ensure that JSP reloading is enabled so the change is picked
   up or
   
   have them re-start portal
   
   
   
   
   
   * refresh their browser and re-try editing shared settings for
   the
   
   JSR286 LRP while in manage pages
