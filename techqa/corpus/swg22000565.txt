Title: IBM FileNet Application Engine (AE) is not affected by the Apache Struts 2.x Vulnerability (CVE-2017-5638) - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 IBM FileNet Application Engine (AE) is not vulnerable to the Apache Struts 2.x Vulnerability. 

CONTENT
IBM FileNet Application Engine (AE) in all editions and all platforms is NOT vulnerable to Apache Struts 2.x Vulnerability (CVE-2017-5638). 
IBM recommends that you review your entire environment and take appropriate mitigation and remediation action.
