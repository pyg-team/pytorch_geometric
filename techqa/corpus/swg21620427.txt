Title: IBM WfM announcement  - 5724-S42 Netcool/OMNIbus Gateway v1.0.0 - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT LIFECYCLE

ABSTRACT
 Withdraw from Marketing announcement - 5724-S42 Netcool/OMNIbus Gateway v1.0.0 

CONTENT
There have been two recent announcements related to the Netcool/OMNIbus Gateways. This note is intended to show that these announcements do not have any adverse impact to our customers. 

The first announcement was the IBM United States Software Announcement letter 212-459 dated 27th November 2012 covering Tivoli Netcool/OMNIbus 7.4 which confirmed that the Netcool/OMNIbus gateways have been added to Netcool/OMNIbus 7.4. 

The second, IBM United States Software Announcement letter 212-459 dated 11th December 2012, confirmed that the older gateway parts will be withdrawn from marketing on the 12th April 2013. 

Key points 

§ There are no functional changes to the gateways. 

§ Current Netcool/Gateway parts and downloads remain available for transition until April 2013. 

§ Support for the gateway components will not be interrupted by these announcements. 

§ The change is simply a packaging exercise to complete the simplification of the Netcool/OMNIbus product structure into a single set. 

§ New enablement metrics have been created in Netcool/OMNIbus covering entitlement for integrations by gateway and/or API connections 

– Event Forwarding (formerly uni-directional Tier 1 and Tier 1 3rd Party gateways) 

– Data Exchange (formerly bi-direction Tier 2 gateway) 

– API exclusions - 

§ Integration to Tivoli components (no charge) 

§ Integration to GUI interfaces (no charge) 

§ Integration for event collection from managed devices (covered by the current requirement to entitle according to the scope of the managed environment) 

§ Existing customer gateway entitlements will be migrated automatically at renewal
