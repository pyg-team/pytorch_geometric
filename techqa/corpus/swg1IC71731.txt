Title: IBM IC71731: ROLES AND PERMISSIONS ARE NOT DOCUMENTED IN THE TIVOLI INTEGRATED PORTAL - United States

Text:
AIX SUBSCRIBE
You can track all active APARs for this component.



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS DOCUMENTATION ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The tsmAdministrator and tsmUser roles and permissions are not
   documented in the Tivoli Integrated Portal help files or in the
   Administrators Guide. The tsmAdministrator has full
   access/permission to all functions in the Tivoli Integrated
   Portal. The tsmUser does not have access/permission for "server
   properties" and "client schedules."
   
   Customer/L2 Diagnostics (if applicable)
   None
   
   *NOTE*
   None
   
   Platforms affected:
   Tivoli Storage Manager version 6.2, all platforms.
   
   Initial Impact: Low
   
   Additional Keywords: TEMS Tivoli Integrated Portal ZZ62
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  ****************************************************************
   * USERS AFFECTED: All Tivoli Storage Manager Administration    *
   *                 Center users.                                *
   ****************************************************************
   * PROBLEM DESCRIPTION: The tsmUser role does not have access   *
   *                      to all Administration Center            *
   *                      components. Documentation states that   *
   *                      the tsmUser role does have access to    *
   *                      all Administration Center components.   *
   *                                                              *
   *                      Users who are utilizing the tsmUser     *
   *                      role must move any IDs that have the    *
   *                      tsmUser role from tsmUser to            *
   *                      tsmAdministrator.                       *
   ****************************************************************
   * RECOMMENDATION:                                              *
   ****************************************************************
   The tsmUser role has limited access to the Tivoli Storage
   Manager components.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION

TEMPORARY FIX
