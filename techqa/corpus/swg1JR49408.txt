PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  When creating a new (e.g. ad-hoc) group, group related caches
   get reset and one of them gets preloaded with available groups.
   If the number of groups is large, a significant performance
   degradation can occur.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Upon group creation, no cache reset is triggered any more. Cache
   resets are now limited to group (property) updates.  In
   addition, resets are limited to the concerned groups and no
   preload is done as consequence of a cache reset.
