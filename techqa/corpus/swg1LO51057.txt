Title: IBM LO51057: UNDERSCORE MISSING FROM EMAIL ADDRESS ON NOKIA DEVICES. - United States

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  If an email address in the From, To, CC, or Bcc fields contain
   an underscore AND also contain a non-ASCII character, such as a
   german umlaut, then the underscore will not appear on the email
   editor and viewer on the Nokia device once Traveler syncs the
   email to the device.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  The problem is caused by a bug in a Nokia decoding API.  The bug
   is being worked around.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customer stated that the e-mail address for Nokia devices was
   not correct so they could not send mail from the device.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The problem was not seen on the latest maintenance level.  Asked
   customer to upgrade.  Customer stated they decided to use
   Blackberry and wanted the problem closed.
   
   
    
   
   

TEMPORARY FIX
 *  NA
