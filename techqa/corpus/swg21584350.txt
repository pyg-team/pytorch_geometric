Title: IBM Scanning Driver Support with IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture 8.0.1 - United States

Text:
Datacap scanner support TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What scanner drivers are supported with IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture 8.0.1? 

ANSWER
IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture 8.0.1 supports scanning directly through both the ISIS and TWAIN APIs. The following types of scanning are supported. 

 * Thick client (DotScan) ISIS scan. 
 * Thick client ISIS fixup. 
 * Thin client (Taskmaster Web) TWAIN scan. 
 * Thick client with Kofax VRS drivers underneath pixkofax ISIS. 
 * Thin client with Kofax VRS drivers underneath TWAIN.


Support for thick client Kofax scan (kScan) has been removed as of version 8.0. For scanning driver support with 8.1, see Hardware and Software Requirements for IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture Version 8.1 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?&uid=swg27022094#SCAN].
