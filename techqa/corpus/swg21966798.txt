Title: IBM Why is the Datacap Navigator 9.0 Scan button disabled after I upgraded IBM Content Navigator 2.0.3? - United States

Text:
ICN; scan.js; Datacap; Content Navigator TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After upgrading to ICN Feature Pack 3, the Datacap Navigator Scan button was disabled. How do I fix that? 

CAUSE
The objects were cached on the client machine.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Clearing the Java applet cache on the workstation, and clearing Websphere temp directory fixed the issue.
