Title: IBM Incorrect behavior occurs when you use Microsoft Internet Explorer 10 with IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) and IBM Business Monitor - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When you use IBM Business Process Manager V7.5.1 Fix Pack 2, IBM Business Process Manager V8.0.1 Fix Pack 2, IBM Business Monitor V7.5.1 Fix Pack 2, or IBM Business Monitor V8.0.1 Fix Pack 2 with Microsoft Internet Explorer 10, you might experience problems with widgets and see messages about the browser being unsupported. 

SYMPTOM
For information about the symptoms of the problem, refer to the table of interim fixes in the Resolving the problem section.


CAUSE
The following products do not support the use of Microsoft Internet Explorer 10: 

 * IBM Business Process Manager V7.5.1 Fix Pack 2 
 * IBM Business Process Manager V8.0.1 Fix Pack 2 
 * IBM Business Monitor V7.5.1 Fix Pack 2 
 * IBM Business Monitor V8.0.1 Fix Pack 2

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The following interim fixes must be installed for IBM BPM and IBM Business Monitor to function properly when you use these products with Internet Explorer 10:
