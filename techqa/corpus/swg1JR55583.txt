LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  While the BPMConfig command runs on a Sun Solaris platform, a
    method is called with a null parameter; however, the method
   does not accept null parameters. As a result, you receive the
   NullPointerException.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  A fix that enhances the BPMConfig command on Sun Solaris
   platforms is planned for inclusion in an upcoming IBM BPM
   V8.5.7.0 cumulative fix.
