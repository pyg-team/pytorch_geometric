Title: IBM Tivoli Common Reporting (TCR) v3.1.2/3.1.2.1: Unable to modify or configure datasource connection details to BIRT reports - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Tivoli Common Reporting (TCR) v3.1.2/3.1.2.1: Unable to modify or configure datasource connection details to BIRT reports 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
1. Go to <TCR_HOME>/bin 

 Windows: <TCR_HOME> = C:\Program Files\IBM\JazzSM\reporting
Unix: <TCR_HOME> = /opt/IBM/JazzSM/reporting

2. Edit trcmd.bat/sh file and go to line 
Windows: BIRT_LIB=%TCR_ADDONS_DIR%/birt/birt-runtime-2_2_2/ReportEngine/lib
Unix: BIRT_LIB=${TCR_ADDONS_DIR}/birt/birt-runtime-2_2_2/ReportEngine/lib

3. Replace with below line
Windows: BIRT_LIB=%TCR_HOME%\lib\birt-runtime-2_2_2\ReportEngine\lib
Unix: BIRT_LIB=${TCR_HOME}/lib/birt-runtime-2_2_2/ReportEngine/lib

4. Save the file
