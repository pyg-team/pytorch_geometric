APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  ReturnParameters in SCOrchestrator_Toolkit cannot handle large
   outputParameterObject
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  ****************************************************************
   * USERS AFFECTED:
   All users of SmartCloud Orchestrator 2.3.0.0 or 2.3.0.1
   ****************************************************************
   * PROBLEM DESCRIPTION:
   SmartCloud Orchestrator cannot handle responses
   larger than 23KB.
   ****************************************************************
   * RECOMMENDATION:
   For permanent resolution apply SmartCloud Orchestrator 2.3.0.1
   iFix 3.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  SmartCloud Orchestrator cannot handle responses
   larger than 23KB. For larger response data the
   "ReturnParameter" call hangs and is detected
   to be hung after ~10 minutes.
   In the BPM WAS log you will see messages similar
   to the ones below:
   
   [2/14/14 8:37:24:706 GMT] 00000101 wle_javascrip I
   GenericRest - method=PUT
   [2/14/14 8:37:24:706 GMT] 00000101 wle_javascrip I
   GenericRest - uri=/kernel/tasks/1805
   [2/14/14 8:48:39:689 GMT] 000000ad ThreadMonitor W
   WSVR0605W: Thread "WebContainer : 6" (00000101)
   has been active for 675139 milliseconds and
   may be hung.
   There is/are 2 thread(s) in total in the server that may
   be hung.
      at java.net.SocketInputStream.socketRead0(Native Method)
      at
   java.net.SocketInputStream.read(SocketInputStream.java:140)
      at com.ibm.jsse2.b.a(b.java:167)
      at com.ibm.jsse2.b.a(b.java:40)
      at com.ibm.jsse2.SSLSocketImpl.a(SSLSocketImpl.java:752)
      at com.ibm.jsse2.SSLSocketImpl.a(SSLSocketImpl.java:320)
      at com.ibm.jsse2.f.read(f.java:21)
   
   The fix enables to store larger string values (>23K) for
   parameter OperationParameter.
   
   The fix for this APAR will be contained in the following
   maintenance packages:
   | ifix | 2.3.0.1-CSI-ISCO-IF0003 applicable for SmartCloud
   Orchestrator 2.3.0 Fix Pack 1 or SmartCloud Orchestrator
   Enterprise 2.3.0 Fix Pack 1
   
   
    
   
   

TEMPORARY FIX
