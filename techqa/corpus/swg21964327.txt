Title: IBM Cognos Planning 10.1.1 FP5 and 10.1 IF4 are now available - United States

Text:
 NEWS

ABSTRACT
 Cognos Planning 10.1.1 Fix Pack 5 and Cognos Planning 10.1 Interim Fix 4 are now available to supported customers. 

CONTENT
Information about downloading and installing the new fix pack and interim fix is available on their respective download pages: 

 * IBM Cognos Planning 10.1.1 Fix Pack 5 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24040441] 
 * IBM Cognos Planning 10.1 Interim Fix 4 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24040501]


These releases address security vulnerabilities. Details on the vulnerabilities are available in the Security Bulletins: 
 * Security Bulletin: Vulnerability in RC4 stream cipher affects IBM Cognos Planning (CVE-2015-2808) [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21902576] 
 * Security Bulletin: IBM Cognos Planning is affected by multiple vulnerabilities [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21964676]
