Title: IBM WebSphere Adapter for IBM i inbound adapter receives binding exception - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
Customer tried to configure the inbound adapter for WebSphere Adapter for IBM i to get the message from the dataqueue on System i. While setting the object information, the customer received an exception when selecting "Export" for the test. It failed to connect to the dataqueue and dumped the error message in FFDC logs.

for "emitDataq1" and then he could avoid this issue successfully. 

CAUSE
This has been identified as a product defect under APAR JR37522

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In the EMD while creating the service, in the "connection properties screen", customer provided a 
prefix for BO name instead of leaving it to the default value null. This has been fixed in APAR JR37522 in WebSphere Adapter for IBM i 7.0.0.1.Interim Fix 01 or above. Please contact IBM Support for the Interim Fix.
