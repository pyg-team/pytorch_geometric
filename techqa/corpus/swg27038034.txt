The following integration services have been deprecated in this release:

 * Administration Services in Jazz for Service Management
 * Security Services in Jazz for Service Management
 * Registry Services in Jazz for Service Management

 [javascript:void()]Important: Only fresh installations of Jazz for Service Management Version 1.1.3.0 will not include the deprecated services. Customers upgrading from earlier versions of Jazz for Service Management that utilize any of the deprecated services can upgrade without losing access to those services. For fresh installations that require one of more of the deprecated services, you must install Jazz for Service Management Version 1.1.2.1 and then upgrade to the latest version.
