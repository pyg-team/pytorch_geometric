Title: IBM How to import an alert template - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 RTM can export/import templates in Web Portal->Console->Import/Export->Import Template. However, you can import graph template, data template, host Template, ELIM Template and data query. You want to know how to import the alert template. 

ANSWER
 

You can follow procedure below to import an alert templateï¼š

1. In Spectrum LSF RTM Web Portal, click Console->Templates->Alert Template.

2. In Alert Templates Management tab, click import button.

3. In Alert template import tab, click "Choose file" to locate your alert template.

4. Click "Yes" button.

Now your template has been imported and you can find it in the Console->Templates->Alert Template-> Your alert template name tab.
