Title: IBM A File copied from IBM Sterling Connect:Direct for z/OS to IBM Sterling Connect:Direct for i5/OS the IFS file is missing the CRLF characters at the end of the records. - United States

Text:
z/OS i5/OS CRLF missing TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 A File copied from z/OS to i5/OS the IFS is missing the CRLF characters at the end of the records. 

SYMPTOM
File copied from z/OS to i5/OS the IFS is missing the CRLF characters at the end of the records .


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Add TEXTFILE(*YES) EORCHAR(0D0A) in the SYSOPTS for the i5/OS file. 

example is: SYSOPTS="TYPE(FILE) CODEPAGE(37,819) CCSID(819) TEXTFILE(*YES) EORCHAR(0D0A)")
