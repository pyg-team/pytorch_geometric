Title: IBM Text searchable item type operations such as create, update, and reindex are slower on Oracle Version 11.2.0.4 than 11.2.0.3 in AIX environments - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 IBM Content Manager 8.4.X or 8.5 operations (create, update, reindex) on text-searchable item types are slower in Oracle version 11.2.0.4 than 11.2.0.3. 

SYMPTOM
After you upgrade to Oracle 11.2.0.4 in an AIX environment, operations such as create, update, and reindex may perform slowly when run on text search item types.


CAUSE
This problem has been accepted by the Oracle support team, reference Bug# 19055653.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Contact Oracle support to obtain the fix.
