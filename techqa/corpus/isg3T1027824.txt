IBM Phpc SCF TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What is the latest version of IBM Platform HPC(PHPC)? 

ANSWER
IBM Platform HPC stands for IBM Platform High Performance Computing. The latest version of IBM Platform HPC is version 4.2.1.. 

IBM Spectrum Cluster Foundation(SCF) starts with version 4.2.2. It comes with Community Edition(CE) version only, and it bundles with IBM LSF Suites.
