Title: IBM Security: How to restrict the Cast Iron Appliance from getting accessed from an external IP - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can I restrict the Cast Iron Appliance from getting accessed from an external IP? 

ANSWER
We currently do not have a way of restricting access from an IP range on the appliance. IBM Support recommends using a standard firewall to achieve this result and putting the Cast Iron appliance behind your firewall.
