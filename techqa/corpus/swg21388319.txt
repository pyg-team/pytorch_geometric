Title: IBM Where can we change the path location of the filesys.ini? - United States

Text:
 

Technote (FAQ) Question Now in 8.2 and greater, in analyst you were able to change the location path of the filesys.ini in tools>options, but now it's greyed out. Answer In 8.2 and greater, to change the location path of the filesys.ini you need to go into the contributor admin console>tools>update filesys.ini options and make your change there.
