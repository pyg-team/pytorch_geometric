ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Description:
   
   If a user specifies table alignment on a table in a rich text
   field that appears on the viewer, when they view the policy the
   next section header floats to the side of the table.
   
   It can be reproduced using any of the two major browsers: IE,
   Chrome
   
   Prerequisites:
   
   OpenPages 7.1.0.1 with Solutions
   
   Assign profile
   1.	Log into the OpenPages UI as OpenPagesAdministrator
   2.	Navigate to Administration -> Profiles and click the
   OpenPages Modules 7.1.0 Master profile
   3.	Scroll down to the bottom and click on the Associate button
   for Associated Users heading
   4.	Tick OpenPagesAdministrator, then click Associate.
   5.	Log out and log back in as OpenPagesAdministrator
   
   Create basic entity structure and objects
   1.	Navigate to Organization -> Business Entities
   2.	Click on the Add New button
   3.	Enter Entity01 as the name and click Save
   4.	On the top right, click on the Add New button and select
   Policy
   5.	In the Parents tab, click on Search and enter Entity01 in
   the search field and click on Ent1 from the search results
   6.	In the General tab, enter Policy01 as the name (if not
   auto-named) and enter any mandatory fields, then click Save
   
   Steps to Reproduce:
   
   1.	Log into the OpenPages application server as
   OpenPagesAdministrator
   2.	Navigate to Policy Management -> Policies
   3.	In the simple filter, enter Policy01 and press enter.
   4.	Navigate to the detail page of  Policy01
   5.	Click on Actions-> Edit this Policy button.
   6.	Edit any of the Rich Text properties in the Policy Details:
   group, say Purpose: (click on the edit  button) .
   7.	Click on the Insert Table button   in the Rich Edit control
   toolbar.  Accept the defaults for all the properties.
   8.	Right click on the table, choose Table Properties, flip
   Align: to Left, click OK.
   9.	Enter some values in the 6 table cells, click Apply.
   10.	Click on the Save button.
   11.	Click on the View Policy hyperlink
   12.	Note the misalignment if the fields
