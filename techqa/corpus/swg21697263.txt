Title: IBM Near term history navigation for OMEGAMON XE on z/OS V5.3.0 - United States

Text:
near term history navigation attribute group NTH TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What near term history data is available for OMEGAMON XE on z/OS V5.3.0 and what navigation path do I use to see it? 

ANSWER
Eleven attribute groups represent the near-term history data that is available: 

 * Address Space CPU Utilization History 
 * Address Space Delay Summary History 
 * Common Storage Utilization History 
 * CPC LPAR Details History 
 * CPC LPAR Summary History 
 * KM5 Device Job History 
 * KM5 Device Resource History 
 * KM5 Storage Details History 
 * KM5 Storage Summary History 
 * Real Storage Utilization History 
 * WLM Service Class Resources History


Refer to the Attributes chapter in the OMEGAMON XE on z/OS: User's Guide for descriptions of the attribute groups and attributes:
