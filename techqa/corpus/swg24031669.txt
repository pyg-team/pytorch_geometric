MPL8: WebSphere MQ for Linux (zSeries) V7.1 - Performance Evaluations DOWNLOADABLE FILES

ABSTRACT
 MPL8 includes performance charts showing Performance measurements to present the performance capabilities of WebSphere MQ V7.1 and allow capacity planning. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
This SupportPac includes: 

• Performance measurements with figures and tables to present the performance capabilities of WebSphere MQ V7.1
• Interpretation of the results and their implications for designing or sizing WebSphere MQ configurations. 

Possible Uses
This SupportPac is designed for people who: 
• Will be designing and implementing solutions using WebSphere MQ for Linux (zSeries) V7.1
• Want to understand the performance limits of WebSphere on this platform 
• Want to understand how to tune WebSphere MQ on Linux (zSeries).

Skill Level Required
The reader should have a general awareness of the SuSE SLES 9 Operating System and of WebSphere MQ in order to make best use of this SupportPac.
