Title: IBM Support for SHA-2 hashing algorithm for Sterling Connect:Enterprise for UNIX - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is SHA-2 hashing algorithm supported by Sterling Connect:Enterprise for UNIX? 

ANSWER
Sterling Connect:Enterprise for UNIX version 2.5.03 iFix9 is certified to work with SHA256 (SHA-2) private and public certificates for FTP and AS2 (EDIINT and HTTP) daemons.
You can obtain iFix9 from the IBM Fix Central website at:
http://www-933.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/ [http://www-933.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/]
