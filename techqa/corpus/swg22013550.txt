Title: IBM TLS support for MS-SQL for BigFix Inventory or BigFix Compliance - United States

Text:
BigFix MSSQL BFI Compliance TLS; Transport Layer Security TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 How do you enable the communication between IBM BigFix Inventory or IBM BigFix Compliance server and its database with TLS (Transport Layer Security)? 

SYMPTOM
"Unable to initialize SSL connection. Unauthorized access was denied or security settings have expired. Exception is javax.net.ssl.SSLException:
Unrecognized SSL message, plaintext connection?"


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
To enable the communication between BigFix Inventory or BigFix Compliance server and its database with TLS enabled, add the following property to the 

<INSTALL_DIR>/wlp/usr/servers/server1/jvm.optionsfile 
on the BigFix Inventory or BigFix Compliance Server side and then restart the server:

-Dcom.ibm.jsse2.overrideDefaultTLS=true
