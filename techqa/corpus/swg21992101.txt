Title: IBM Hold Notice Confirmation report for an Inactive Atlas Hold Notice - United States

Text:
Atlas Hold Notice Confirmation Report TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can we run a Hold Notice Confirmation Report for an Inactive Atlas Hold Notice? 

CAUSE
The Hold Notice was made Inactive and the confirmation information was needed

ANSWER
 

Hold Notice Confirmation report works only for notice in Sent status as per the link below
http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSXPJK_6.0.3/com.ibm.aps.use.doc/apsmr048.html [http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSXPJK_6.0.3/com.ibm.aps.use.doc/apsmr048.html] 

You can follow the steps below to identify the Information in the Atlas database 

 1. Identify the Inactive Noticeid from the browser address bar 
 2. Login to Atlas Database as PSSAPL 
 3. Run the query after replacing the noticeid - select cl.*, p.firstname,p.lastname,p.loginid,p.email from noticeremindersent nrs,confirmation_logging cl, person p where nrs.noticereminderid= cl.noticereminderid and cl.userid=p.personid and nrs.noticeid=<notice_id>;
