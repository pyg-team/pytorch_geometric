Title: IBM Cannot execute a DVS test in a migrated workspace - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 JVM error when executing a Decision Validation Services (DVS) test in a workspace migrated from 7.1. 

SYMPTOM
Getting one of the following errors when executing a rule project with the 'DVS Excel file' launch configuration:


Java(TM) Platform SE Binary has stopped working. - Or - 
An unhandled win32 exception occurred in javaw.exe. 
CAUSE
As indicated in the documentation at Migrating from JRules > Migrating from JRules V7 > How to migrate from JRules V7 [http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/dmanager/v7r5/topic/com.ibm.decisions.migrating.jrules/Content/Business_Rules/_pubskel/Infocenter_Primary/ps_Decisions_Rules_Migrating40.html], you must import rule projects into a new workspace to migrate them.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Importing the projects to migrate into a new 7.5 workspace solves the issue.
