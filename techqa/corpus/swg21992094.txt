Atlas Matter History Person TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I find Historical Information for Atlas Matters? 

CAUSE
Identify change in Matter status

ANSWER
 

You will need access to the Atlas database in order to retrieve the information 

 1. Login to Atlas database as PSSAPL 
 2. Identify the Matterid from the browser address bar when you navigate to the Matter 
 3. Run the following query after replacing Matterid select lm.*,p.firstname,p.lastname,p.loginid from legalmatterhistory lm, person p where lm.legalmatterid=<matter-id>and lm.modifiedby=p.personid;
