Text:

ABSTRACT
 Members of the IBM License Metric Tool Support team shared information on deploying version 9.2 on Microsoft Windows Servers. After the presentation by Silviu Gligore, attendees took the opportunity to ask our panel of experts questions. Throughout the event, attendees commented and asked questions in the IBM Connections Cloud Meeting group web chat. 

CONTENT
This Open Mic session was the fourth webcast hosted by ILMT Technical Support. Our goal is to provide insight on how the License Metric Tool works and to teach on-going sessions that help both users and administrators understand, maintain, troubleshoot, and resolve issues. 

 * Topic: Successful deployment of ILMT 9.2 on Windows Servers 
 * Date: Tuesday, 15 December 2015 
 * Time: 11:00 AM EST (16:00 UTC/GMT, UTC-5 hours) for 60 minutes
