ABSTRACT
 This Open Mic presentation aims to keep the audience informed about SSL configurations and the latest security concerns on Domino.

Throughout the event, attendees will also be encouraged to comment or ask questions in the IBM Connections Meetings Web chat. Join us for this interactive, educational, lively session. Follow us on Twitter @IBM_ICSsupport.
