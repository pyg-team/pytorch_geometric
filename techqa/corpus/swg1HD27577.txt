ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  STCAI / CATParts do not switch to design mode when
   you double click on them after having saved the
   assembly to SMARTEAM.
   Scenario:
   1) Start CATIA in CACHE mode and connect to ST
   2) Open the Product 000011890 (from CATIA)
   3) Check some Parts in the tree: Double click on
   the parts 000011897, 000011888, 000011903 to load
   these parts in design mode - it is working well.
   Double click again on the parts 000011897,
   000011888, 000011903 to be sure, that they are
   back in visualisation mode.
   4) activate the main node 000011890 and select ST save
   5) now check again the nodes of the parts
   000011897, 000011888, 000011903. Double click on
   the nodes of 000011897, 000011888, 000011903
   Problem: the part won't be loaded in design mode
   and the icons in the CATIA tree are indicating,
   that they are no longer known by ST AND it is not
   possible to load these parts
   in design mode.
   .
