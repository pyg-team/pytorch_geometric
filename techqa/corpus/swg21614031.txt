Title: IBM How to configure People Picker Portlet to search by email - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM
How do you configure search in the People Picker Portlet using the email attribute?

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
1. Ensure that PK96710 is applied.
2. Log on to the WebSphere® Integrated Solutions Console.

3. Click Resources -> Resource Environment -> Resource Environment 
Providers. 

4. Find and click the WP PeopleService resource environment provider. The 
resource environment provider name is case sensitive.

5. Click custom properties. 

6. Click the custom properties page and configure the values for the 
following properties to email:

pickerPeopleSearchAttribute 
pickerGroupSearchAttribute 

7. Save changes. 

8. Restart IBM® WebSphere Portal server to reflect the changes.
