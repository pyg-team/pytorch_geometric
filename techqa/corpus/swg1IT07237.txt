APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  For a problem that causes DataPower to restart during appliance
   startup, information about the running Firmware Version or
   DateTimeStatus might be missing from the error report.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customer error reports sometimes do not contain key information
   such as date and time status.  This is due to a problem in the
   timing of when the error report was gathered.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Fix is available in 6.0.0.14, 6.0.1.10, 7.0.0.7 and 7.1.0.5.
