ABSTRACT
 The Support team talked about how to install or upgrade to the newly released IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) version 9.2.8. Attendees were given an opportunity to ask questions of the panel of experts. 

CONTENT
For this open mic session, Silviu Gligore and his teammates provided a collection of resources so you can install the latest ILMT/BFI update successfully.
