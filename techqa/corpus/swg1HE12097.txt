APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  In the IBM Content Collector web form that is used to collect
   additional archiving information, email documents are sometimes
   displayed with empty subject lines. The problem occurs if the
   subject line of one of the email documents contains a carriage
   return.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  N/A
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Caused by an error in the Domino template that is delivered with
    IBM Content Collector, email documents are sometimes displayed
   with empty subject lines in the web form that is used to collect
    additional archiving information. The problem occurs if the
   subject line of one of the email documents contains a carriage
   return.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Starting with IBM Content Collector 3.0.0 Fix Pack 5, the Domino
    template has been fixed. Use the Domino Template Enablement
   tool to enable the fixed Domino template.
