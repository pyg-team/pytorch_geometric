Title: IBM Cipher suites 04, 05, 0A, 2F, and 35 are not available (SCI55212) - United States

Text:
STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Cipher suites 04, 05, 0A, 2F, and 35 are not available (SCI55212) 

SYMPTOM
Cipher suites 04, 05, 0A, 2F, and 35 are not available when defining node in Secure+ Parmfile


CAUSE
IBM Cryptographic Services Security Level 3 (FMID JCPTnnn) not installed on 
mainframe.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Install IBM Cryptographic Services Security Level 3 (FMID JCPTnnn)
This is required if you wish to use cipher suites - 
1) SSL V3.0 Triple DES SHA 
2) SSL V3.0 RC4 SHA US 
3) SSL V3.0 RC4 MD5 US 
4) SSL_RSA_AES_128_SHA 
5) SSL_RSA_AES_256_SHA
