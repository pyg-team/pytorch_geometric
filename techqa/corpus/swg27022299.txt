Title: IBM Recommended Fixes for IBM Business Process Manager V7.5.x products - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 This page provides links to the recommended maintenance packages that are available for IBM Business Process Manager suite of products. 

CONTENT
IBM Business Process Manager products collectively refers to IBM Business Process Manager Advanced, IBM Business Process Manager Advanced: Process Server, IBM Business Process Manager Standard, and IBM Process Business Manager Express. 



Software maintenance updates for the IBM Business Process Manager products are supplied in the form of bundled fix packages, which are called fix packs or refresh packs. These updates are summarized in the following table or tables, which are organized by release. 


When you apply a fix pack or refresh pack to an installation of the IBM Business Process Manager products, the version of that installation changes to reflect the update. For example, if you apply V7.5.0 Fix Pack 1 to an installation of IBM Business Process Manager Advanced V7.5, the new version of that installation is V7.5.0.1.
