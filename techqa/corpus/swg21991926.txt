Title: IBM Issue with number of recipients link in notice sent page - United States

Text:
atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 After upgrade to Atlas 6.0.3.3 with WebSphere version 7.0.0.39 , when I click on number of recipients link in notice sent page of the hold notice , why the following error displayed ?



"The Page cannot be found" 

CAUSE
Due to changes in expression language (EL) functions causing this issue.

ANSWER
 

 * Upgrade WebSphere version from 7.0.0.39 to WebSphere version 8.5.5 on Atlas server. 
 * Contact IBM support representative to get the APAR HE12569 fix which is part of Atlas 6.0.3.3_IF1_LA002.
