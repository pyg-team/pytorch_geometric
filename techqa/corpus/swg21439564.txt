PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The LSW_BPD_INSTANCE contains a EXECUTION_STATUS field. This article explains the meaning of the values in this field. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
You need to query the database to get the status of your business process definition (BPD) instances. What is the meaning of the EXECUTION_STATUS field in the LSW_BPD_INSTANCE table? 

Flag Meaning 1 The BPD instance is active. 2 The BPD instance has completed. 3 The BPD instance has failed. Look at the ERROR and ERROR_STACK_TRACE for further details. 4 The BPD instance has terminated. 5 The BPD instance did not start because its message event condition returned false. 6 The BPD instance is suspended, 
Note: Querying the internal system tables is not supported. This information is for documentation purposes. The values are subject to change at any time without notice.
