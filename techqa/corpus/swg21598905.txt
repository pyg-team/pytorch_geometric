Title: IBM Unable to log onto FileNet Content Engine. - United States

Text:
login to FEM hang; FNRCC0110E: CONTENT_FCP_OPERATION_FAILED TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Unable to connect to FileNet Content Engine via FileNet Application Engine Workplace or FileNet Enterprise Manager. It comes up partially and hangs. Sometimes it will hang while navigating the object store. 

SYMPTOM
 

 * Object Store connects to Image Services via FileNet Content Federation Services for Image Service (CFS-IS). 
 * Issues reported with other clients as well connecting to IS such as FileNet IDM Open Client. 
   


CAUSE
Image Services in bad state.
