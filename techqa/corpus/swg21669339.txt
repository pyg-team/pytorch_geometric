Title: IBM How to solve the AFU error message in IBM Content Collector (ICC) v4.0? - United States

Text:
-AFU-ERROR-CONDITIONS; CSSS fix pack 4.0.0.2 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 I get the following exception in ICC indexing log after I installed ICC 4.0 environment on Windows platform:

[Exception=For input string: "-AFU-ERROR-CONDITIONS" [MSGHandler: convertMSGtoEmailItem()]

How to solve it?




ANSWER
To solve the problem, you need to install Content Search Service Support (CSSS) fix pack 4.0.0.2 based on ICC v4.0 GA version.
