Title: IBM Error code=8096 when working in Rational Business Developer connected to Rational Team Concert - United States

Text:
RBD; RTC; crashs TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Attempts to use IBM Rational Business Developer connected to an IBM Rational Team Concert server, result in error "Java was started but ... ". 

SYMPTOM
You connected a Rational Team Concert server to Rational Business Developer and you perform actions on the server. Then the Eclipse workbench, java.exe, stops with the following error message. 

Java was started but returned exit code=8096 


[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21502684&aid=1] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21502684&aid=1]

Examples of actions you can do are: creating a work item and creating a user.

CAUSE
One known cause is due to the video driver and when two different versions of drivers are installed on the machine (i.e 6.14.10.5284 and 6.14.10.5303) 


Note: Other causes are also possible but have not been reported.


ENVIRONMENT
This problem has been seen with ThinkPad laptops



DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
Look at the java core. The following entries support the hypothesis of the faulty video drivers. 

 

 1. The last call before the problem relates to the Microsoft gdiplus.dll graphics library. 
    
 2. The correct values passes correctly to the Standard Widget Toolkit (SWT) before the error occurs.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Update the video driver to the newest version 

Link: Intel Download Center [http://downloadcenter.intel.com/]
