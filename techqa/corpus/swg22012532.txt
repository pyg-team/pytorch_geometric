Title: IBM Security Bulletin: IBM Security SiteProtector Appliance has released firmware 1. 26 (for SP3001) and firmware 2.13 (for SP4001) in response to the vulnerabilities known as Spectre and Meltdown. - United States

Text:
PSIRT SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 IBM has released the following firmware 1.26 for SiteProtector SP3001 appliance and firmware 2.13 for SiteProtector SP4001 appliance in response to CVE-2017-5753, CVE-2017-5715 and CVE-2017-5754. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID: CVE-2017-5753 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2017-5753] 

CVEID: CVE-2017-5715 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2017-5715] 

CVEID: CVE-2017-5754 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2017-5754]
