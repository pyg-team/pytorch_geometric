LOCAL FIX
 *  Stop opm completely, including web ui. As OPM instance owner
   connect to perfdb and issue the following statement:
   db2 update db2pm.mt_table set mt_tableelementid=4 where
   mt_table_name='EV_LOCK' and mt_type='T' and mt_category='CONF'
   Start opm and configure a database once more.
