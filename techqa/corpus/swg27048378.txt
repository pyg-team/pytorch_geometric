 RELEASE NOTES

ABSTRACT
 IBM StoredIQ 7.6.0.8 is a fix-pack release that focuses on enhancements for the existing Box connector as well as the addition of harvest audits in the Administrator interface. Improvements to the Data Workbench include a new infoset dashboard. Stunnel was implemented to provide secure encryptions on the appstack, gateway and dataserver. Read these notes before installing or using IBM StoredIQ 7.6.0.8.
