Title: IBM Issues occur when cleaning up local documents from IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) if you use the BPMProcessInstanceCleanup command - United States

Text:
BPMProcessInstanceCleanup; clean up; cleanup; delete; commands; IBM Business Process Manager; BPM; Business Process Manager; process instance; process FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 When you use the BPMProcessInstanceCleanup command to delete process instances, the local documents are not automatically deleted. You must manually delete these documents. 

CONTENT
To delete the local documents, follow the instructions in IBM BPM Content Integration [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/blogs/44906fab-d10e-49a2-a83a-3a19e421cbf5/entry/Purging_local_documents?lang=en].
