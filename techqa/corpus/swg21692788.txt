Title: IBM How do I find out the exact InfoSphere BigInsights version I am running? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I find out the exact InfoSphere BigInsights version I am running? 

ANSWER

For finding out the exact InfoSphere BigInsights version, run 'biginsighslevel' script as biadmin user.

[biadmin@myhost ~]$ $BIGINSIGHTS_HOME/bin/biginsightslevel
IBM InfoSphere BigInsights Enterprise Edition v3.0.0.0, build Linux-amd64_8_b20140711_1547, type Enterprise
