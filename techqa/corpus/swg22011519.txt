Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Information Disclosure Vulnerability in IBM WebSphere Portal (CVE-2017-1698) - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 A fix is available for an information disclosure vulnerability in IBM WebSphere Portal (CVE-2017-1698). 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID: CVE-2017-1698 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2017-1698]
DESCRIPTION: IBM WebSphere Portal could reveal sensitive information from an error message that could lead to further attacks against the system.
CVSS Base Score: 5.3
CVSS Temporal Score: See https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/134390 [https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/134390] for the current score
CVSS Environmental Score*: Undefined
CVSS Vector: (CVSS:3.0/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:L/I:N/A:N) 

AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
 

Affected Product Affected Versions IBM WebSphere Portal 9.0.0.0 - 9.0.0.0 CF15 IBM WebSphere Portal 8.5.0.0 - 8.5.0.0 CF15 IBM WebSphere Portal 8.0.0.0 - 8.0.0.1 CF22 IBM WebSphere Portal 7.0.0.0 - 7.0.0.2 CF30 
For unsupported versions IBM recommends upgrading to a fixed, supported version of the product.
