Title: IBM Setting session timeout interval to "No timeout" prevents login to SKLM GUI - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Setting session timeout for SKLM to "No timeout" so that login sessions never expires and restarting WebSphere kicks you out of the SKLM GUI. 

SYMPTOM
User gets kicked out the SKLM GUI and gets the following message on the login screen : 

"Your session has become invalid. This is due to session timeout, an administrator has logged you out, or another user has invalidated your session by logging on with same user ID." [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSWPVP_2.5.0.3/com.ibm.sklm.doc_2.5.0.3/install_guide/tsk/tsk_insguide_tklm_postinstall_setkeepalive.html?lang=en]


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
This will get fixed in coming FixPack, i.e. SKLM v2.5 FP 7 and SKLM v2.6 FP 1
