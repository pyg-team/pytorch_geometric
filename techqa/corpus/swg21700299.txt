Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Vulnerability in the Dojo Toolkit affects IBM Business Process Manager, Image Construction and Composition Tool shipped with IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator Enterprise (CVE-2014-8917) - United States

Text:
Security; smartcloud orchestrator; dojo; CVE-2014-8917 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 A vulnerability in the Dojo Toolkit affects IBM Business Process Manager and IBM Image Construction and Composition Tool, shipped with IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator Enterprise (CVE-2014-8917). 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
Consult the following security bulletins for vulnerability details and information about fixes for affected supporting products that are shipped with IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator and SmartCloud Orchestrator Enterprise.
Security Bulletin: Cross-Site Scripting vulnerabilities in Dojo affect IBM Business Process Manager (BPM), WebSphere Lombardi Edition (WLE), and WebSphere Process Server (WPS) - CVE-2014-8917 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21883360],

Security Bulletin: Vulnerability in dojox/form/resources/*.swf and dojox/av/resources/*.swf XSS vulnerability affects IBM Image Construction and Composition Tool shipped with IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator (CVE-2014-8917) [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21970662].

AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
 

Principal Product and Version Affected Supporting Product and Version IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator V2.2, V2.2.0.1 IBM WebSphere Application Server Network Deployment 8.0 IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator V2.3, V2.3.0.1 up to Interim Fix 6 IBM Business Process Manager Standard V8.5.0.1
IBM Image Construction and Composition Tool 2.3.0.1 REMEDIATION/FIXES
If you are running IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator V2.3 or V2.3.0.1 up to Interim Fix 6 and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator Enterprise V2.3 or V2.3.0.1 up to Interim Fix 6 , upgrade [http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/selectFixes?parent=ibm%7ETivoli&product=ibm/Tivoli/IBM+SmartCloud+Orchestrator&release=2.4.0&platform=All&function=all] to IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator 2.3.0 Fix Pack 1, Interim Fix 7 [http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/quickorder?parent=ibm%7ETivoli&product=ibm/Tivoli/IBM+SmartCloud+Orchestrator&release=2.3.0.1&platform=All&function=fixId&fixids=2.3.0.1-CSI-ISCO-IF0007&includeRequisites=1&includeSupersedes=0&downloadMethod=ddp&source=fc]. 

Follow Instructions in the README.


For unsupported IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator 2.2, 2.2.0.1 and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator 2.2, 2.2.0.1 Enterprise IBM recommends upgrading to a fixed, supported release of the product.
