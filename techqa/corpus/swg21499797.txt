Title: IBM New Reporting Option, V7RI for Maximo 6.2.8 - United States

Text:
v7ri; report; maximo 6; birt; ibmeam; TPAEREP TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What is the V7RI Reporting Option, and how is it installed and configured? 

ANSWER
In Maximo® Asset Management 6.2.8 Release, a new report integration option is available. This integration enables a new reporting option targeted at streamlining future report upgrades, and enabling you to use the embedded reporting tool of the Maximo 7 releases. 
This guide will present you with the details to understand and implement this integration including - 

Understanding the V7RI Report Option
Configuring the Version 6 and Version 7 Systems
Creating V7 Custom Reports for your V6 Instance
Supported Platforms and Functionality
Reference Materials

In the ‘Understanding the V7RI Report Option’ section, an overview of the integration will be given. This includes top level architecture diagrams and screen shots.

In the second section of ‘Configuring the Version 6 and 7 Systems’ the specific steps required to configure your 6 and 7 instances for this will be included. These steps will include the modification of property system files, registering the new reports in your Version 6 instance and creating new report users. It also includes steps for testing and troubleshooting the integration.

The third section will highlight steps to get your started creating V7 BIRT reports, and key features to consider for your custom report needs. 

In the next section on ‘Supported Platforms and Functionality’ a review of the system and report specific functionality enabled in this integration will be detailed. And then in the final section on ‘Reference Materials’, key support documents will be highlighted to assist you in your custom report development. 

For more details on the V7RI integration, reference this url [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/wikis/home?lang=en#!/wiki/IBM%20Maximo%20Asset%20Management/page/Maximo%206%20Reporting]. Additionally, you can find more details on other reporting features by accessing the Maximo Report Wiki Pages available here [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/wikis/home?lang=en#/wiki/IBM%20Maximo%20Asset%20Management/page/Reporting] [http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/wikis/display/maximo/Reports]or the Maximo Report Reference Materials located here [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/wikis/home?lang=en#!/wiki/IBM%20Maximo%20Asset%20Management/page/Report%20Resources]
