Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Vulnerabilities in Node.js affect IBM i - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 Node.js is used by IBM i. IBM i has addressed the applicable CVEs. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID: CVE-2015-8027 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2015-8027]
DESCRIPTION: An unspecified vulnerability in Node.js could allow a remote attacker to cause a denial of service.
CVSS Base Score: 7.5
CVSS Temporal Score: See https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/108360 [https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/108360] for the current score
CVSS Environmental Score*: Undefined
CVSS Vector: (CVSS:3.0/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:N/I:N/A:H)

AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
Releases 7.1 and 7.2 of IBM i are affected. 

REMEDIATION/FIXES
The issue can be fixed by applying a PTF to the IBM i Operating System.
Releases 7.1 and 7.2 of IBM i are supported and will be fixed.
