Title: IBM Jazz™ for Service Management is not affected by the Bash vulnerabilities (CVE-2014-6271, CVE-2014-7169, CVE-2014-7186, CVE-2014-7187, CVE-2014-6277, and CVE-2014-6278) - United States

Text:
jazzsm11relnotes; jazzsm1101relnotes; jazzsm1102relnotes; jazzsm1103relnotes; jazzsm1110relnotes FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 Jazz™ for Service Management is not vulnerable to the Bash vulnerabilities that have been referred to as “Bash Bug” or “Shellshock” and the two memory corruption vulnerabilities. 

CONTENT
Jazz™ for Service Management in all editions and all platforms is NOT vulnerable to the Bash vulnerabilities (CVE-2014-6271, CVE-2014-7169, CVE-2014-7186, CVE-2014-7187, CVE-2014-6277, and CVE-2014-6278).
IBM recommends that you review your entire environment to identify vulnerable releases of Bash including your Operating Systems and take appropriate mitigation and remediation actions. Please contact your Operating System provider for more information.
