Title: IBM Special characters, such as ‘, –  etc. when copied from MS Word are not supported in Atlas search, . - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When you copy a matter name containing special characters from MS Word and paste it in the Search Matters field in Atlas, it does not display the desired results. 

SYMPTOM
In the Matter Name field, type “Department of Property's audit”. 

 1.  In the subsequent fields, enter the required information and click Save and Close. A new matter is created. 
 2. Type the same matter name, “Department of Property's audit” in MS word. The special character (’) is depicted differently. 
 3. Copy this matter name from MS Word and paste it in Search Matters field in Atlas. The desired search result is not displayed.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
 

To resolve this problem, you must either type the matter name in the Search Matters field or copy it from text editors, such as Notepad or Notepad++.
