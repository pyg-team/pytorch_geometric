QUESTION
 What are the supported browsers for WebSphere DataPower SOA Appliances? 

ANSWER
The list of supported browsers is specific to the DataPower firmware version and is documented in the Release Notes for each version. Use of an unsupported browser may result in missing configuration tabs or other unpredictable WebGUI behaviors. 

See Supported firmware versions and recommended upgrade levels for IBM WebSphere DataPower SOA Appliances. [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21237631]


 * For firmware versions v3.8.0 and later, click on Release Notes next to your DataPower firmware version on this page. On the next page, click on the link for your DataPower appliance model to access the Release Notes for your appliance. 
 * For firmware versions prior to v3.8.0, click on Release Notes next to your DataPower firmware version on this page. On the next page, scroll past the fix pack information to find the attached PDF document containing the Release Notes for your appliance.
