Title: IBM Error Message while creating a Atlas Hold Notice - United States

Text:
Atlas Roles Insufficient privileges TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why do I get a Insufficient privileges error while creating a Atlas Hold Notice? 

CAUSE
This is caused by a product defect

ANSWER
 

When creating a Hold Notice or adding a courtesy copy recipient to a Notice, you sometimes get a server side error: " You have insufficient privileges to a MatterNotice" 

This is caused by APAR HE12584. Please contact IBM Atlas support for assistance.
