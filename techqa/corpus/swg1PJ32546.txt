   The comments containing special german characters like &#65412;, &#65428;
   and others. These special characters in the comments are not
   transferred correctly. Looks like RDA has an issue reading
   these comments during reverse engineering.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Remove German special characters like &#65412;, &#65428; from comment.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  RDA is unable to reverse engineer within Design Studio if the
   comment of the table column contains German special characters
   like &#132;, &#148;.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION

TEMPORARY FIX
 *  This is fixed with InfoSphere Warehouse v9.5.2.
