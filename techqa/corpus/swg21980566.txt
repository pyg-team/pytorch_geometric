Title: IBM How to change the doc and page type in the Batch View for Verify task from IBM Datacap Navigator? - United States

Text:
navigator; batch view; verify TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How to change the doc and page type in the Batch View for Verify task from Datacap Navigator? 

ANSWER
Do the following:
1. Disable "Read-only view" from the Verify task configuration.
2. Change the "Props for var-Type" from 0 to 1.
