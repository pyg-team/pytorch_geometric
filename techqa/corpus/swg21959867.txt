Title: IBM PureData for Analytics - How to clear crm_mon resource errors - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How to clear crm_mon resource errors 

ANSWER
You can use the command to clear a failed resource action in crm_mon. 
crm_resource -r <resource name> -H <host name> -C 

Example:
Failed actions:
drbd_exphome_device_start_0 (node=netezza_h2, call=15, rc=1): Error

[root@netezza]# crm_resource -r drbd_exphome_device -H netezza_h2 -C
