Title: IBM Right click event Show Root Cause fails - United States

Text:
root; cause; rootcause; suppress; script; administrator; cgi-bin; launch TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

THIS DOCUMENT APPLIES ONLY TO THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE VERSION(S):
 English 

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The following error is received: Failed to execute CGI "/cgi-bin/launch-rootcause-event.cgi". Contact 
your administrator. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Modify launch cgi in $NCHOME/omnibus_webgui/etc/cgi-bin 

(Windows NCHOME\omnibus_webgui\etc\cgi-bin) 

 

change the top line of the 
launch-rootcause-event.cgi or launch-suppressed-events.cgi

#!/usr/bin/perl

to use your ITNM perl

For example

#! /opt/IBM/tivoli/netcool/precision/perl/bin/perl

(Windows perhaps something like)

#!D:\opt\IBM\tivoli\netcool\precision\perl\bin\perl

TIP may have to be restarted.
