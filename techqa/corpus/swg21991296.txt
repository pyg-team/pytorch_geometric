Title: IBM Response to Hold Notice with Virtual Interview is coming up as Replied, No Confirmation - United States

Text:
Hold Interview quesionnaire TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why do we see response to Hold Notice with Virtual Interview as Replied, No Confirmation? 

CAUSE
The interview questionnaire was changed recently and Interviews created recently all have the issue

ANSWER
 

 1. Login to Atlas 
 2. Click Admin > Interview Questionnaire 
 3. Click on the recently created questionnaire 
 4. Click Edit 
 5. Verify if there is more than one Confirmation question 
 6. Make changes as required 
 7. Save and Close
