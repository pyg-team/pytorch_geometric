Description


The BPM Healthcare Pack includes the following assets:


 * Healthcare Capability Models: The Healthcare Capability Models are also based on HL7-Electronic Health Record (EHR) Standard and IBM best practices.

 * Healthcare Process Models: The Healthcare Process Models are based on IBM best practices.

 * Healthcare Service Models: The Healthcare Service Models are based on the ASC X12 Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) messages for HIPAA and IBM best practices.

 * Healthcare Common Components: The Healthcare Common Components include Healthcare common services that are Healthcare-specific common web service implementations based on the Healthcare industry standards. The common services are web service implementations that use the HL7 and ASC X12 EDI models for HIPAA standards.

 * Healthcare Business Vocabulary: The Healthcare Business Vocabulary is based on healthcare standards such as HL7 Vocabulary, HL7 RIM, HL7-VOC, ASC X12 EDI models, and IBM best practices.

 * Healthcare Business Object Models: The Healthcare Business Object Models is based on the HL7 Reference Information Model (RIM).

 * Healthcare Solution Scenario: The Healthcare Solution Scenario is a BPM implementation packaged as a reference to help understand the end-to-end delivery of a BPM solution for the healthcare industry. In this pack, the Benefits Eligibility Solution Scenario has been included.

Globalization limitations


The globalization limitations identified for this release of the Business Process Manager Healthcare Pack are as follows: 

 * The Business Space widgets, provided with the Solution Scenarios in Business Space, are available only in English.

 * The terms and their descriptions in the Business Vocabulary are available only in English.

 * The online help for Asset Navigator is available only in English, Brazilian Portuguese, French, German, Japanese, and Spanish languages.

The accessibility limitations identified for this release of the Business Process Manager Healthcare Pack are as follows:
