Title: IBM Power 8: DSPFMWSTS Errors with Message CPF3580 - No Server Firmware Status to Display - United States

Text:
CPF4580 firmware ivm standalone stand alone TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 DSPFMWSTS errors with message CPF3580 - No server firmware status to display. 

SYMPTOM
DSPFMWSTS errors with message CPF3580: 

Message ID . . . . . . . . . : CPF3580 
Message file . . . . . . . . : QCPFMSG 
Library . . . . . . . . . : QSYS
Message . . . . : No server firmware status to display.
Cause . . . . . : Your hardware model does not support server firmware or there is no firmware data available to display.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
You should apply the following PTFs from APAR MA44465:

o R710 MF59969
o R720 MF59956 

If unable to apply the PTF right away, the following work-around to resolve the CPF3580 message is available as follows: 

1. Issue the STRSST command and sign in. 2. Select Option 1. Start a service tool. 3. Select Option 4. Display/Alter/Dump. 4. Select Option 1. Display/Alater Storage. 5. Select Option 2. Licensed Internal Code (LIC) data. 6. Select Option 14. Advanced analysis. 7. Enter the following data and press Enter:

1LIDFW 8. Enter the following data:

Options: rebuild all 
Verify the issue has been resolved by issuing the DSPFMWSTS command.
