Title: IBM Project tab appears blank when you create a new project in WebSphere Cast Iron Studio - United States

Text:
CI 7.0; Cast Iron; Cast Iron 7.0; Cast Iron Cloud Integration TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When you create multiple new projects in Cast Iron Studio, the Project tab appears blank occassionally. 

CAUSE
This behavior is observed when you perform the following steps : 

1.Create a new project in WebSphere Cast Iron Studio and do not save.
2.Create another new project, provide a project name and click OK.



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Close and reopen the project. The Project tab is displayed.
