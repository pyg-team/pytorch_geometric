Atlas Preservation Matter TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can we change custodian status from Release to On Hold in a preservation plan? 

CAUSE
The plan owner set the custodian status to Release

ANSWER
No. This change cannot be performed through the Atlas Application. You can contact IBM Atlas support for assistance
