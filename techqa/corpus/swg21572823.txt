Title: IBM Tivoli Integrated portal login page customization - United States

TIPL2CONF; customize Tivoli Integrated portal login page TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can I customize login page in Tivoli Integrated portal? 

ANSWER
Question: Can I add text on the login Page in TIP?
Answer: No, you can not add any text on the login page.

Question: Can I add hyperlinks on the login page in TIP?
Answer: No, you can not add any hyperlink on the login page.
