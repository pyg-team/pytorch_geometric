PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 You are attempting to startup CICS when the following errors occur after DFHAP1203I Language Environment/370 is being initialized:
DFHSO0001 An abend (code 0C4/AKEA) has occurred at offset X 'FFFF' in module DFHSOIS.
DFHDM0105 Unsuccessful initialization of DFHSO domain. CICS will terminate.
DFHKE1800 ABNORMAL TERMINATION OF CICS IS COMPLETE. 

SYMPTOM
DFHSO0001 [http://pic.dhe.ibm.com/infocenter/cicsts/v5r1/topic/com.ibm.cics.ts.messages.doc/DFHSO/DFHSO0001.html] An abend (code 0C4/AKEA [http://pic.dhe.ibm.com/infocenter/cicsts/v5r1/topic/com.ibm.cics.ts.messages.doc/AKxx_abend_codes/AKEA.html]) has occurred at offset X 'FFFF' in module DFHSOIS.
DFHDM0105 [http://pic.dhe.ibm.com/infocenter/cicsts/v5r1/topic/com.ibm.cics.ts.messages.doc/DFHDM/DFHDM0105.html] Unsuccessful initialization of DFHSO domain. CICS will terminate. 


CAUSE
Abend in Language Environment (LE) module due to a backlevel LE library, SCEERUN. The failing instruction is a MVO instruction in LE module CEEEV003.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Ensure that your CICS region is referencing the correct LE libraries. You must use the libraries that came with the operating system you are using.
