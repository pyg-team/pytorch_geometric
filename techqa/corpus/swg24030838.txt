ILOG Rule Solutions Office DOWNLOADABLE FILES

ABSTRACT
 This document describes how to download WebSphere ILOG Rule Solutions for Office V7.1.1.4 eAssembly images from IBM Passport Advantage . 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
 

 WebSphere ILOG Rule Solutions for Office is an add-in for Microsoft Office 2007 and 2010. It enables business users to edit rules in documents known as RuleDocs. 

 RuleDocs are Microsoft Office documents that can present rules along with documentation, supporting information, and even the original policies. 

Contents

 This document provides a list of the eAssembly images for Rule Solutions for Office V7.1.1.1 and V7.1.1.4, and describes how to download the images from IBM Passport Advantage . 

More information

 For more information on this product, visit the WebSphere ILOG Rule Solutions for Office home page [http://www.ibm.com/software/integration/business-rule-management/rule-office/support/index.html]. 

Downloading and assembling the product

 1. With your browser, navigate to the Find Downloads and Media [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/paocustomer/sdma/SDMA?PO=DOWNLOAD_FIND_BY_PART_NUMBER] page on IBM Passport Advantage Online.
 2. Sign in using your IBM ID.
 3. Select the Find by part number search option under Download finder options.
 4. Search for downloadable images by part number (refer to list below).
 5. Download all the parts you require.
 6. Unpack each part into a separate directory on your computer.
 7. Follow the instructions in the Quick Start Guide (CZM1QML) to install the product.
