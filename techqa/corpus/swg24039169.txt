 DOWNLOADABLE FILES

ABSTRACT
 This Interim Fix will upgrade your IBM Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.3 Fix Pack 1 release. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
This Interim fix is an update of IBM Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.3 Fix Pack 1 for existing IBM Cognos Disclosure Management customers. This Interim Fix (server patch) includes a fix for APAR PI32246 - Incorrect data shown in locked objects after upgrade to Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.3 FP1 listed in this Customer Alert:
http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21694135 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21694135]

PREREQUISITES
This applies to IBM Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.3 Fix Pack 1 available from Fix Central. The 10.2.3 Fix Pack 1 version of the product must already be installed. Please note that the Interim Fix can be applied to either the IBM Cognos Disclosure Management or the IBM Cognos Disclosure Management Data Aggregation installation.

INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
For environments that have UAC (User Access Control) enabled, please run Update.exe (Server) using the 'Run as Administrator' option. 


Please do not install this Interim Fix over IBM Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.3 Fix Pack 1 Interim Fix 1. A separately available Interim Fix will be provided for that installation.
