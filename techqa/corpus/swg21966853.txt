Text:

QUESTION
 Where can I find the IBM TRIRIGA language packs for my install? 

CAUSE
Need to import language packs and don't know where I can find them. 

ANSWER
The IBM TRIRIGA Language packs can be found at the installation directory <IBM TRIRIGA installation directory>\userfiles\LanguagePacks.
