QUESTION
 Can the contents of a Web Page or URL via a Monitor in IBM Enterprise Content Management System Monitor (ECM SM) be displayed? 

ANSWER
No, currently, the ECM SM only verifies that a Web Page is available. It does not display the contents of theWeb Page. This may be a future enhancement
