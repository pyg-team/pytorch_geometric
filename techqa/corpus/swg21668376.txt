Title: IBM The Process Center console for IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) is not displayed in IBM Process Designer after an update - United States

Text:
white screen blank screen PD upgrade TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After you apply an update to IBM Business Process Manager and IBM Process Designer, in rare cases, there can be caching issues. These caching issues can prevent the Process Center console from displaying in IBM Process Designer or IBM Integration Designer. 

SYMPTOM
You can access the Process Center console from a browser. However, some IBM Process Designer or IBM Integration Designer users cannot load the initial Process Center console view.

The following error is seen in the SystemOut.log file for the Process Center when the issue occurs:

CAUSE
Old information exists in the browser cache for the operating system, which interferes with IBM Process Designer or IBM Integration Designer when it tries to display the Process Center console

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
You must clear the browser cache to resolve the issue. Complete the following steps: 

 1. Clear the browser cache for the browsers that you use. For Microsoft Internet Explorer, select Safety > Delete Browsing History. 
    
    
 2. Ensure that all your browser windows are closed and reopen IBM Process Designer or IBM Integration Designer. Re-attempt the login to open the Process Center console view.
    
    
 3. Click F5 to force a refresh of the browser window if it continues to come up with a blank screen.
    
    

The issue does not occur for upgrades to Version 8.5.5 or later.
