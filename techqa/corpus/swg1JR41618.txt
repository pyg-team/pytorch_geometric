Title: IBM JR41618: SERVER SLOWDOWN AND HUNG THREADS DUE TO BUSINESS DATA SEARCH CACHE ISSUE - United States

APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The server startup is slowed down and hung threads appear in the
   logs due to a large amount of searchable business data.
   This problem is more frequent as more snapshots are deployed.
   The initial load of the BusinessDataAliasCache loops over
   branches, versions, projects, and snapshots to load all BPDs to
   extract information to store in the BusinessDataAliascache.
