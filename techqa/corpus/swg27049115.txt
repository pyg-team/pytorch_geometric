ABSTRACT
 The IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) and IBM BigFix Inventory (BFI) technical support Level 2 and Level 3 teams shared information on how the products work in relation to IBM. The panel of subject matter experts answered related questions from the attendees. 

CONTENT
For this Open Mic session, Hope Maxwell-Daley explained, and Artur Zezula demonstrated:


 1. How IBM i differs from other operating systems 
 2. What you must know in order to maintain LMT/BFI and IBM i
