PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  DataPower customers configuring the Web Service Proxy processing
   rules using something other than a WSDL Component Type of
   Fragment Identifier.
   
   The Web Service Proxy is not correctly restoring processing
   rules configured with a WSDL Component Type of something other
   than Fragment Identifier to the policy tree.
   
   Fix will be available in next major releases.
