QUESTION
 SCOM 2007 probe : Where is the probe documentation? 

CAUSE
 

The SCOM 2007 Probe was replaced with the SCOM 2007 R2 probe 
ANSWER



The SCOM 2007 probe for UNIX has been withdrawn, as has the Windows version. The Windows version was replaced with the SCOM 2007 R2 probe. The two version's of the SCOM 2007 probes are configured differently. 


It is recommend that the SCOM 2007 probe is upgraded to the SCOM 2007 R2 probe.
Please review the probes documentation and plan the upgrade.
