Title: IBM Is Microsoft LifeCam NX-6000 supported with Sametime Meeting Room client? - United States

Text:
Sametime MRC client; meeting room; camera; life cam; lifecam TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
Is Microsoft LifeCam NX-6000 a supported camera with the Sametime Meeting Room client? When attending an on-line video meeting with this camera, you find that the video is blacked out and you might get error code 12033.

ANSWER
No, the NX-6000 is not a supported camera with the Sametime Meeting Room client. However, the Microsoft LifeCam VX-3000 will work. Refer to the system requirements for a list of supported cameras.
