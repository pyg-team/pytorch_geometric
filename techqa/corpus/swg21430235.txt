ICDC; TS; Transformation Server; EOS; End of service TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Where can i find end of service dates for CDC? 

ANSWER
Go to:
http://www.ibm.com/software/support/lifecycle/index_i.html#ibm-content [http://www.ibm.com/software/support/lifecycle/index_i.html#ibm-content] and scroll down to Infosphere Change Data Capture. You will find the current list of supported 
versions and their end of service date. If the EOS date is blank it has not been announced.
