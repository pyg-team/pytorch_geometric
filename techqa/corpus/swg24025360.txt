ABSTRACT
 IBM WebSphere Healthcare Content Pack version 7.0 is a prebuilt accelerator for the WebSphere Business Process Management (BPM) offering. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
This document provides the eAssembly part number " CR9K4ML" required for download of the IBM WebSphere Healthcare Content Pack version 7.0 product from the Passport Advantage Web site [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm]. 

The assets provided in the WebSphere Healthcare Content Pack and their description is provided here.

Healthcare Capability Models: The Healthcare Capability Models are based on Health Level 7 (HL7)-Electronic Health Record (EHR) and IBM best practices. These include business capability and process maps. The Healthcare Capability Models are used to provide a view of business capabilities and business processes used in the healthcare industry.

Healthcare Process Models: The Healthcare Process Models are based on IBM best practices. The Healthcare Process Models are the foundation for implementation of an Healthcare specific BPM solution, enabling it to be reused across multiple solutions.

Healthcare Service Models: The Healthcare Service Models are based on the ASC X12 Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) messages for HIPAA and IBM best practices. They are prebuilt service interfaces and schemas that facilitate interoperability across diverse platforms for healthcare industry. These industry standards provide the messaging schemas that can be used to define the Industry Service Interfaces.

Healthcare Common Components: The Healthcare Common Components are based on the ASC X12 EDI messages for Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the HL7 standards, and IBM best practices. They include common services that can be used as-is or can be extended to enable transactional functions.

Healthcare Business Vocabulary: The Healthcare Business Vocabulary is based on healthcare standards such as HL7-Reference Information Model (RIM), HL7-VOC, ASC X12 EDI models, and the IBM best practices. It is used to provide more information about the relationships between industry-specific terms. The Healthcare Business Vocabulary is a taxonomy of the business terms for the healthcare industry.

Healthcare Business Object Model: The Healthcare Business Object Model is based on HL7-RIM. The Healthcare Business Object Model is a logical representation of business entities and attributes that set up a business domain or a subvertical within a domain.

Healthcare Solution Scenarios: The Healthcare Solution Scenarios are packaged BPM implementation as a reference to understand end-to-end delivery of a BPM solution for the healthcare industry. The Healthcare Solution Scenarios use most of the assets from the WebSphere Healthcare Content Pack, which have either been used or extended as per the use case requirement.
