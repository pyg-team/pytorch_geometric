ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The users have to click two times on the Hold notice link on
   the Matters list page to display the Hold Notice.
   Scenario1: You have  a Hold Notice1  on matter1. You have to
   click Hold Notice1 twice on the Matters list page to display
   the Hold Notice1.
   
   Scenario2: You have a Hold Notice1, and Hold Notice2. To
   display the Hold Notice2, You have to either click Hold Notice2
   twice or Click Hold Notice1 first and then click Hold Notice2
   on the Matters List page.
   
   This issue happens only on Internet Explorer8 or later such as
   IE10.
