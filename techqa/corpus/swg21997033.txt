Title: IBM Dataserver services changing from Healthy to Vulnerable - United States

Text:
SIQ TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does the Dataserver service change from healthy to Vulnerable after logging into appstack? 

CAUSE
Memory allocated was not the supported number

ANSWER
 

This could be due to amount of Memory allocated to the dataserver. 

 1. Login to the Dataserver 
 2. Click on Administration > Dashboard tab 
 3. Review the Application status and make sure it is green 
 4. Click on Controller Link 
 5. Review and confirm that all services are running fine 
 6. If needed, make changes to memory
