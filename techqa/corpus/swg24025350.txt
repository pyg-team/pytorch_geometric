Downloading the fix pack 

When you click the download link below, a sign-in page opens. Enter your IBM ID and password to proceed. If you have forgotten your IBM ID or have never registered, follow the instructions on the sign-in page. 

After signing in: 
1. Select IBM InfoSphere Content Collector V2.1.1 Fix Pack 1. 
2. Accept the license agreement to proceed. 
3. Download the readme file and the fix pack deliverables that are required for your environment.
