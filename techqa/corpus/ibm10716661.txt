ABSTRACT
The Development team explained what is included in V6.0.0.20 and V7.0.1.10 of IBM Security Identity Manager (ISIM) released in July, 2018.

CONTENT
IBM Security Identity Manager (ISIM) developers covered the new enhancements and serviceability items, as well as common customer pain points for which solutions are being provided in the new releases of 6.0.0.20 and 7.0.1.10.
