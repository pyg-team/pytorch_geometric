LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  DC configuration (config.sh) fails due to use of double-quote
   characters inside the genericJvmArguments.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The fix will include support for double-quote characters inside
   genericJVMArguments.

   release:
