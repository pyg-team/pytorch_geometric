Title: IBM A servlet NullPointerException occurs when calling a web service in IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After you upgrade to IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) V7.5.1 Fix Pack 1, you cannot run any web service or any WSDL in IBM Business Process Manager. Instead, you see a NullPointerException message in a browser. 

SYMPTOM

CAUSE
During an upgrade to IBM Business Process Manager V7.5.1 Fix Pack 1, there are critical profile upgrade steps. When these steps are not completed, classes are not initialized.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
To resolve this issue, complete all of the steps in the Profile upgrade instructions for V7.5.1 Fix Pack 1 (V7.5.1.1) IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) product installations with existing profiles [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27035490] document.
