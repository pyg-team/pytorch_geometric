Title: IBM CVE-2017-5638 affect on Sterling Connect:Direct - United States

Text:
security; Apache Struts; CDw CDu C:D TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 A critical vulnerability in Apache Struts (CVE-2017-5638) has been identified as being actively exploited.

Are IBM Sterling Connect:Direct for Microsoft Windows and IBM Sterling Connect:Direct for Unix affected by this vulnerability?


CAUSE
Apache Struts vulnerability [https://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2017-5638] identified. 

ANSWER
Sterling Connect:Direct for Microsoft Windows and UNIX are unaffected by any vulnerabilities in Apache Struts, because we do not employ that technology in those products.
