ABSTRACT
 This WebSphere Support Technical Exchange covers configuring security in SOAP nodes (SOAPInput, SOAPReply and SOAPRequest nodes). It also discusses SSL configuration of SOAP nodes and WS-security capability & configuration in WebSphere Message Broker (WMB) V6.1. Please note that even though the content was written based on WMB V6.1, the content is still relevant to WMB V7.0, WMB V8.0 and IBM Integration Bus (IIB) V9.0.


Level of Difficulty: Intermediate
