PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The initial release of 5.2.0.5-P8CPE-IF002 contained unsigned PE
   client jars.
   The 5.2.0.5-P8CPE-IF002 was rebuilt so that the jars were
   properly signed and this new 5.2.0.5-P8CPE-IF002 was uploaded to
   Fix Central on March 27, 2017.
