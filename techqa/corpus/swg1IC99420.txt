ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Web console does not open any dashboards, shows blank page
   Flash modules in browser cache from prior OPM versions are not
   upward compatible
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  (A) fully clear the browser cache ("Everything" not just the
       last few days)
   (B) force a cache refresh / clean reload:
       - Press and hold Shift and left-click the Reload button
       - Press "Ctrl + F5" or press "Ctrl + Shift + R" (Windows,
         Linux)
       - Press "Command + Shift + R" (MAC)
