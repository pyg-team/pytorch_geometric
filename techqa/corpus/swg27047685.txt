Title: IBM Open Mic Webcast: BFI Custom Signature and Policy and Process - Tuesday, 29 March 2016 [Includes link to replay; presentation is attached] - United States

Text:
Endpoint-open-mic; ISS_OPEN_MIC; Software Usage Analysis WEBCAST

ABSTRACT
 Sherrie Clarke and fellow members of the Support teams shared information on IBM BigFix Inventory Catalog Signature Coverage Policy and Process. They explain what the process expectation is for adding a custom signature to the official catalog and what your other options are. 

CONTENT
This Open Mic session was the third 2016 webcast provided by IBM Security License Metric Tool and BigFix Inventory Technical Support. Our goal is to provide insight on how BigFix Inventory (formerly Software Use Analysis) works and to teach on-going sessions that help both users and administrators understand, maintain, troubleshoot, and resolve issues with their product.
