ABSTRACT
 This document provides a comprehensive list of recommended and generally available (GA) fixes for IBM WebSphere Extended Deployment, along with a list of previously delivered updates. 

Tables are organized by version in the order they were released. These lists include Fix Packs, Cumulative Fixes, and Interim Fixes.
