ABSTRACT
 Members of the IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) support team shared information on rectifying incorrect Processor Value Unit (PVU) core values. 

CONTENT
For this open mic session, experts from Technical Support discussed PVU core values in detail. The goal is to to enable users of IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) v9.x to check on the incorrect PVU core values before sending the issue to the Support team for assistance and resolution.




This was a very interactive, educational, lively session. 


 * Topic: What to check when I have incorrect PVU core values 
 * Date: Tuesday, 9 May 2017 
 * Time: 11:00 AM EDT (15:00 UTC/GMT, UTC-4 hours) for 60 minutes
