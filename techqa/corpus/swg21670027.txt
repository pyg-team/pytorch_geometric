Title: IBM SmartCloud for Social Business Services Not Vulnerable to OpenSSL Heartbleed Bug (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
Are SmartCloud for Social Business services affected by the Open SSL Heartbleed bug (CVE-2014-0160)?

ANSWER
The Heartbleed bug (CVE-2014-0160) is a worldwide security vulnerability affecting services with SSL/TLS encryption using the OpenSSL library. We have reviewed all the systems hosting each of the SmartCloud for Social Business services; and would like to assure you of the following: 

 * All SmartCloud for Social business systems have been tested and verified that they do not exhibit the vulnerability. 
 * Some of our applications use acceleration provided by Akamai. For their statement of support, refer to the Akamai Heartbleed FAQ [https://blogs.akamai.com/2014/04/heartbleed-faq-akamai-systems-patched.html].
