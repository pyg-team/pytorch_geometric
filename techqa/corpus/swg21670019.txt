Title: IBM Informix Servers are not affected by the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 IBM Informix Server products (Online, Informix Dynamic Server, XPS, and SE) are not vulnerable to the CVE-2014-0160 OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability. 

CONTENT
CVE-2014-0160 - Heartbleed Vulnerability 
IBM Informix Server versions in all editions and all platforms are NOT vulnerable to the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160) because they do not use an affected version of OpenSSL.

Remediation: No action required.

Change History: 11 April 2014, original document published.
