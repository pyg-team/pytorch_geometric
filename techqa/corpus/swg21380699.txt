Title: IBM Supported Citrix environments for Rational Performance Tester - United States

Text:
RPT; server; client; citrix; environment TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What are the Citrix server and client environments supported by IBM Rational Performance Tester (RPT) Extension for Citrix ? 

ANSWER
Rational Performance Tester supports Citrix clients going from version 9 to 11 and Citrix server versions: 4, 4.5 and 5.
