 * IBM Business Process Manager Advanced

 * IBM Business Process Manager Standard 
 * IBM Business Process Manager Express


After you download the package, see the Installing instructions. 
Note: This page includes only the downloads for IBM Business Process Manager V8.6. For other downloads that might be needed for the upgrade, see Downloading the repositories to the local system [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27050084#download] on the Installing page. 
If you have Internet access on the system where you are installing the cumulative fix, you do not need to manually download the download packages. IBM Installation Manager will find the cumulative fix in the live repository. 

A separate download is not available for the desktop IBM Process Designer. You can update the desktop Process Designer from the Process Center after installing the upgrade.
