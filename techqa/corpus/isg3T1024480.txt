Title: IBM Would 2016 leap second impact Symphony? - United States

Text:
leap second. TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Would 2016 leap second cause any problem? 

CAUSE
A leap second is added in 2016 at the end of the year.

ANSWER
For Symphony and Conductor with Spark, there's no obvious impact. There might be minor impact to the display or the duration time of some jobs that are started and completed within the leap second. However, no impact to execution of such jobs.
