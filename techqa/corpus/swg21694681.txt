Title: IBM NoSuchMethodError missing setUserProperties() method in JCC driver - United States

Text:
java jdbc jcc NoSuchMethodError setUserProperties TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Upgrading to JCC driver 3.65.110 may result in application failing with NoSuchMethod error for setUserProperties(). 

SYMPTOM
java.lang.NoSuchMethodError: com/ibm/db2/jcc/DB2SimpleDataSource.setUserProperties(Ljava/util/Properties;)V";


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Upgrade to latest 10.1 Fix Pack driver.
