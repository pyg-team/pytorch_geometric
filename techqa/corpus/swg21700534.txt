Title: IBM Impact of the addition of Leap Second time on WebGUI - United States

Text:
adjust; timezone; tz TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What is the impact of the addition of Leap Second on WebGUI? 

CAUSE
Every few years, a one-second adjustment is added to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) in order to keep its time of day close to the mean solar time.

ANSWER
The addition of Leap Second has no impact to WebGUI functionality. Therefore, preventive maintenance is not necessary.
