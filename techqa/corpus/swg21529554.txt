STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Using the offline utilities how do you select batches with certain status flags? (SCI21821) 

ANSWER
Use the -F parameter to control the selection criteria of status flags for batches.
Example:
cmulist -i mailboxa -FA!T
This will list all batches in MailboxID mailboxa with a status flags A but not status flags T.
