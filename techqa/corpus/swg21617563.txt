ssl; pegasus; twainpro TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I resolve TwainPRO License Validation Check errors when scanning in TMWeb Scan via SSL? 

CAUSE
During Taskmaster Web scanning with a scanner under SSL, the following error message is displayed: 

The Pegasus Imaging Corp. license validation check has failed for the TwainPRO control. You do not have an appropriate license to use this ActiveX control.


ANSWER
1. First, make sure you have enabled the browser setting "do not save encrypted pages to disk" in Tools | Internet Options | Advanced and then retest.
