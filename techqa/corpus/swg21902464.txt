Title: IBM All batch reports fail without error message - United States

Text:
notification TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After migrating from another IBM Cognos instance, all batch reports (running in the background or scheduled reports) will fail instantly without any error message in run history detail. 

CAUSE
During the migration, a copy of the earlier IBM Cognos notification store database was restored and configured as the notification store database for new IBM Cognos environment.

DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
All batch reports will fail.
Checking the cogserver.log, there will be many occurrence of CNC-BAL-0503 The Server has failed.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Create and configure a blank database as notification store database.
