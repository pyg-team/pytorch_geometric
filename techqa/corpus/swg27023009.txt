ABSTRACT
 This document provides access to the detailed system requirements information on the supported releases of the IBM Business Process Manager Tools and Add-Ons. 

CONTENT
The detailed system requirements information is available through the Software Product Compatibility Reports [https://www.ibm.com/software/reports/compatibility/clarity/softwareReqsForProduct.html] web site. This web site provides you with the ability to dynamically generate operating system, prerequisite, server virtualization environment, translation, end of service, and detailed system requirements reports for your specific product, release, and operating system. 


The following table provides direct links to the detailed system requirements information for the specified release and operating system for the IBM Business Process Manager Tools and Add-Ons products.

Microsoft Office and Sharepoint Add-Ons


Note: The BPM Microsoft Office and Sharepoint add-ons have been withdrawn [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21700493] and are no longer available through standard support. While existing customers may continue to use their Microsoft Office and Sharepoint add-on licenses, support would only be available through an extended support agreement. The BPM Microsoft Office and Sharepoint add-ons will work with the following IBM BPM products... 

 *  IBM Business Process Manager Advanced V7.5, V7.5.1, V8.0, V8.5.0 Fix Pack 1 (8.5.0.1), 8.5.5, and 8.5.6
 * IBM Business Process Manager Standard V7.5, V7.5.1, V8.0, V8.5.0 Fix Pack 1 (8.5.0.1), 8.5.5, and 8.5.6
 * IBM Business Process Manager Express V7.5, V7.5.1, V8.0, V8.5.0 Fix Pack 1 (8.5.0.1), 8.5.5, and 8.5.6



Note: Your entitlement to support, if any, is dependent upon your license and/or maintenance agreements for IBM Business Process Manager Tools and Add-Ons products and is limited to your use of the relevant prerequisite with a supported IBM Business Process Manager Tools and Add-Ons product.
