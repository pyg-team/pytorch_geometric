Title: IBM Acknowledged events show text in black - United States

Text:
ack events; black font; acknowledged event text TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When alerts are acknowledged in the Active Event List, the text for the alarm is not displayed in a white font, unlike when viewed through a native Event List. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Verify that 

ael_user_properties_acknowledge_font_color:white 

./netcool/omnibus_webgui/etc/system/userdefaults.props 

is set.
