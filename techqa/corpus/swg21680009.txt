QUESTION
 How to create Question for Survey? 

ANSWER
Procedure to create Question for Survey
1 Click Administration -> Survey Management -> Questions
2 Click New Question
3 Fill following fields 
a) Question (required)
b) Type (required) There are three types CHECKBOX, FREEFORM, or RADIO
An answer is also required if you select CHECKBOX or RADIO
c) Site 
d) Organization
4 Click Save Question
5 Click Change Status, and make it Active
