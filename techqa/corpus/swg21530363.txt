STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 An FTP client gets "No Batches For Transmission" when there are batches in the mailbox. (SCI61446) 

SYMPTOM
An FTP client gets "No Batches For Transmission" when there are batches in the mailbox.


CAUSE
The 2 most common reasons are: Batches are flagged with a "Y" or the batches do 
not have an "R" flag).

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
If the batches are flagged with a "Y" then have the client receive them in binary mode. If batches do not have an "R" flag, use cmustatus to add the "R" flag. If the batches have a "T" or "D" flag, investigate the cause. Use cmustatus to turn off the "T" or "D" flag, but it is recommended research be done first.
