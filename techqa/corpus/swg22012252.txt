Title: IBM In Master Data Management (MDM) server, Setting up TLS1.2 doesn't seem to work - United States

Text:
SSL security TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 In MDM, after following the steps to setup TLS1.2 in the below link it didn't seem to work. 
https://developer.ibm.com/answers/questions/206952/how-do-i-configure-websphere-application-server-ss.html [https://developer.ibm.com/answers/questions/206952/how-do-i-configure-websphere-application-server-ss.html] 
How to configure TLS1.2 for MDM to work with all services ,MQ, etc. ? 

CAUSE
TLS1.2 configuration is not setup correctly 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In addition to the steps in the above link, do the following: 

- Enabled TLSv1.2 at client side.
- Corrected the entries for com.ibm.ssl.protocol=TLSv1.2 under ssl.client.props.
