Title: IBM RS00197: THE MODEL BOM ORIGIN IS INCORRECT FOR THE XMLBINDING SAMPLE - United States

APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  In the xmlbinding sample, when updating bom entry model, one
   will see message "This BOM entry cannot be updated because its
   origin is not recognized. You can redefine it in your project
   properties."
   
   The default origin is "xom:/xmlbinding-xom/data/financial.xsd",
   once changing it to
   "xom:/xmlbinding-rules//xmlbinding-xom/data/financial.xsd", the
   bom can be updated.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Manually change the bom origin to
   xom:/xmlbinding-rules//xmlbinding-xom/data/financial.xsd
