Title: IBM The IBM Datacap V9.0 documentation states incorrect information about the LookupEx variable support - United States

Text:
Datacap Desktop; LookupEx; support; IBM Datacap V9.0 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The information about LookupEx variable states that the Lookup variable is supported by the Datacap Desktop. However, this information is incorrect.

The following link on the IBM Datacap V9.0 documentation states this incorrect information:
http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSZRWV_9.0.0/com.ibm.dc.develop.doc/dcadg318.htm [http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSZRWV_9.0.0/com.ibm.dc.develop.doc/dcadg318.htm] 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The correct information must state that the LookupEx variable is not supported by the Datacap Desktop in the IBM Datacap V9.0.
