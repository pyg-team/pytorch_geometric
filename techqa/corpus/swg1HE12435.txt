APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When in a Preservation Plan or Collection Plan, browsing away
   from most tabs without first saving will open a prompt
   reminding the user to save.  However, this prompt does not
   appear for the Attachments tab.  Instead, users are able to
   browse away from the tab without first Saving, causing loss of
   any attachments recently added.  Customer is adding 3-5
   attachments, and finding that they are not part of the plan.
