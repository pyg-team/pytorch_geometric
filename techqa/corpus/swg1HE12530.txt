ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  "Remove" and "Edit Reason" buttons are not disabled when no
   recipients are selected in Hold Notice Recipient page.
   Steps to reproduce the problem:
   1. Log in to Atlas.
   2. Click on Matters. Select a matter.
   3. Click on Holds > New > Hold Notice.
   4. In the Create a New Notice pop-up window, input the required
   fields and select Create.
   5. In the next screen, go to Rules tab.
   6. Click on Recipients.
   7. In the list of recipients, select the custodians which you
   want to remove.
   8. Click on "Recipients" Recipients list will get displayed
   9. Select the custodians which you want to remove and click
   on Remove from Request. Select either options to maintain hold
   or release hold.
   10. After the selected custodians are removed, the Edit Reason
   and Remove buttons should be disabled. But they are still
   enabled.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Select another user and unselect it. Then the Remove and Edit
   Reason buttons become disabled.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  "REMOVE" AND "EDIT REASON" BUTTONS ARE NOT DISABLED WHEN NO
       RECIPIENTS ARE SELECTED IN HOLD NOTICE RECIPIENT PAGE.
