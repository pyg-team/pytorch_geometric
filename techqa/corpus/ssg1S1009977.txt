Title: IBM Storwize V7000 Unified V1.5 is going out of support in September 2017. - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 IBM Storwize V7000 Unified V1.5 is going out of support in September 2017. 

CONTENT
Recommendation
IBM Storwize V7000 Unified V1.5 end of support announcements were made last year. Please refer to announcement letter links provided below for various regions.
You are encouraged to plan the upgrade of Storwize V7000 Unified systems to V1.6.x well before the earlier version goes out of support.
