Title: IBM Troubleshooting problems with viewers in IBM Content Navigator - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 If you have a problem with a viewer or viewing feature, you might have a problem with your IBM Content Navigator configuration or your IBM Content Navigator web application server configuration. 

CONTENT
Most problems can be addressed by reviewing the attached troubleshooting document to determine the cause of the problem and how to resolve the problem. 

However, if you have a problem with a Daeja ViewONE viewer in an SSO environment, you might need to set additional configuration parameters. See the following article for more information: Daeja ViewONE viewers can fail in HA and SSO environments [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg22005170].
