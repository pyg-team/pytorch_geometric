From the IBM Systems Director command line enter smcli lsver to determine the level of IBM Systems Director installed.

Affected Product and Version(s) Remediation Instructions IBM System Director Storage Control 4.2.6 Technote 829848035 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=nas777ef7055eff65a21862581aa00672455] in the Support Portal [https://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/support/] IBM System Director Storage Control 4.2.7 Technote 829848035 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=nas777ef7055eff65a21862581aa00672455] in the Support Portal [https://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/support/] IBM System Director Storage Control 4.2.8 Technote 829848035 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=nas777ef7055eff65a21862581aa00672455] in the Support Portal [https://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/support/]
