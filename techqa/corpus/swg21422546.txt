Title: IBM ClassCastException when using New Group Scorecard - United States

Text:
com.ibm.ws.cache.servlet.CacheProxyRequest; org.apache.jetspeed.portlet.PortletRequest; scorecard; new group scorecard TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM
ClassCastException is encountered when using the New Group Scorecard button.

SYMPTOM
java.lang.ClassCastException: com.ibm.ws.cache.servlet.CacheProxyRequest
incompatible with org.apache.jetspeed.portlet.PortletRequest


CAUSE
This is caused by enabling servlet caching.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Disable servlet caching. As per the screenshot below, this can be disabled by using the administration console and navigating to "Application servers > WebSphere Portal > Web container" and unchecking the "Enable servlet caching" box.
