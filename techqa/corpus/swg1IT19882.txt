ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When using the Secure Gateway Client in DataPower, with an
   on-premise destination configured as TLS:ServerSide, the SSL
   handshake may fail when attempting to connect to the
   destination. The DataPower log may show an error similar to the
   following:

   UNABLE_TO_VERIFY_LEAF_SIGNATURE
