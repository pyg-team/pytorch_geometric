Title: IBM Automatically re-synch ITMS Engine TIME After Daylight Saving's Time - United States

Text:
auto synch time dst TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can the ITMS/Engine TIME re-synch after Daylight Saving Time (DST) change without any intervention? 

CAUSE
Current design of the ITMS/Engine requires issuing the TIME RESET command to re-synchronize the clock. 

ANSWER
APAR OA46343 has been opened to re-synchronize the ITMS/Engine's clock automatically. This affects any agent or Tivoli Management Server (TMS) executing KLV PGM=KLV). The PTFs are as follows:
