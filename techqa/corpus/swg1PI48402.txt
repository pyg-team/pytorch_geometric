APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Whenever the labels in the Layers dimension of the pivot table
   is longer than the table is wide, the label is truncated.
   Sometimes it is truncated in the Viewer, but most often it is
   truncated in the Print Preview and Exported file.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  This issue will be addressed in the next Fix Pack. Please
   refer to the Fix Pack schedule at
   http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21599157 for
   information on upcoming Fix Packs.
