Title: IBM Db2 is not affected by Apache Struts vulnerabilities. - United States

Text:
 NEWS

ABSTRACT
 IBM Db2 is not vulnerable to Apache Struts vulnerabilities. 

CONTENT
IBM Db2 in all editions and all platforms is NOT vulnerable to any Apache Struts vulnerability. 

Apache Struts v1 exists in the sqllib/samples/java/Websphere/OptimisticLocking.war archive. It it is not used in any way or form by IBM Db2. It is safe to remove the Websphere directory and its contents. The Apache Struts libraries will be removed in a future fix pack.
