Title: IBM JR54269: WHEN YOU USE INTERNET EXPLORER TO DISPLAY SAVED SEARCH RESULTS THE SCROLL BAR IS SOMETIMES INCORRECTLY POSITIONED AT THE TOP - United States

Text:
  A FIX IS AVAILABLE
Version 8.5 Refresh Pack 7 for the IBM Business Process Manager products [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24041575]


SUBSCRIBE
You can track all active APARs for this component.



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  In Process Portal, when you display tasks in a saved search,
   scroll down the list, and then run a task or click on the Work
   tab, the next time you click the saved search tab you notice
   that the scroll bar is at the top and the rows that you had
   previously scrolled down to view are displayed. You know that
   there are rows that are hidden at the top. You scroll down and
   back up and the rows reappear.
   
   PRODUCTS AFFECTED
   IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) Advanced
   IBM BPM Standard
   IBM BPM Express
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  In Internet Explorer, the scroll bar does not automatically
   position itself based on the location of the rows that are in
   focus.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  A fix that that correctly positions the scroll bar in Internet
   Explorer will be included in an IBM BPM V8.5.6.0 cumulative fix.
   
   
    
   
   

TEMPORARY FIX
 *  Not applicable
