Title: IBM Solaris 10 containers are supported by Netcool®/Proviso® 4.4.3.3 - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 Can we use Solaris 10 container for Netcool®/Proviso® 4.4.3.3? 

CONTENT
Solaris 10 container are supported by Netcool®/Proviso® 4.4.3.3 

Solaris 10 for SPARC Platforms 

For information on upgrading from Solaris 9 to Solaris 10, go to the following Sun Microsystems site: 

http://docs.sun.com/app/docs/doc/817-5505 [http://docs.sun.com/app/docs/doc/817-5505] 

This site contains documentation on Solaris installation and upgrades. 

Operating Systems and Kernel 

Solaris 10 

Solaris kernel version SunOS enterprise 5.10, Generic_120011-14 or higher, from Solaris 10 08/07. 

To determine the patch level on your system, run the following command: 

uname -v 

Note: All Netcool/Proviso modules are tested to run on an End User distribution of Solaris 10.

Solaris 10 Virtualized Containers 

If using Solaris 10 virtualized containers, you must create containers with "whole root" partitions. Proviso will not 

work in containers with "sparse root" partitions.

Technology Packs 

Installation of technology packs on Solaris 10 requires IBM Java 1.5 R6 JRE 1.5 R6 or higher. The correct version 

is installed with the Topology Editor in the following default location: 

/opt/IBM/proviso/jvm/jre/bin 

DataView & Novell exteNd 

Novell exteNd 4.0 and DataView on Solaris 10 do not require any additional patches. 

Note: DataView real-time reports require JRE 1.5.0 or higher plug-in for the user’s browser.
