Title: IBM Identify the version of BIRT that is running in Atlas - United States

Text:
Atlas BIRT Reports TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do you identify the version of BIRT that is running in Atlas? 

CAUSE
Sometimes the reports do not run properly if you do not have the latest version of BIRT bundled with Atlas

ANSWER
 

Launch the URL 
http://<host>:<port>/AtlasReports 

You will find the viewer and engine version of BIRT listed on the page 

The versions are 4.2.1 or 2.5.1
