ABSTRACT
 Ram Reddy, of the IBM Security Directory Support team, discuss upgrading from SDS 6.4 to SDS 8.0.1 VA to use existing remote DB2 database. After the presentation, attendees were given an opportunity to ask the panel of experts questions. 

CONTENT
On March 28th, the Directory Support team hosted an ISDS Open Mic webcast to discuss upgrading from SDS 6.4 to SDS 8.01 VA to use existing remote DB2 database.

Throughout the event, attendees were encouraged to comment or ask questions in the Q&A Panel within the WebEx Event Center. We hoped you had the opportunity to join us for this interactive, educational, lively session.

 * Date: Tuesday, 28 March 2017
