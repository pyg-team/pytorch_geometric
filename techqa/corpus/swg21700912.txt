Title: IBM Network Intrusion Prevention (GX) leap second support - United States

Text:
gx; ips; leap; second; fixpack; patch; ntp TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Will your GX-Series IPS account for the leap second that was introduced on June 30, 2015? 

ANSWER
The following fixpack applies the appropriate update to the tzdata package for the 4.6.2 firmware: 4.6.2.0-ISS-ProvG-AllModels-System-FP0007 [http://www-933.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/selectFixes?parent=Security%2BSystems&product=ibm/Tivoli/Proventia+Network+Intrusion+Prevention+System&release=4.6.2&platform=All&function=all].

Note: Users of NTP will not be affected as any time adjustments are made automatically.
