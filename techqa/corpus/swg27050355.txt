ABSTRACT
 IBM Security Guardium Support held this Open Mic on October 26, 2017 discussing the security and supportability of your Guardium environment. 

CONTENT
This Open Mic asks the question "Is Your Guardium Environment Secure & Supportable?"


The discussion covers:


 * Shared Secrets 
 * ID's and Passwords 
 * Ports and Firewalls 
 * Backups and Archives, and 
 * Supportability Options


Learn how to keep your Guardium environment safer and ready for support!
