ABSTRACT
 This download is V8.0.1 Fix Pack 1 for the IBM Business Process Manager products. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
In this document, IBM Business Process Manager Advanced, IBM Business Process Manager Standard, and IBM Business Process Manager Express are collectively referred to as the IBM Business Process Manager products.


IBM Business Process Manager is a comprehensive business process management platform that gives you visibility and insight to manage business processes. It scales smoothly and easily from an initial project to a full enterprise-wide program. IBM Business Process Manager harnesses complexity in a simple environment to break down silos and better meet your needs. 


V8.0.1 Fix Pack 1 (V8.0.1.1) is the first fix pack for the IBM Business Process Manager Version 8.0.1 products, which first shipped in November 2012 on the following operating systems:


 * AIX 
 * Linux 
 * Solaris 
 * Windows 
 * z/OS



This fix pack addresses product defects found both by customers and thru internal testing since the IBM Business Process Manager products V8.0.1 were first shipped. Overall, more than 300 fixes were included and tested, which leads to improved product stability and quality. 


A complete list of the APARs that are addressed with this fix pack is available in the following document: Fix list for IBM Business Process Manager Version 8.0 products [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27036594]. Product components with the most significant improvements thru these fixes include the BPMN Process Engine, Process Portal, Coaches, as well as Bindings and APIs. 


The links in the download section link to the download packages for V8.0.1 Fix Pack 1 (V8.0.1.1) for the following products on all applicable operating systems:  * IBM Business Process Manager Standard 
 * IBM Business Process Manager Advanced 
 * IBM Business Process Manager Advanced - Process Server 
 * IBM Business Process Manager Express 
 * IBM Business Process Manager Advanced for z/OS
   Note: This download is available through the "IBM BPM Advanced Process Server for z/OS 8.0.1.1" download package.



A separate download is not available for IBM Process Designer. You can update IBM Process Designer from the Process Center after installing the appropriate fix pack. PREREQUISITES
You can install this fix pack on top of IBM Business Process Manager Version 8.0.1 products only.
