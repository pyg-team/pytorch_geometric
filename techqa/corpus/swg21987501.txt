QUESTION

ANSWER
1)run "db2cluster -cm -list -HostFailureDetectionTime" to check HostFailureDetectionTime configuration value. 

2)To reset HostFailureDetectionTime configuration value.
root# /opt/IBM/db2/V10.5/bin > ./db2cluster -cm -set -option HostFailureDetectionTime -value 4
