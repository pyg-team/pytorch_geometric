Title: IBM Maximo full support for IE11 in 7.5.0.8 and 7.6 - United States

Text:
TPAEUI; internet; explorer; 11; 11.0; ie; browser; support; compatibility; compatible; supported; mode; full; jaws TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Maximo support for Internet Explorer 11. 

ANSWER
Full support for Internet Explorer 11 will be made available within Maximo Asset Management fixpack 7.5.0.8. Full support is also available in Maximo Asset Management 7.6. 

IE11 support will not be backported to any previous fixpack level of 7.5.0.x 

Full IE11 support does not include JAWS compatibility. 

Full IE11 support does not require Internet Explorer compatibility mode.
