Title: IBM Cognos Incentive Compensation Management - Configuration - SSO and web enablement of users - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 To have access to ICM Web, do all users have to be web-enabled even when web access is determined by SSO ? 

ANSWER
Yes, each user needing access to ICM must be web enabled irrespective of whether SSO is being used to determine web access or not.
