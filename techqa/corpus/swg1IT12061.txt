APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Under load a SSL proxy  service may intermittently hang,
   leading to a timeout of the transaction.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Affected are DataPower configurations using SSL Proxy service.
   
   The service may stop transferring data when a burst of high
   traffic of several kB or more passes through it.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The fix is available in 6.0.0.18, 6.0.1.14, 7.0.0.11, 7.1.0.8
   and 7.2.0.3
   
   For a list of the latest fix packs available, please see:
   http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21237631 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21237631]
