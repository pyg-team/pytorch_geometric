Title: IBM QRadar: SSH connections to QRadar using PuTTY may fail with a fatal error after upgrading to 7.2mr3 - United States

Text:
qradar; ssh; upgrade; putty TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 You may find that you receive a fatal error when attempting a SSH connection to QRadar using PuTTY after upgrading to QRadar 7.2mr3. 

SYMPTOM
After the upgrade, the following PuTTY exception may be received:

PuTTY Fatal Error:
Couldn't agree a client-to-server cipher (available: aes128-ctr,aes192-ctr,aes-256-ctr,arcfour256,arcfour128,arcfour)


CAUSE
An older version of PuTTY may be in use which does not support the cipher used by this version of QRadar. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Upgrading to PuTTY version .60 or later will address the issue.
