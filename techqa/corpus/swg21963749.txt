scp transfer rate faster arcfour cipher TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How to make the transfer rate of secure copy (scp) faster 

ANSWER
 

You can select the cipher to use for encrypting the data transfer using secure copy (scp).

The cipher, arcfour, appears to be the option that delivers the fastest transfer rate.


examples:

$ scp -c arcfour 200M_testfile nz@au1:/nzscratch
nz@au1's password: 
200M_testfile 100% 200MB 11.9MB/s 00:16

$ scp -c aes128-ctr 200M_testfile nz@au1:/nzscratch
nz@au1's password: 
200M_testfile 100% 200MB 8.7MB/s 00:23

$ scp -c arcfour256 200M_testfile nz@au1:/nzscratch
nz@au1's password: 
200M_testfile 100% 200MB 10.5MB/s 00:19

$ scp -c arcfour128 200M_testfile nz@au1:/nzscratch
nz@au1's password: 
200M_testfile 100% 200MB 11.8MB/s 00:17 

$ scp -c blowfish-cbc 200M_testfile nz@au1:/nzscratch
nz@au1's password: 
200M_testfile 100% 200MB 11.1MB/s 00:18
