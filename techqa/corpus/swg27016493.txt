ABSTRACT
 This WebSphere Support Technical Exchange Open Mic session discusses and addresses queries from customers on different security aspects in WebSphere Message Broker (WMB). 

Please note that when this session was recorded WMB V6.1 was the current version but most of the concepts discussed here are still applicable to WMB V7 and V8 and IBM Integration Bus (IIB) V9.
