 DETAILED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

ABSTRACT
 This document summarizes the hardware, platforms, and software supported by the IBM® InfoSphere® Optim™ Test Data Management Solution for z/OS® for PeopleSoft Enterprise v11.3. 

CONTENT


Version 11.3 of the InfoSphere Optim Test Data Management Solution for z/OS for PeopleSoft Enterprise is tested and distributed with version 7.2 of the IBM InfoSphere Optim Test Data Management Solution for DB2® for z/OS. Detailed hardware and software requirements for the InfoSphere Optim Test Data Management Solution for DB2 for z/OS are at the following site: http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27036953 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27036953] 

SUPPORTED PEOPLESOFT ENTERPRISE SOFTWARE
Version 11.3 of the InfoSphere Optim Test Data Management Solution for z/OS for PeopleSoft Enterprise provides support for the following PeopleSoft suites and modules: 

FINANCIAL AND SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT (FSCM) (VERSION 9.1)
 * Accounts Payable
 * Asset Management
 * Billing
 * Commitment Control
 * Contracts
 * Expenses
 * General Ledger
 * Inventory
 * Order Management
 * Project Costing
 * Purchasing

HUMAN CAPITAL MANAGEMENT (HCM) (VERSION 9.1)
 * Base Benefits
 * Benefits Administration
 * Global Payroll
 * North American Payroll
 * Organizational Development
 * Recruiting
 * Time and Labor
 * Workforce Administration
 * Workforce Development
 * Workforce Monitoring
