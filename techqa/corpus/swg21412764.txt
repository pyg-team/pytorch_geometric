Title: IBM StackOverflow exception when launching WebSphere Business Space - United States

Text:
v70rnotes; WID TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 You may receive a StackOverflow exception when launching WebSphere Business Space in the Eclipse internal browser. 

SYMPTOM
