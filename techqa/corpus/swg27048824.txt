ABSTRACT
 Ram Reddy of the IBM Security Directory Support team discussed configuring the SDS 8.0.1 Virtual Appliance with a remote DB2 database. After the presentation, attendees were given an opportunity to ask the panel of experts questions.

On January 17th, the Directory Support team hosted an ISDS Open Mic webcast and discussed configuring SDS 8.0.1 Virtual Appliance with a remote DB2 database.

Throughout the event, attendees were encouraged to comment or ask questions in the Q&A Panel within the WebEx Event Center.
