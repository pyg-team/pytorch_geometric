Title: IBM CDM Is Creating An Empty Scenario Tag For C36 - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The XBRL instance generated by CDM 10.2.4 with corresponding Filing package EIOPA Final V3 is rejected by the regulator when is is submitted, with the next error:

"The content of element 'xbrli:scenario' is not complete. One of '{WC[##other:"http://www.xbrl.org/2003/instance [http://www.xbrl.org/2003/instance]"]}' is expected." 

SYMPTOM
The XBRL instance generated by CDM is rejected.


CAUSE
This issue was caused by a self closing tag <xbrli:scenario /> wrongly generated.

ENVIRONMENT
Cognos Disclosure Management 10.2.4

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
This was resolved in CDM 10.2.4 IF16, with package EIOPA Final v6 .
