Title: IBM User receiving Reminder Notice Sender Execution Failed Alert after upgrading to Atlas 6.0.3.3 - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does the user receive Reminder Notice Sender Execution Failed Alert after upgrading to Atlas 6.0.3.3? 

CAUSE
The error message is "The timer task failed due to error : java.sql.SQLException: ORA-01652: unable to extend temp segment by 128 in tablespace TEMP1"


ANSWER
This is caused due to insufficient space in the temp tablespace. The DBA needs to allocate sufficient space for the temp tablespace
