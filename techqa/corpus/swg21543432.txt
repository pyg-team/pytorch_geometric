STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can I get a report of statistical information? 

ANSWER
Use the Activity Reporting System (ARS) and create SMF Records or write your own program. The SAMPLE (SDGASAMP in V5.1 and later) library contains examples to write Stat Exits. Reference the Connect:Direct for z/OS Facilities Guide for additional information on ARS.
