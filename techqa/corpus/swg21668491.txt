ANSWER
The WebSphere MQ Resource Adapter can be downloaded from Fix Central [https://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/]. Just search on "WS-MQ-Java-InstallRA" in the [https://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/]search box, or click on the following link:



 * Download the WebSphere MQ Resource Adapter [http://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/search_results?sn=spe&filter=keywords:ibmsupportfixcentralsearch&q=WS-MQ-Java-InstallRA]



Or search for the WebSphere MQ Resource Adapter manually:



 1. Go to Fix Central [https://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/].
    
    
 2. In the Product Selector field enter "WebSphere MQ" and select it from the list.
    
    
 3. In the "Installed Version" field select "7.5".
    
    
 4. In the "Platform" field select your platform or just choose "All", then press Continue.
    
    
 5. On the next page select the "Browse for Fixes" button and make sure that the "Show fixes that apply to this version" button is also selected, then press Continue.
    
    
 6. Select the WS-MQ-Java-InstallRA version you want and download it, for example:
