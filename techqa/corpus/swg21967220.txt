SHA1 SHA2 algorithms WPG Express TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Does WPG Express 6.0 support SHA-2 algorithms? 

CAUSE
Customer needing to know for AS2 encryption.

ANSWER
No, WPG Express 6.0 does not support the SHA-2 algorithm. It only supports the SHA1 algorithm.
