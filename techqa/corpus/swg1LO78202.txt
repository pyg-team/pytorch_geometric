ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  *** Description ***
   "Remove Meeting invitations from my Inbox after I respond to
   them." are enabled on the option of mail database.
   New mail notification is also enabled.
   
   In this environment, when invitation accepts automatically,
   invitation is removed from Inbox.
   Therefore, even though we get new mail notification, we can not
   see the invitation on inbox.
   
   The request is not to notify new mail when "Remove Meeting
   invitations from my Inbox after I respond to them." is enabled.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  disable new mail notification or "Remove Meeting invitations
   from my Inbox after I respond to them."
