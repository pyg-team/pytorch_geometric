ABSTRACT
 The IBM® InfoSphere® Optim™ Data Privacy Enterprise Edition, IBM InfoSphere Optim Data Privacy Work Group Edition, IBM InfoSphere Optim Data Privacy for Hadoop, and IBM InfoSphere Optim Data Privacy for Data Warehousing consist of a number of components. The system requirements for the components are compiled in this document.
