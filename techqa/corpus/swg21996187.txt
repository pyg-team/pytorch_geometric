 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I upgrade my Datacap application to Datacap V9.1.0? 

ANSWER
To upgrade your Datacap application to V9.1.0, complete the following steps:
Before Upgrade:
----------------- 

 1. Ensure to take full backups of the Admin, Engine, and Fingerprint database tables. 
 2. Ensure that the Admin, Engine and Fingerprint databases are transferred to SQL Server, Oracle and DB2. 
 3. Run the installer to install IBM Datacap V9.1.0. You do not need to uninstall IBM Datacap 9.0.1 prior to upgrade. 
 4. Migrate your Datacap V9.0.1 applications to V9.1.0. Database schemas may need to be updated.

After Upgrade: 
-----------------  1. Validate that the upgrade to V9.1.0 is successful with no errors displayed to user. 
 2. Validate that the Install.log contains no errors or warnings. 
 3. For each application migrated to V9.1.0, validate that batch records are intact and have identical details compared with V9.0.1 FP2 using the following clients:  * Datacap Desktop 
     * Datacap Studio 
     * Fastdoc 
     * TMWeb 
     * RV2
