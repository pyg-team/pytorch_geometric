QUESTION
 Is IIDR/CDC (LUW) handling Leap Second event correctly? Is there any action required? 

CAUSE
Leap Second event.

ANSWER
Leap seconds are not especially rare events. 

There have been several occurrences of such since the first release of the CDC technology in the 90's. 
IIDR/CDC handles leap seconds automatically for LUW environments. 
No special user configuration or action is required.
