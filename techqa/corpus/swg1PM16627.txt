LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  When remote actions are used during stress testing a
   RepositoryException occurs for the end user.
   The full stack trace is returned as the cause and is seen in the
   UI.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  A change was made to return the simple error string and log the
   RepositoryException to the Portal log files.
