   associated.
   
   2. Go to Organization (T&D) and add the three crafts into the
   T&D
   Options ? Configure Crafts section.
   
   3. Go to Administration, Resources, Crew Types (T&D).
   
   4. Create a new Crew Type with the three crafts.
   
   5. Go to Administration, Resources, Crew Management (T&D).
   
   6. Create a new Crew in Crew Management and associate the new
   crew type
   with a calendar and shift.
   
   7. Add Labor Assignments for each Craft on the Labor Assignments
   tab.
   
   8. Go to Work Orders ? Work Orders (T&D)
   
   9. Create a new WO, go to the Plans tab, click new row under the
   Labor
   window.
   
   10. Add the new Crew into the Crew field and press tab.
   
   11. The following error is received.
   
   Client is getting a BMXAA4214E - An unknown error has occured.
   Please
   contact your system administrator for assistance.
