ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  A customer reported issue with Traveler client when the switch
   from WLAN connection to GPRS connection on Nokia s60 device.

 *  NA
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customer reported that if they switch from WLAN to GPRS
   connection on a Nokia device, Traveler would not reconnect on
   GPRS.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The Traveler client was updated to hanlde this scenario.
