   Learn in less than two minutes about the differences between the default and manual scan configurations for IBM BigFix Inventory and IBM License Metric Tool. 
   
   Open Mic replay: Steps to perform proper bundling from Passport Advantage to PVU report - 2018-02-07 [https://www.securitylearningacademy.com/course/view.php?id=2543]
