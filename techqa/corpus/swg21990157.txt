Title: IBM Adding a company logo to Hold Notice content - United States

Text:
Logo Atlas Hold Content TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do you add a company logo to a Hold Notice content? 

CAUSE
Uploading an image using the rich text editor may sometimes not work

ANSWER
You can use the $image text editor place holder in the main body or the header to include the company logo either in the Hold Notice or Hold Notice Template
