probe nco-p-alcatel-5620-sam-v10-3_0 5620 sam release 12 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Does the Probe for Alcatel-Lucent 5620 SAM support SAM Release 12.0? 

ANSWER
The probe nco-p-alcatel-5620-sam-v10-2_0 only supports Alcatel 5620 SAM v10 and Alcatel 5620 SAM v11.
Use probe nco-p-alcatel-5620-sam-v10-3_0 which supports Alcatel-Lucent 5620 SAM Release 12.
