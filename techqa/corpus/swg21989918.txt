Title: IBM Atlas Hold Notice Template update and Published Notice - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Does updating a Hold Notice template affect a published Notice? 

CAUSE
Hold Notice content was updated after Hold Notice using that template was published

ANSWER
Any update to a Hold Notice template ( Rules or Content) after a Hold Notice is created and published, will not affect the one that was already created. New Hold Notice created using the template after you make the change will only be impacted
