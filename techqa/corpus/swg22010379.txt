Title: IBM How to upgrade older versions of IBM SPSS to new versions - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 I am using an older version of an IBM SPSS product. I want to upgrade to the newest version but I do not know the steps. How can I upgrade my IBM SPSS software to the newest version? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Upgrading is easy. Please contact your local SPSS Sales group. Contact information can be located at the following link: 

https://www.ibm.com/planetwide/ [https://www.ibm.com/planetwide/] 

In North America, call IBM directly at 1-800-543-2185 and use Priority code SPSS. 

We have also included two documents you can use for quick information on SPSS Statistics. One lists what is new in SPSS Statistics 25 and another is an easy to use order form. 

Lastly, see a quick video on what's new in SPSS Statistics!

https://developer.ibm.com/predictiveanalytics/2017/07/18/spss-25-subscription-summary/ [https://developer.ibm.com/predictiveanalytics/2017/07/18/spss-25-subscription-summary/]
