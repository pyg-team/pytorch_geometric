Title: IBM Content Manager OnDemand Windows Client does not print hightlights and stamps - United States

Text:
highlight stamp note OnDemand TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why do I not see highlights and stamps in the printout after I hit the 'Print All Notes' button on CMOD Windows client? 

ANSWER
Hightlights and stamps are considered graphical notes, which are not printable by design.
