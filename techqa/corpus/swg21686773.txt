Title: IBM How to solve the following information in the p8 error log: FNRCE0001E: E_ACCESS_DENIED: The requester has insufficient access rights to perform the requested operation. - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 FNRCE0001E: E_ACCESS_DENIED: The requester has insufficient access rights to perform the requested operation. 

SYMPTOM
Caused by: com.filenet.api.exception.EngineRuntimeException: FNRCE0001E: E_ACCESS_DENIED: The requester has insufficient access rights to perform the requested operation. failedBatchItem=0 errorStack={ 


CAUSE
Caused by: com.filenet.api.exception.EngineRuntimeException: FNRCE0001E: E_ACCESS_DENIED: The requester has insufficient access rights to perform the requested operation. failedBatchItem=0 errorStack={ 

Note that the error code FNRCE, seems to indicate that it is a Content Engine (CE) error. I am not a CE expert, but most likely security related, a user who is trying to make a change to a CE object doesn't have permission to make the change. From the stack below, it looks like a component manager step is being executed.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
This error could be normal correct behavior, perhaps the user is trying to make a change that they are not allowed to make, or perhaps the permissions on the document being changed are not correct. Customer will need to look at the document in CE that is having the problem and investigate what the user is trying to do and verify that the user has permission to make the change they are trying to make.
