Title: IBM BMXAA6421E The system could not connect to the JDBC source - United States

Text:
TPAESCCD; Cannot; start; maximo; sccd; BMXAA6421E TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Encounter error BMXAA6421E when starting Maximo/SCCD. 

SYMPTOM
Receive following error in SystemOut.log :-
[ERROR] [] [] BMXAA6421E - The system could not connect to the JDBC source:
jdbc:db2://DB2_hostname:50005/maxdb75[jcc][t4][2043][11550][4.11.69] Exception java.net.ConnectException: Error opening socket to server DB2_hostname/192.168.1.1 on port 50,005 with message:
Connection refused: connect. ERRORCODE=-4499, SQLSTATE=08001


CAUSE
DB2 services are not started.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In Window Service, make sure DB2 services are started e.g. 

DB2 - DB2COPY - CTGINST1-0
DB2 - DB2COPY - DB2-0
