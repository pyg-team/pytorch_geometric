Title: IBM Cannot upgrade IBM Business Process Manager on Solaris with IBM Installation Manager V1.8.8 - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 If you are running on Solaris and want to upgrade IBM Business Process Manager (BPM), you cannot use IBM Installation Manager V1.8.8 to install the upgrade. 

SYMPTOM
The installation fails with an error message similar to:
Business Process Management FixPack nnncannot be installed on the current platform 

 where nnn is the upgrade level


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
To resolve the problem, upgrade Installation Manager to V1.8.9 or later, and re-install the IBM BPM upgrade.
