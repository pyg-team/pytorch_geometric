ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  There is a Scroll bar in Mail thread in iNotes.
   However, when a user changes the size of Internet Explorer
   Window, the Scroll bar is disappeared.
   
   (Steps to reproduce)
   1. Setup Domino 9.0.1 FP5 and register 2 users (User1 and
   User2, User2 is iNotes user with Internet Explorer 11 or 8.)
   2. User1 sends a mail to User2.
   3. User2 reply it to User1.
   4. Repeat Step 2 and 3 with more 12 times in each user. (There
   are more 24 threads.)
   5. User2 select the last mail from User1.
   6. Click to open or collapse preview pane.
   7. Click "Threads"
   8. You can see the scroll bar in the right side in Mail Threads.
   9. If you change the size of Internet Explorer Window, the
   scroll bar is disappeared. <- Reproduced
   
   After reproduction, if you choose other mail, the scroll bar is
   appeared again in the Mail Threads.
   
   If you use Firefox, this problem is not reproduced.
