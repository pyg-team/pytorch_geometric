 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 This flash documents the status of IBM product and service repositories that are accessed using the IBM Installation Manager and IBM Packaging Utility during install and upgrade of IBM products. 

CONTENT
This flash contains status information regarding issues (downtime or expected service windows) for the IBM product and service repositories accessed through the IBM Installation Manager and Packaging Utility. We will update this status page when the repositories are inaccessible (OFFLINE) and when repositories are accessible (ONLINE). We work to bring the repository access ONLINE as a high priority.
