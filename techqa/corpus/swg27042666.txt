Title: IBM Worklight Foundation, IBM Worklight, and IBM Mobile Foundation support Apple iOS 8 Beta 4 - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 IBM Worklight and IBM Mobile Foundation V5.0.6 Fix Pack 2 and V6.0.0 Fix Pack 2, IBM Worklight V6.1.0 Fix Pack 1 and IBM Worklight Foundation V6.2 support the development of applications running on Apple iOS 8 Beta 4 with a list of known issues and solutions. 

CONTENT
As of July 2014, IBM Worklight Studio and Device Runtime supports the development of mobile applications running on Apple iOS 8 Beta 4. For further information, see IBM Worklight and iOS 8 (Mobile playground for IBM Worklight) [http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/blogs/worklightsmobileplayground/entry/ibm_worklight_and_ios_8]
