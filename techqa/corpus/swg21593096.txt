Title: IBM Reverting the Navigation Bar from Left Pane to the Toolbar - United States

Text:
navbar; Go To; left pane; Navigation; bar; TPAECUSTOMIZATION TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can the Navigation Bar functionality be reverted to the toolbar instead of the 'Left Pane'? 

ANSWER
Within SmartCloud 7.5, out of the box, the Navigation Bar is now a 'Left Pane' within the UI:
[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=1] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=1]

To enable the Navigation Bar to be contained within the Toolbar, please follow the below steps:

1) Go To – System Configuration – Platform Configuration – System Properties – within the Global Properties pane, filter for 'mxe.webclient.systemNavBar':

[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=2] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=2]

2) Change the Global Property 'mxe.webclient.systemNavBar' from 1 to 0, click on Save – click on the checkbox corresponding with the 'mxe.webclient.systemNavBar' – click on the 'Live Refresh' on the toolbar:

[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=3] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=3]

Click 'OK' to commit the changes:

[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=4] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=4]

3) Navigate to the Start Center. The 'Left Pane' will now be removed and the functionality of the Navigation Bar will be contained within the toolbar for all Applications:

[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=5] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21593096&aid=5]
