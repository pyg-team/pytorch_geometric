Title: IBM The IBM Business Process Manager (BPM), WebSphere Process Server (WPS), and WebSphere Lombardi Edition products are not affected by the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 These products are not vulnerable to the CVE-2014-0160 OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability. 

CONTENT
The following product editions and platforms are NOT vulnerable to the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160): 

 * IBM Business Process Manager Standard
   
   
   
 * IBM Business Process Manager Express
   
   
   
 * IBM Business Process Manager Advanced
   
   
   
 * WebSphere Process Server
   
   
   
 * WebSphere Lombardi Edition



Remediation: No action is required. 

Change History: 11 April 2014: original document published
