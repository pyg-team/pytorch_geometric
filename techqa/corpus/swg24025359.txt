ABSTRACT
 IBM WebSphere Insurance Content Pack version 7.0 is a prebuilt accelerator for the WebSphere Business Process Management (BPM) offering. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
This document provides the eAssembly part number "CR9K5ML" required for download of the IBM WebSphere Insurance Content Pack 7.0 product from the Passport Advantage Web site [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm]. 

The assets provided in the IBM WebSphere Insurance Content Pack and their description is provided here.

Insurance Capability Models: The Insurance Capability Models are based on the Association for Cooperative Operations Research and Development (ACORD), Industrial Research Institute (IRI) standard, and IBM best practices.

Insurance Process Models: The Insurance Process Models are based on IBM best practices. The Insurance Process Models are the foundation for implementation of the insurance-specific BPM solution, enabling it to be reused across multiple solutions.

Insurance Service Models: The Insurance Service Models are based on IBM IAA-IDM and IBM best practices. The Service Models are prebuilt service interfaces and schemas that facilitate interoperability across diverse platforms for insurance industry. These industry standards provide the messaging schemas that can be used to define the Industry Service Interfaces.

Insurance Common Components: The Insurance Common Components are based on ACORD, the Industrial Research Institute (IRI) standard, the HR-XML standard, and IBM best practices. The Insurance Common Components include common services that can be used as-is or can be extended to enable transactional functions.

Insurance Business Vocabulary: The Insurance Business Vocabulary is based on ACORD, e-Business Expert Group for Insurance (eEG7), IAA, Industrial Research Institute (IRI) standard, and IBM best practices. The Insurance Business Vocabulary is used to provide more information about the relationships between industry-specific terms. The Insurance Business Vocabulary is a taxonomy of the business terms for the insurance industry.

Insurance Business Object Models: The Insurance Business Object Model is based on ACORD, eEG7, and IBM best practices. The Insurance Business Object Models is a logical representation of business entities and attributes that set up a business domain or a subvertical within a domain.

Insurance Solution Scenarios: The Insurance Solution Scenarios are out-of-box packaged BPM implementation as a reference to understand end-to-end delivery of a BPM solution for the insurance industry. They use most of assets from the WebSphere Insurance Content Pack, which have either been used or extended as per the use case requirement.
