QUESTION
 You are having a problem while deploying RuleApps to Rule Execution Server (RES). You would like to know what documentation you must collect (MustGather) so that the IBM Operation Decision Manager Support team can diagnose your problem. If you gather this documentation before contacting support it will expedite the troubleshooting process, and save you time. 

ANSWER
1. Version and fixpack level of ODM modules used.
- The Rule Execution Server console (click About on the top right of the console)
- The Decision Center console (click About on the top right of the console)
- The Rule Designer (Help > About Eclipse JDK, click IBM icon)
For more details, see Find the version of JRules/WODM components [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21400738]. 

2. The steps used to generate and deploy the ruleapps.
