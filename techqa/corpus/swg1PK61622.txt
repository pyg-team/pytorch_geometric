ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When delete MailAddress of some users continuously, the first
   deletion successes but the subsequent deletion is not reflected
   in LDAP. 'Modify Request' of the subsequent deletion is not sent
   to LDAP server from WebSphere Portal.
   
   0. prepare two users like the following on WP.
   
    UserID: user1/user2
    Email:  user1@lotus.com/user2@lotus.com
   
   1. logon as administrator and move [Access] - [Users and Groups]
      - "All Authenticated Portal Users".
   2. click [Edit] icon of 'user1'.
   3. delete 'Email' value and click [OK].
   4. click [Edit] icon of 'user1' again.
   -> Email value was deleted correctly.
   
   5. click [Edit] icon of 'user2'.
   6. delete 'Email' value and click [OK].
   7. click [Edit] icon of 'user2' again.
   
   -->> 'Email' value remained in the field. It was not deleted and
   was kept as "user2@lotus.com", even though an administrator had
   deleted the value certainly.
