QUESTION
 How to troubleshoot installation and upgrade on OMNIbus 7.x? 

ANSWER
Fix the cause of the errors, e.g. increase disk space in file system, install required prerequisite components, and then attempt the installation/upgrade again.


If error message is not indicative of the cause then contact your local support center to raise a PMR with the product support team for further assistance.
