QUESTION
 Do we have any patches or Fix Pack for IBM SPSS Concurrent Licensing Tools ? 

ANSWER
Our IBM SPSS licensing technology is a third party product from Safenet.
We do not have patches for theses products but if there are issues we report them to Safenet development.
When they resolved the issue, we implement the updates in our next versions of SPSS products.
