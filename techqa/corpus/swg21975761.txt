Title: IBM CSP999E snode security is incompatible with  configuration and pnode settings. - United States

Text:
TLS SSP CSP999E TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The connection between two IBM Sterling Connect:Direct nodes through IBM Sterling Secure Proxy, using TLS v1.0 for the pnode and TLS v1.2 for the snode fails. 

SYMPTOM
CSP999E snode security is incompatible with configuration and pnode settings.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In IBM Sterling Secure Proxy enable the 'Inbound and outbound sessions can have different levels of encryption' field in the Sterling Connect:Direct Adapter Configuration Advanced tab.
