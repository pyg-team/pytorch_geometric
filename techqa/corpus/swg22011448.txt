Title: IBM DPR-ERR-2056 while running reports in pdf and excel - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Simple Reports with hardly 300-400 records fail with DPR-ERR-2056 while running in pdf and excel .
Reports work fine in html,csv,xml 

CAUSE
Report design had images which could not be found at the defined location

ENVIRONMENT
Issue occurred in a new migrated Cognos environment

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Added all the required images from the old Cognos Environment to the New Cognos environment in the same location that was defined in the reports (image path)
