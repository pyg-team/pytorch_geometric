DASH; JazzSM; Widget; detailed; information; support. TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can I get the detailed information about a Widget shown on a Page in DASH? 

CAUSE
Sometimes this information is needed by IBM support to assist in diagnosing problem.

ANSWER
Assessing detailed information about a Widget implemented in DASH. 

1) Access the page in DASH that contains the Widgets, in the upper right of the screen select the "Page Options" icon shown below: 


[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21974000&amp;aid=1]

Right click on the Widget itself, then select "About"


[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21974000&amp;aid=2]

This will open a new Window with all the information about the Widget listed below:
