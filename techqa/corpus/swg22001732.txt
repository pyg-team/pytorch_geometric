Title: IBM Matter Dashboard loads slowly in StoredIQ for Legal - United States

Text:
StoredIQ for Legal Matters TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does Matter Dashboard load slowly in StoredIQ for Legal ? 

CAUSE
This is caused by dashboard cache settings

ANSWER
 

 1. Login to StoredIQ for Legal as ilgadmin 
 2. Navigate to Admin > System Settings 
 3. Under Dashboard section, set Cache to Yes 
 4. Save the changes 
 5. Logout
