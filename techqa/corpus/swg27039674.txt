 DETAILED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

ABSTRACT
 The IBM® InfoSphere® Optim™ Test Data Management Enterprise Edition and IBM InfoSphere Optim Test Data Management Work Group Edition consist of a number of components. The system requirements for the components are compiled in this document.
