ABSTRACT
 This webcast is appropriate for IBM Infrastructure Security clients who have either recently moved from GX to XGS or are considering the move. Considerations such as which policy contents will port over to XGS and which will not port were examined along with other support tips. 

CONTENT
IBM wants your product deployment to go smoothly. In this Open Mic, callers heard how to make that happen. Our goal in Open Mics is to provide insight on how IBM Security products work and to teach on-going sessions that help users and administrators understand, maintain, troubleshoot, and resolve issues with their XGS appliance. This Open Mic session was the sixth webcast provided by IBM Infrastructure Security Technical Support. 

 * Topic: Policy migration from GX to XGS 
 * New Date: Tuesday, April 28, 2015 
 * Time: 11:00 AM ET (15:00 UTC/GMT, UTC-4 hours) for 60 minutes
