Title: IBM Process Engine Unable to launch Workflow from Filenet Workplace XT, got error. - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Unable to launch Workflow from Filenet Workplace XT, got the following error,

"Internal system error" 

SYMPTOM
Unable to launch workflow


CAUSE
Open the workflow map in Process Designer and check what step processor is used for the workflow steps. If the Content Navigator step processor is used, change it to the HTML step processor (Filenet) since Workplace XT is unable to parse ICN step processors.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
If the Content Navigator step processor is used, change the steps to use the HTML step processors (Filenet).
