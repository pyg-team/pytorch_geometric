Title: IBM CWLAA6003 error occurs while saving filters in Netcool WebGUI - United States

Text:
TIPL2; TIPL2FAQ; CWLAA6003 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 The following error occurs in the Tivoli Integrated Portal while saving filters:

CWLAA6003: Could not display the portlet at this time, the portlet's module may be being updated 

SYMPTOM
The SystemOut.log file was showing java.lang.NullPointerException at the time issue occurred. 

PortletServle E com.ibm.ws.portletcontainer.core.PortletServlet doDispatch EJPPC0104E: Error occurred while dispatching portlet
java.lang.NullPointerException

ServletWrappe E SRVE0068E: Uncaught exception thrown in one of the service methods of the servlet: DashboardPortlet. Exception thrown : javax.servlet.ServletException
javax.portlet.PortletException 


Nested Exception is java.lang.NullPointerException
at com.ibm.isclite.service.datastore.portletentities.AbstractPortletEntityServiceImpl.saveWindowPortletPreferences(AbstractPortletEntityServiceImpl.java:1062)
at com.ibm.isclite.container.provider.PersistenceProviderImpl.storePortletrPreferences(PersistenceProviderImpl.java:538)
at com.ibm.isclite.container.provider.PersistenceProviderImpl.store(PersistenceProviderImpl.java:307)


CAUSE
Above exceptions suggest that some of the xml files may be corrupted.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Please provide below files for investigation. The location of files depends on version of Tivoli Integrated Portal. You can run listIU.sh (or in Windows listIU.bat) command to find out TIP version. 

TIP 1.x version: 

<TIPHOME>/profiles/TIPProfile/config/cells/TIPCell/applications/isclite.ear/deployments/isclite/isclite.war/WEB-INF 

TIP 2.x version:
<TIPHOME>/profiles/TIPProfile/config/cells/TIPCell/applications/isc.ear/deployments/isc/isclite.war/WEB-INF 

You may use below technote as a guideline to determine if some files are corrupted and fix them if needed.
