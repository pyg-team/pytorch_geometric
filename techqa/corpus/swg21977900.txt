Title: IBM Sametime Chat Window Does Not Launch Correctly With MAC PC - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM
Same chat Window does not launch with Sametime 9 client on MAC PC.

SYMPTOM
Customer installed Lotus Notes 9 and upgraded the Sametime Embedded client with part # CN6VDEN. When attempting to start a chat with a user that is online the sametime chat window never opens


ENVIRONMENT
Customer is running MAC OSX10 El Capitan Version 10.11.3
