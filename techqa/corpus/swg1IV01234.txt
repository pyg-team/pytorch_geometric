ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  replicated in 7116
   JobTaskSpec are not deleted after JobPlan or JobTask are deleted
   
   Steps to reproduce the issue:
   1. Go to -> Planning -> Job Plan
   2. Create a Job Plan named TEST1.
   3. Associate a Classification with it (one Classification that
   got
   Attributes). Add a Job Plan Task and associate the same
   Classification.
   4. Save.
   5. Click at Select Action -> Delete Job Plan.
   6. Create a new Job Plan named TEST1.
   7. Associate a Classification with it (one Classification that
   got
   Attributes).
   8. Save and the error will happen.
   
   BMXAA4129E The record already exists to Job Plan=x, Task=y,
   Attribute=z,
   Secction=.
