Title: IBM Maximo Asset Management SaaS users with Maximo Scheduler 7.5 versions experience TOC problems in the documentation in Knowledge Center - United States

Text:
msc751defect; msc752defect; TPAESCHED; TPAESCHEDULER TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When opening application help from inside a Maximo Scheduler application, the help is displayed with the incorrect table of contents in the left hand pane. 

SYMPTOM
The incorrect table of content displays. The user sees the Maximo Asset Management SaaS table of contents instead of the Maximo Asset Management Scheduler table of contents.


ENVIRONMENT
This problem affects Maximo Asset Management Scheduler 7.5.2 users who run Maximo Asset Management SaaS.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Using the navigation in the table of contents (left hand) pane, select Back to All Products, choose Maximo Asset Management Scheduler, and then choose the correct release.
