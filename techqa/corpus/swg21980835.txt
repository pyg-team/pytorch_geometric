Title: IBM Where can I find the compatibility information for the old IBM TRIRIGA versions? - United States

Text:
TRIINSTALL; TRICONFIG; Compatibility; matrix; information; old; version TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Where can I find the compatibility information for the old IBM TRIRIGA versions? 

CAUSE
Need to check compatibility for the old IBM TRIRIGA versions, and the page I could find only shows this information for the most recent version only.

ANSWER
The IBM TRIRIGA compatibility information for the old versions is available on this link: Archive of IBM TRIRIGA Support Matrices [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/wikis/home?lang=en#!/wiki/IBM+TRIRIGA1/page/Archive+of+IBM+TRIRIGA+Support+Matrices]

When opening it, click on the Attachments tab for the complete IBM TRIRIGA Support and Compatibility Matrix information for the old versions in PDF files.
