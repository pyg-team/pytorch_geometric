 DETAILED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

ABSTRACT
 IBM Tivoli Netcool Performance Manager, Version 1.4.1 is a bundled product that consists of two main components, a Wireline Component and a Wireless Component.
Tivoli Netcool Performance Manager 1.4.1 and its associated components have hardware and software requirements, including supported middleware and installation technologies.
