Title: IBM Compatible Rational SoDA and Rational Requisite Pro version with Rational ClearCase 7.1.2.4 - United States

Text:
SoDA; Requisite Pro; Clearcase; version; compatible TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What is the compatible version of IBM Rational SoDA and IBM Rational Requisite Pro with IBM Rational ClearCase 7.1.2.4? 

CAUSE
Rational SoDA 7.0.4.3 and Rational Requisite Pro 7.1.1.1 installation does not proceed due to compatibility issues with Rational ClearCase 7.1.2.4.

ANSWER
With reference to the document id 4028116 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24028116], 7.0.4, 7.1.2, and the 8.1.1 licensing components are aligned releases. They are supported for use with each other when installed on the same machine. 


However , you need to install Rational SoDA 7.0.4.4 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24030544] and Rational Requisite Pro 7.1.2.04 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24030766] with the Rational ClearCase 7.1.2.04.
