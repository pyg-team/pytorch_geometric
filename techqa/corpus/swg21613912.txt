Title: IBM Changing scan timeout in 7.2.0 - United States

Text:
sw_scan_timeout; ILMT; agent; scan TECHNOTE (FAQ)

THIS DOCUMENT APPLIES ONLY TO THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE VERSION(S):
 English 

QUESTION
 How do I change sw_scan_timeout in ILMT 7.2.0 

ANSWER
For agents version 7.2.0 or less this "sw_scan_timeout" can not
be adjusted. Please upgrade the agent to version 7.2.1 or higher.
