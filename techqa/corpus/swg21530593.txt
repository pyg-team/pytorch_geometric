STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can I view detailed statistics one page at a time? (SCI86256) 

ANSWER


This can be achieved with a simple script, similar to the following:


NDMAPICFG=/path/to/ndm/cfg/cliapi/ndmapi.cfg
export NDMAPICFG
/path/to/ndm/bin/direct << EOJ > statlog<BR>sel stat pnum=$1 detail=yes;<BR>quit;<BR>EOJ<BR>more statlog<BR>rm statlog
