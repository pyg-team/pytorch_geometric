INFORMATION CENTER
All of the information that is in the PDF documentation is included in the information center, where you can easily search across all documentation. In the information center, the documentation will be displayed in your language (if it has been translated into that language) based on your browser locale setting. The information center is updated more often than the PDF documentation.
