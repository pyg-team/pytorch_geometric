APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS DUPLICATE OF ANOTHER APAR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  SSL connection can result in NullPointerException in
   RACFInputStream.
   
   With SSL enabled the following may be seen:
   ExtendedMessage: BBOO0220E: SSLC0008E: Unable to initialize SSL
   connection.
    Unauthorized access was denied or security settings have
   expired.
   Exception is javax.net.ssl.SSLHandshakeException:
   
   
   The following stack trace can be seen with this problem:
   
   java.lang.NullPointerException
                  at
   com.ibm.crypto.provider.RACFInputStream.<init>(Unknown Source)
                  at
   com.ibm.crypto.provider.safkeyring.a.getInputStream(Unknown
   Source)
                  at java.net.URL.openStream(URL.java:1041)
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  1) cd <JAVA_HOME>/lib/security
   2) cp cacerts cacerts.ori
   3) keytool -delete -alias thawtepremiumserverca
   -keystore cacerts -storepass changeit
