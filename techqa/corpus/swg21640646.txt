Title: IBM WODM 7.5.0.1 installation not showing in ILMT console - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does my IBM Websphere Operational Decision Management (WODM) 7.5.0.1 installation not shown in the IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) console under those server clients for this software? 

CAUSE
 ILMT uses tags that are not properly configured in early revision of IBM WebSphere Operational Decision Management.

ANSWER
Full support for ILMT has been added in IBM WebSphere Operational Decision Management in fix pack 7.5.0.2. You must update your installation to the latest fix pack of V7.5 to be able to find the components of WebSphere Operational Decision Management V7.5
