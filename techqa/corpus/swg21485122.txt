Title: IBM Collaboration and Deployment Services 4.2 and Decision Management 6.1 messages are still being written to SystemOut - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When installing C&DS and DM6, the log4j.xml file is used to direct all C&DS and DM6 messages to a seperate log file, to keep the systemOut.log file clean. Even message from source SystemOut are being printed in the systemOut.log file , which might indicate that in our source code still System.out.println () are located.
