CAUSE
Client device has incompatible Excel add-in installed.

Example:
In one real-life case, the client PC had the Essbase 'Excel and EQD COM Addin' (version ESB11.1.2.1.102B147) installed.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Disable the incompatible Excel add-in. 

 
Steps:
The following steps are based on Office 2010. The steps will be different for different versions of MS Office.
1. Launch Microsoft Excel
2. Click "File - Options"
[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21970966&amp;aid=1]

3. Click 'Add-ins':
[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21970966&amp;aid=2] 

4. Locate any active non-CDM Excel add-ins, which are listed inside 'Active Application Add-ins' 

 * Disable these


5. Test.
