ABSTRACT
 This document describes how to download IBM Datacap Version 9.1.2. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION
To download IBM Datacap Version 9.1.2 from Fix Central, complete the following instructions:


 1.  Contact IBM representative to obtain the Individual fix IDs. For more information, see IBM Support [https://www.ibm.com/support/home/?lnk=ushpv18hcwh1&lnk2=support]. 
 2.  Click this link: https://www-945.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/ [https://www-945.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/]. The Fix Central page opens, and displays the Find Product section. 
 3.  Sign in using your IBM credentials. If you have forgotten your IBM ID or have never registered, complete the instructions on the sign-in page. 
 4.  In the Product selector field, type Datacap, and click the Go arrow. 
 5.  From the list, select Datacap. 
 6.  From the Installed Version list, select 9.1.2.0, and click Continue. 
 7.  In the Platform list, ensure that Windows 64-bit, x86 is displayed, and then click Continue. 
 8.  On the Identify fixes page, in the Individual fix IDs field, type the fix ID in the format as: 9.1.2.0_Datacap_Production_Install:xxxxxxx, and then click Continue. 
 9.  On the Select fixes page, verify that the correct product version is selected, and then click Continue. 
 10. On the Download options page, select the appropriate option, and click Continue. 
 11. Complete the on-screen instructions to complete the download.
