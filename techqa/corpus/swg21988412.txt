Title: IBM Alert is not triggered when no attorney paralegal specified for a matter. - United States

Text:
atlas; alerts TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 When Alert Recipients added manually and no designated attorney and paralegal assigned to a matter in Atlas, Why does an Alert email is not getting triggered even though the preference set to receive alerts ?

.


CAUSE
Atlas requires for every matter a designated attorney and paralegal.

ANSWER
When there is no attorney or paralegal assigned for a matter ,alerts will not be generated even having alert recipients for that matter.
