Title: IBM Domino 9.0.1 Social Edition System Requirements - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
This document provides minimum system requirements for the 9.0.1 release of IBM Domino. Higher maintenance levels, such as fix packs and service packs, may be supported as they become available. 

CONTENT
The detailed system requirements information is the complete list of hardware requirements, supported operating systems, prerequisites and optional supported software and operating system restrictions.

For detailed system requirements for IBM Domino Administrator, IBM Domino Designer, IBM Notes, and IBM Notes Traveler, see: http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27007909 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27007909]

For detailed system requirements for IBM iNotes, see: http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27007763 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27007763]

For detailed system requirements for IBM Enterprise Integrator for Domino, see: https://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27011525 [https://www-304.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27011525]

For IBM i platform-specific information, see: http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27036983 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27036983]

 * Requirements by platform - The complete list of hardware requirements, supported operating systems, prerequisites and optional supported software and operating system restrictions.
