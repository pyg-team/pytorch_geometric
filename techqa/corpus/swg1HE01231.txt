   INFRASTRUCTURE: Fonts:  Hindi and Arab fonts don't work in
   CATIA.
   EXPLANATION:
   Hindi scripts and Arabic scripts are not supported currently.
   Please see the chapter "Starting
   a Session in a Language Other than English" in documentation,
   you can find the list of
   languages currently supported by CATIA.
   Documentation:

   in a Language Other than English on Windows\ Locales Whose
   Use with Version 5 has been validated
   .
   DEVELOPMENT REQUEST JUSTIFICATION:
   Supporting Hindi and Arabic scripts will involve lot of
   development efforts due to intricacies
   involved in these scripts and can be taken up only as
   separate planned enhancement .
