ABSTRACT
 This Open Mic about Sniffer restart & High CPU correlation alerts was held on 14 September, 2017. A copy of the presentation is attached and a full replay is available through the IBM Security Support YouTube channel and through the IBM Security Learning Academy. 

CONTENT



This Open Mic covered how to troubleshoot underlying problems causing you to receive Sniffer restart & High CPU correlation alerts. This will include: 

 * Finding which appliance(s) are involved 
 * Narrowing down the time frame when it occurred 
 * Reviewing and interpreting logs for that appliance to identify the cause 
 * Steps to resolve the issue
