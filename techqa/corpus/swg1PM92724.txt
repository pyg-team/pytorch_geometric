Title: IBM PM92724: MLS AND SMP PROPERTIES MAY BE MISSING AFTER SERVER UPDATE - United States

Text:

Fixes integrated in WebSphere Portal 8.0.0.1 Combined Cumulative Fixes [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24034497]



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The mls.properties and smp.properties files may not be copied to
   the profile directory when upgrading from 8000 directly to 8001
   CF05 or 8001 CF06.
   
   As a result, executing the required ConfigEngine task to deploy
   the respective WCM extensions will fail due to the missing
   properties file.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  The mls.properties and smp.properties files may not be copied to
   the profile directory when upgrading from 8000 directly to 8001
   CF05 or 8001 CF06.
   
   As a result, executing the required ConfigEngine task to deploy
   the respective WCM extensions will fail due to the missing
   properties file.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The mls.properties and smp.properties will be copied into the
   profile directory if required during the CF installation
   process.
