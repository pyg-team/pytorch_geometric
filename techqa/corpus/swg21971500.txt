Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Vulnerabilities in GSKit affect IBM MQ Appliance (CVE-2015-7421, CVE-2015-7420) - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 Vulnerabilities in GSKit affect IBM MQ Appliance. IBM MQ Appliance has addressed the applicable CVEs. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID:CVE-2015-7421 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2015-7421]
DESCRIPTION:An unspecified vulnerability in GSKit could allow a remote attacker to obtain sensitive information.
CVSS Base Score: 3.7
CVSS Temporal Score: Seehttps://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/107695 [https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/107695]for the current score
