Title: IBM NENU Fails to Delete Batches in IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture - United States

Text:
nenu delete oracle TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 NENU is unable to delete batches in an Oracle 11g database. 

SYMPTOM
No batches are returned to NENU from the query search.


CAUSE
QuerySetAge() is unable to do a proper query against Oracle 11g.

ENVIRONMENT
Oracle 11g database

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Upgrading to 8.1.0.2 will resolve the problem.
