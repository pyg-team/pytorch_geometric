Title: IBM Which Connect:Direct for z/OS releases support TLS v1.0, v1.1 and TLS v1.2? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Which TLS versions are supported by IBM Sterling Connect:Direct for z/OS? 

ANSWER
TLS v1.0, v1.1, and v1.2 are supported by the following Connect:Direct for z/OS releases.

Connect:Direct for z/OS, Release 5.0 – TLS v1.0
Connect:Direct for z/OS, Release 5.1 – TLS v1.0
Connect:Direct for z/OS, Release 5.2 – TLS v1.0, TLS v1.1, and TLS v1.2
