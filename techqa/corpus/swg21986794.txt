Title: IBM How to confirm a Global Hold Reminder after the next Global Hold Reminder issued ? - United States

Text:
atlas ghr TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How to confirm a Global Hold Reminder (GHR) after the next GHR issued in Atlas ? 

CAUSE
When you generate a corresponding report the custodian record for this GHR will shows as "No Reply"

ANSWER
Always the latest GHR notice takes precedence. Which means if a GHR is missed then you can only confirm the latest GHR notice.
