Title: IBM Disable ITCAM for J2EE 6.1 Data Collector on JBOSS JVM on UNIX - United States

Text:
J2EE JBOSS CAMJ2JBDC CAMJ2ALL TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How is the ITCAM J2EE DC disabled from starting up when the JBOSS JVM starts? 

ANSWER
Comment out the following lines that begin with JAVA_OPTS in the run.sh script, which is used during the start up of the JBOSS JVM:


JAVA_OPTS="-agentlib:am_ibm_15 -Xbootclasspath/p:${TOOLKIT_HOME}/lib/bcm-bootstrap.jar:${AM_HOME}/lib/ppe.probe-bootstrap.jar:${AM_HOME}/lib/ext/jboss/jbossbcm.jar"

JAVA_OPTS="$JAVA_OPTS -Dam.appserver=$APPSERVER -Dam.nodename=$NODENAME -Dappserver.platform=$PLATFORM -Dsun.rmi.client.logCalls=false -Djava.rmi.server.logCalls=false"

JAVA_OPTS="$JAVA_OPTS -Dtoolkit.home=$PRODUCT_HOME/toolkit -Dam.home=$PRODUCT_HOME/itcamdc -Ditcam61.home=$PRODUCT_HOME -Dcom.ibm.tivoli.transperf.instr.probes.impl.was.Globals.traceLevel=0 -Dorg.omg.PortableInterceptor.ORBInitializerClass.com.ibm.tivoli.itcam.dc.orbinterpretor.Initializer"

JAVA_OPTS="$JAVA_OPTS -Djava.rmi.server.RMIClassLoaderSpi=com.ibm.tivoli.itcam.jboss.sdc.ItcamJBossRMIClassLoader -verbose:gc -Xverbosegclog:/opt/apps/IBM/itcam/DC/jboss4-gc-log.log.SEServer025_01"
