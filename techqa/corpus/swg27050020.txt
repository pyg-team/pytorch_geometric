ABSTRACT
 IBM Security Guardium Support hosted an Open Mic on Tuesday 18, July 2017 to discuss the troubleshooting of 'no traffic' issues. 

CONTENT
Many Guardium issues show up as traffic missing from a report. There are so many different possible causes it's difficult to know where to look. This Open Mic discussed troubleshooting strategies you can use to quickly narrow down the issue and focus efforts on the real root cause of the missing traffic.
