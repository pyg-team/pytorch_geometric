LOCAL FIX
 *  Please update less than 64 DB CFG parameters to avoid the
   unexpected behavior.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  UPDATE DB CFG parameters more than 64 at a time, may result in
   unexpected behaviour.
