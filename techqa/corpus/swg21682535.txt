Title: IBM The URL of the product Welcome page for IBM Mobile Foundation, IBM Worklight, and IBM Worklight Foundation documentation has changed - United States

Text:
IMF50; IMF60; WL61; WL62; Worklight; Worklight Foundation; Mobile Foundation TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 In the Knowledge Center, the Welcome page for the IBM Mobile Foundation, IBM Worklight, and IBM Worklight Foundation product documentation is no longer available at the following URL: www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSZH4A/welcome.html 

SYMPTOM
You access the original Welcome page URL at http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSZH4A/welcome.html. However, you see the following error message: 

The topic was not found. The link might not be correct, or the topic does not exist. Check that the URL is correct and try again.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In the Knowledge Center, the product Welcome page for IBM Mobile Foundation, IBM Worklight, and IBM Worklight Foundation has changed URL, and is now available at http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSNJXP/welcome.html [http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSNJXP/welcome.html].
