Title: IBM PM61849: JUNCTION COOKIE WITH JUST A SLASH CAUSES PAGEBUILDER2 THEME TO FAIL - United States

Text:
  A FIX IS AVAILABLE
Fixes integrated in WebSphere Portal 7.0.0.1 & 7.0.0.2 Combined Cumulative Fixes [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24029452]

APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When using WebSeal as authentication gateway, it is possible to
   transport the junction as a cookie (IV_JCT). If this cookie
   contains just a slash ("/"), the PageBuilder2 theme in
   WebSphere Portal is unable to load the head.jsp and throws the
   following error:
   
   [EJCBD0021E: The URI [res:/PageBuilder2/themes/html/PageBuilder2
   /head.jsp] and parameters []] could not be processed:
   [EJCBC0009E: The entity [PageBuilder2/themes/html/PageBuilder2/h
   ead.jsp] could not be be found.]]]
   at org.apache.jasper.runtime.PageContextImpl
   .handlePageException(PageContextImpl.java:693)
   at com.ibm._jsp._Default._jspService(_Default.java:1327)
   at com.ibm.ws.jsp.runtime.HttpJspBase.service(HttpJspBase
   .java:99)
   at javax.servlet.http.HttpServlet.service(HttpServlet
   .java:831)
   at com.ibm.ws.cache.servlet.ServletWrapper
   .serviceProxied(ServletWrapper.java:307)
   at com.ibm.ws.cache.servlet.CacheHook.handleFragment(CacheHook
   .java:574)
   at com.ibm.ws.cache.servlet.CacheHook.handleServlet(CacheHook
   .java:250)
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Do not use a junction cookie with just "/".
