Title: IBM Unresolved rules after amending a map rule. - United States

Text:
WTX ITX Map unresolved rules corrupt .opt TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Unresolved rules after amending a map rule in the Transformation Extender Design Studio. 

SYMPTOM
Unresolved rules after amending a map rule and the map compiles successfully.


CAUSE
Sometimes the .MOPT file can get corrupted which leads to the behaviour of showing the rules in the output card and in the unresolved rules window. 


ENVIRONMENT
Using IBM WebSphere Transformation Extender (WTX)/IBM Transformation Extender (ITX) Eclipse based Map Designer and changing a map rule. 

The ITX/WTX Map compiles successfully but reports that there are some unresolved rules. 

DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
There are unresolved rules displayed and the map compiles successfully after amending a map rule.
