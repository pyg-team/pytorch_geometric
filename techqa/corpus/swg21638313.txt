CAUSE
The Modeler adapter installed into CADS environment is not compatible within the existing SPSS Collaboration and Deployment installation.

ANSWER
Ensure you use a Modeler adapter that is compatible with your version of SPSS Collaboration and Deployment Services installation. Also ensure that Modeler/C&DS adapter version coincides with the version of Modeler Client end users use. Compatibility information can be obtained from the link below.
