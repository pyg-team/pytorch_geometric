Title: IBM PureApplication System Release Note: Virtual machine deletions occasionally fail on W3700 systems - United States

Text:
v2110rlsnotes RELEASE NOTES

ABSTRACT
 An attempt to delete a virtual machine occasionally fails on W3700 systems. 

CONTENT
Problem:When you attempt to delete a virtual machine on a W3700 system, the deletion might fail due to a timeout in the PowerVC.

The error that is logged for this scenario is: 

 * 
 * 
 * <virtual machine name>
 * 
 * <server name>
 * 
 * 
 * 
 * <server name>
 * 
 * 

Resolution: Wait about 25 minutes and try the delete again.
