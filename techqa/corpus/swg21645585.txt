Title: IBM MFS generated COBOL copybooks use the MSG label as the 01 level identifier - United States

Text:
RDz; MFS TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 MFS generated COBOL copybooks use the MSG label as the 01 level identifier. 

CAUSE
Limitation

ANSWER
To prevent duplicate names in the copybook, COBOL copybooks generated from MFS source use the actual MSG label as the 01 level identifier in the COBOL copybooks instead of the generic INPUT-MSG and OUTPUT-MSG level 01 names.
