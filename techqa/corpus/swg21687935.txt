MDM; DB2; compatability TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is MDM v11 compatible with DB2 on z/OS v11 

CAUSE
N/A

ANSWER
MDM v11 Advanced Edition is compatible with DB2 on z/OS v11, but MDM v11 Standard Edition and Hybrid are not compatible with DB2 on z/OS v11 due to different versions of Data Direct.
