Title: IBM XWork Server 8.5.3 System Requirements - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 This document provides minimum system requirements for the 8.5.3 release of IBM XWork Server. Higher maintenance levels, such as fix packs and service packs, may be supported as they become available. However, new release versions are not supported at this time. 

CONTENT
