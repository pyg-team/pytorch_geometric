QUESTION

ANSWER
You can find information for all supported Versions of DB2 for z/OS in the IBM Knowledge Center [.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSEPEK/db2z_prodhome.html]. IBM Knowledge Center is a one-stop shop for IBM technical documentation. It provides an IBM-wide view of technical information for multiple offerings in a single location on the web. IBM Knowledge Center is the official home for technical product documentation, eventually replacing the information centers. 

You can also download PDFs for specific DB2 for z/OS manuals, in the DB2 for z/OS Product documentation page [https://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSEPEK_12.0.0/home/src/tpc/db2z_12_prodhome.html].
