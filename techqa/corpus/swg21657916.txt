Title: IBM CA Wily Introscope V9.0.8.2 might cause StackOverflowError when a custom application tries to launch Business Process Choreographer microflow for WebSphere Process Server (WPS) - United States

Text:
CA Wily Introscope 9.0.8.2; StackOverflowError; LocalActivityContext; workarea.ContextNode TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When you configure and enable CA Wily Introscope V9.0.8.2, a custom application uses the WebSphereBPELProvider class (the standard API) for managing process-related objects in Business Process Choreographer (com.ibm.bpe.api.*). The launchFlowWithInstanceName method attempts to kick off a Business Process Chareographer microflow. However, a StackOverflowError error returns intermittently and the microflow is not launched.
