Title: IBM Why does the Preservation Plan remains in Pending Approval state ? - United States

Text:
atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Atlas Preservation Plan is routed for approval and the approver as noted in Plan has approved the plan but Why does the Preservation Plan remains in "Pending Approval" state ? 

CAUSE
One possible cause could be using multiple browser tabs in a single browser session.

ANSWER
Using Multiple browser tabs in a single browser session is not supported in Atlas as it causes issues with session and data.
