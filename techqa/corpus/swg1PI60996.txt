APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  You are automating IBM SPSS Statistics via VB scripting in a
   Microsoft Excel macro.  Your macros works fine in a 64-bit
   environment (Widowns 64-bit, Office 64-bit, Statistics 64-bit).
   However, it fails with and Excel.exe APPCRASH related to the
   RPCTransport.dll.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  This issue will be addressed in the next Fix Pack. Please
   refer to the Fix Pack schedule at
   https://www-01.ibm.com/software/analytics/support/fixes/pbi_sche
   dule.html [https://www-01.ibm.com/software/analytics/support/fixes/pbi_schedule.html]  for
   information on upcoming Fix Packs.
   
   In the interim:
   
   The user can manually edit the line 25 and 26 in the
   C:\Program Files (x86)\IBM\SPSS\Statistics\23\jvmcfg.ini
   from
   -Xms256M
   -Xmx512M
   to
   -Xms128M
   -Xmx256M
   to have the issue fixed.
