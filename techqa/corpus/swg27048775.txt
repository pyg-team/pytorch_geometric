ABSTRACT
 Members of the IBM BigFix Inventory Support teamshared information on the Scan Health widget status. Attendees also got a glimpse of the new Support Agent Tool for IBM License Metric Tool in IBM Support Portal. 

CONTENT
For this open mic session, Support agent Les Popow explained the outdated catalog status on the Health Scan widget in detail, along with how to fix it. He also told about the IBM BigFix proxy settings scenario which may impact the catalog downloads. Les also told about the new Support Agent Tool that is available in the beta version of the new IBM Support Portal for IBM License Metric Tool. It was a rather educational session. 

 * Topic: Outdated Catalog status on the Scan Health widget, and how to fix it 
 * Date: Thursday, 29 September 2016 
 * Time: 11:00 AM EDT (15:00 UTC/GMT, UTC-4 hours) for 60 minutes
