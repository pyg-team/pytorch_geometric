PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Is there a limit on the number of cases and variables in SPSS for Windows? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
In SPSS 11.0 for Windows (and later), there is no case or variable limit on data sets. The only limits will come from your computer's ability to process large data files
