AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
This vulnerability affects IBM Integration Designer used in IBM Business Process Manager. 

 * IBM Integration Designer 8.5.7.0 
 * IBM Integration Designer 8.5.6.0 
 * IBM Integration Designer 8.5.5.0 
 * IBM Integration Designer 8.5.0.1
