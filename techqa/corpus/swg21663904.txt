Title: IBM BMXAA2278E - You do not have authorization for the application in Maximo URL - United States

Text:
BMXAA2278E; browser; IE; TPAEUI; Internet Explorer; MS; Microsoft; Authorization; URL; Maximo TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 If Maximo opened 1 browser and you try to open another session through IE,system is opening User start center and you can get error:BMXAA2278E-You do not have authorization for the app 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Firstly, understand the use of concurrent browser support by reading the following article: 

https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/blogs/a9ba1efe-b731-4317-9724-a181d6155e3a/entry/understanding_concurrent_browser_support_in_tpae1?lang=en [https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/community/blogs/a9ba1efe-b731-4317-9724-a181d6155e3a/entry/understanding_concurrent_browser_support_in_tpae1?lang=en]

Secondly, read up on this MS article as this often will resolve this issue, see here:

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/218153 [http://support.microsoft.com/kb/218153]
