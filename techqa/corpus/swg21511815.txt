Title: IBM Cloning an existing domain - United States

Text:
ITNM; TESTDOMAIN TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can I clone a domain that already exists? 

ANSWER
Do not copy any files. Since you are cloning, it makes copies of the files for you. Run:
ncp_perl /opt/IBM/tivoli/netcool/precision/scripts/perl/scripts/domain_create.pl -domain <NEW_DOMAIN> -clone <EXISTING_DOMAIN> -nopolls -password <NCIM_PASSWORD>
