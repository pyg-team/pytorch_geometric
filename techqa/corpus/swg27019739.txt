Title: IBM Open Mic Replay: Migrating to WebSphere Commerce V7.0 - United States

Text:
wste_webcast; WSTE; Support Technical Exchange; education; presentation; webcast; STEwebcastDocument; Migration; commerce migration WEBCAST

ABSTRACT
 This talk focuses on what IBM has done to improve the WebSphere Commerce migration process by reducing complexity, risk and the time it takes to perform a migration. Receive insight and guidance into preparing and planning for a migration in V7.0, and a chance to interact with experts on the topic.
