Title: IBM License features incorporated in "Tools and Utilities" key while generating Rational Rhapsody licenses - United States

Text:
Tools and Utilities; License Feature TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What are the features incorporated with the "Tools and Utilities" License key for IBM Rational Rhapsody? 

CAUSE
You would like to know all the features of the Tools and Utilities license key

ANSWER
The license features that come incorporated with IBM Rational Rhapsody Tools & Utilities Add on keys are as follows: 

 * ReporterPro 
 * Reporter 
 * RPGetConnected 
 * RPGetConnectedWind 
 * RPGetConnectedVxw 
 * RPGetConnectedSol 
 * RPGetConnectedN 
 * RPGetConnectedG 
 * RPUNCS 
 * RPTcSEAddon 
 * RPGraphPanel 
 * RPRG 
 * RPRG_DOORS 
 * RPRG_X2TXT 
 * RPRG_REQPRO 
 * RPRG_RP 
 * RPRG_SIMULINK 
 * RPRG_TAGGER 
 * RPRG_CALIBERRM 
 * RPUSimulink 
 * RPXMI 
 * RPTAUXMI


Note: Important functionalities of Rational Rhapsody such as Gateway [ RPRG], Import and Export of XMI files in Rhapsody [RPXMI], Panel Diagrams [RPGraphPanel] and Reporter Plus [ReporterPro] among others are covered
