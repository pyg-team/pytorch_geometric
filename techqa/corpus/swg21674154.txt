Title: IBM Error java.io.IOException on fix pack installation - United States

Text:
disk; capacity; maximum; cap; memory TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Installing of fixpack failed with error:

java.io.IOException: No space left on device

Output directory = /usr/ibm/common/acsi/logs/root 

CAUSE
Not enough space on /usr/ibm/common/acsi/logs/root directory.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Increase the amount of free space on /usr/ibm/common/acsi/logs/root and reinstall the fix pack.
