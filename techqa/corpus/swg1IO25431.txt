ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The insert button on a second scan on Navigator it's ignoring
   the position of the new image insertion, normally inserting the
   image at the middle of the batch
   This is weird behavior and was seen with customer application.
   They had Start Batch panel fields and for some reason, those
   fields were indirectly interfering with the position of scanned
   pages to insert.
