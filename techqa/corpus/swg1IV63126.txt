ERROR DESCRIPTION

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Situation Distribution Available list empty for ITCAM 7.1
   
   
   For situations involving ITCAM 7.1 attributes, the portal client
   Situation Distribution Available and Assigned lists show no
   Managed Systems.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The logic was changed so Managed Systems at the ITCAM 7.1 level
   will be recognized as eligible distribution targets for
   situations involving those agents' attributes.
   
   
   The fix for this APAR is contained in the following maintenance
   packages:
   
     | fix pack | 6.3.0-TIV-ITM-FP0004
