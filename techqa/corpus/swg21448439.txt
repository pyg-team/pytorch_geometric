Title: IBM Lotus Quickr Connectors 8.5 is a separate choice on IBM Fix Central - United States

Text:
 NEWS

ABSTRACT
 Beginning with the 8.5 versions, the Lotus Quickr choices on the IBM Fix Central site have been updated. 

CONTENT
Beginning with the 8.5 versions, the choices for Lotus Quickr on the IBM Fix Central [http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/] site have been updated. 

To download fixes for the Lotus Quickr Connectors 8.5, you can now select "Lotus Quickr Connectors" as the product value on IBM Fix Central. Previously, any Connectors fixes were included within the Lotus Quickr product choice. 

Also, you can find fixes for the 8.5 versions by selecting either "Lotus Quickr for Domino" or "Lotus Quickr for WebSphere Portal" as the product value, as appropriate. To find fixes for versions prior to 8.5, select "Lotus Quickr" as the product value, then use the Recommended option to navigate to your deployment type.
