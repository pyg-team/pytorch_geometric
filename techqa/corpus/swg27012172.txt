ABSTRACT
 This WebSphere Support Technical Exchange is designed to illustrate the configuration and Implementation of SSL with HTTP nodes in WebSphere Message Broker (WMB) V6.x. It covers a step-by step approach in configuring HTTPS in the message flow nodes, bar files, broker and HTTP Listener properties. The concepts in this webcast were demonstrated using WMB V6 but the concepts are applicable in WMB V7, V8 and IBM Integration Bus (IIB) V9.


Level of Difficulty: Intermediate

Presenter(s): Vivek Grover
