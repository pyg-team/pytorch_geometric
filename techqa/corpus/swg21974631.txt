Title: IBM Why does the notice name drop down is blank when generating Atlas Hold Notice Confirmation report? - United States

Text:
atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does the notice name drop down is blank when generating Atlas Hold Notice Confirmation report even there is a active notice for a matter ? 

CAUSE
Having incorrect location for ReportBaseFolder parameter will cause this issue

ANSWER
 

 1. Go to Admin --> Components --> Reporting. 
 2. Edit the Reporting component and correct the value for ReportBaseFolder. 
 3. Save the change and retry the report.
