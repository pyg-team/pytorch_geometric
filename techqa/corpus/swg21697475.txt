Title: IBM Import/Export Desktops from ICN 2.0.2 to 2.0.3 - United States

Text:
export; import TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Why am I unable to import a desktop exported from IBM Content Navigator V2.0.2 environment into IBM Content Navigator V2.0.3 environment? 

SYMPTOM
Error message stating the desktop was created in a different version of the product and can not be imported.


CAUSE
IBM Content Navigator desktop import is supported with in the same release of the product.
