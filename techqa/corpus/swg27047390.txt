ABSTRACT
 IBM® StoredIQ Platform provides scalable analysis and governance of unstructured data in-place across disparate and distributed email, file shares, desktops, and collaboration sites. Its products enable companies to discover, analyze, and act on data for eDiscovery; records retention and disposition; compliance; and storage optimization initiatives.

This document features the most requested documents as well as those identified as valuable in helping answer your questions related to IBM StoredIQ. The list will be updated periodically to reflect new trends and issues.
