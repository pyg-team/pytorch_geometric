Determine which method to use to install the images on your system.

 * Install the images.  1. Unpack the tar.gz files into a directory.
    2. Install from the unpack directory.
   
   
 * Burn the images to optical media and install.
   Follow the instructions to burn to optical media [//www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=nas8N1022089] because the procedure might be different from what you normally use.
