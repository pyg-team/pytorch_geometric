ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The Matter Master List screen does not return results due to
   poor performance of the underlying stored procedure used to
   gather the data to display on the screen. This issue can occur
   for legal matters that contain a large amount of custodian
   activity for Hold Notices, Interviews and Collections.
