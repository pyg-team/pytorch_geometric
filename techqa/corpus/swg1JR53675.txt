ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  After  migrating stand-alone environment from WPS 6.0x to BPM
   7.5.1 environment following exceptions are seen during servers
   startup
   
    SibMessage    E
   [CommonEventInfrastructure_Bus:mydevNode03.server1-CommonEventIn
   frastructure_Bus] CWSIS1544E: Multiple previous owner ids found
   in the messaging engines data store.
   
   SibMessage    I
   [CommonEventInfrastructure_Bus:mydevNode03.server1-CommonEventIn
   frastructure_Bus] CWSIS1593I: The messaging engine,
   ME_UUID=CA42D7BCF068E50F, INC_UUID=61FC61FCC605B7FC, has failed
   to gain an initial lock on the data store.
   
    Same error seen for  SCA.APPLICATION.mydevNode03Cell.Bus and
   SCA.APPLICATION.mydevNode03Cell.Bus
   
   
   The above is caused by duplicate SIBUS created during target
   profile creation and one during WAS post upgrade.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  You can delete one of the SIBUS created during Target profile
   creation  and thin complete the migration step
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  duplicate BUS member
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The issue only happened on WPS standalone and workaround is
   simply to remove the dulicate one.
