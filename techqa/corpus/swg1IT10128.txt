ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  High MQ traffic might result in an unexpected DataPower
   appliance restart when MQ sharing conversation is enabled.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Disable MQ sharing conversation.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customers who use MQ sharing conversation will meet
   box-restarted when the load is high.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Fix is available in 6.0.0.16, 6.0.1.12, 7.0.0.9, 7.1.0.6 and
   7.2.0.0.
