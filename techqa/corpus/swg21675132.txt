CAUSE

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Hourglass is sending Sterling Connect:Direct invalid return codes. Hourglass fix PM10335 states in the problem summary that this problem will be fixed in the next release of Hourglass. 

As a workaround, exempt Sterling Connect:Direct from Hourglass to prevent the issue from occurring.
