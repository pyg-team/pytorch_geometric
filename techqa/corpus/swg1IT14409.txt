ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Enabling sharing conversations in MQ may result in high CPU,
   device unresponsiveness and potentially an unplanned device
   restart.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Affected is use of MQ Sharing Conversation.
   Customers who use MQ Sharing Conversation get high CPU.
