ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The problem Decription: In Invoice application/Advanced Search,
   When trying to select Invoice Date (From / To) with today, Date
   Control do not respond.
   
   Env :  BS7505
   
   Replication steps.
   
   1. Go to the Invoice Application.
   2. Click 'Advanced Search'.The More Search Field Dialog opens.
   3. IN Invoice Date Field, Click Datelookup
      *** ex. Current Date is 05/08/2014
      Click 06/08/2014.
      Then, selected date(06/08/2014) will be displayed in Invoice
   Date
   field
      Click Datelookup again.
      Then, try to select today (05/08/2014).. But, not response :
   PROBLEM
   !!!
      It means that I cannot select today after selecting other
   date
   first.
      No problem when selecting today at the beginning.
      No problem in the Invoice Main Tab
