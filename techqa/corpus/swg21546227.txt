STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I back up and restore my Connect:Direct configuration? 

ANSWER
To backup user and netmap settings please do the following:

1. Open the CONNECT:Direct Configuration Utility
2. Click on file extract.
3. Save the map.cfg and user.cfg
4. When installing CONNECT:Direct again you will be asked if you wish to use these files. Say yes and browse to the correct path , follow the prompts to continue the installation.
