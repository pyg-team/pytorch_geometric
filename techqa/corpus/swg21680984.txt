Title: IBM Setting a reasonable LTPA time out value for TDWC. - United States

Text:
LTPA; timeout; security.xml TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Setting LTPA timeout value to 2147483647 in security.xml may cause FireFox and IE to prompt for authentication again after login. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
2147483647 is in minute and it is 4085 years. There is no reason to have this number this large.
Use reasonable time out value instead of unnecessary very large numbers.
