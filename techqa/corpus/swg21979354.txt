Title: IBM Usage of wildcards for Person CSV report in Atlas - United States

Text:
atlas; wildcard TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do you use wildcards in Person CSV report in Atlas ? 

CAUSE
The search results does not change with or without using wildcards in Person CSV report.

ANSWER
In Person CSV report with or without using wildcards you will get the same results for the search string. 

For example : If you are searching for "ABC" as Firstname and you are expecting 4 results . 
With (ABC%) or without (ABC using wildcards you will get same set of results.
