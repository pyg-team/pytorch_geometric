Title: IBM Uninstall DE (Deployment Engine) - United States

Text:
Uninstall Deployment Engine TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Is there a procedure to uninstall/remove DE on Unix and Windows? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
 

1) Uninstalling DE on Unix (Root install)
Source the DE environment: ". /var/ibm/common/acsi/setenv.sh" or "source /var/ibm/common/acsi/setenv.csh" depending on your shell.
Shutdown DE : acsisrv.sh -stop 
To uninstall DE, run following command : "/usr/ibm/common/acsi/bin/si_inst.sh -r -f"
Remove DE's database backup: "rm -rf /usr/ibm/common/acsi"

2) Uninstalling DE on Unix (Non-root install)

DE exists under ~/.acsi_$USER, so: "rm -rf ~/.acsi_$USER"
Also: "rm -rf ~/tivoli/cit"
Also: "rm -rf ~/.cit"

3) Uninstalling DE on Windows

Source the DE environment: "C:\Program Files\IBM\Common\acsi\setenv.cmd"
Stop the DE: From the Services window, Select IBM ADE Service from the Services window and then select Stop from the Action menu.
Remove DE: "C:\Program Files\IBM\Common\acsi\bin\si_inst.bat -r -f"
Remove DE's database backup: "rmdir /s C:\Program Files\IBM\Common\acsi"
(On 64 bit Windows, replace "Program Files" with "Program Files (x86)" in the above commands.)
