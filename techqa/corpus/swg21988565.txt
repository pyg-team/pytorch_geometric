Title: IBM EIF probe does not process events in order of arrival - United States

Text:
connectionmode cl co connection_less connection_oriented TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Events from LFA (Log File Agent) are not being processed in the order that was sent to the EIF probe. 

CAUSE
ConnectionMode of connection_less was used which does not guarantee event order.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Change the ConnectionMode to connection_oriented for EIF sender if event sequence processing by EIF probe is required.
