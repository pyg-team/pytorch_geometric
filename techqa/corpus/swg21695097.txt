Title: IBM Recent changes to Russian time zones might cause the Network IPS (GX) to incorrectly use Daylight Saving Time - United States

Text:
gx; tzdata; russian; daylight; savings; time TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 In the Network IPS (GX), how do you correct time zones that changed in Russia in 2014? 

CAUSE
The IBM Intrusion Prevention System firmware 4.6.2 includes daylight saving time for Russian time zones. When time zones are changed in the real-world, we must update the local tzdata package on the IPS in order to maintain a correct time and date calculation.

ANSWER
The tzdata package that includes this update is included in 4.6.2-ISS-ProvG-AllModels-System-FP0005 [http://www-933.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/downloadFixes?parent=Security%2BSystems&product=ibm/Tivoli/Proventia+Network+Intrusion+Prevention+System&release=4.6.2&platform=Windows&function=fixId&fixids=4.6.2.0-ISS-ProvG-AllModels-System-FP0005&includeRequisites=1&includeSupersedes=0&downloadMethod=http].
