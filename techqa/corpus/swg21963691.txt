Title: IBM Is JRE 1.8 supported with ITCAM for J2EE v7.1.1.0.2? - United States

Text:
CAMJ2INST JRE 1.8 J2EE v7.1.1.0 agent TECHNOTE (FAQ)

THIS DOCUMENT APPLIES ONLY TO THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE VERSION(S):
 US English 

QUESTION
 Is JRE 1.8 supported with ITCAM for J2EE v7.1.1.0.2? 

ANSWER
No sorry the JRE 1.8 is not supported with the new ITCAM for J2EE v7.1.1.0.2 at this time (2H2015), although this may change in the future after testing is complete. In the meantime, you have to use JRE 1.7 or lower with the DC/J2EE agent.
