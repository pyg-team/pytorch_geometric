LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  ****************************************************************
   * USERS AFFECTED:                                              *
   * All Impact Users                                             *
   ****************************************************************
   * PROBLEM DESCRIPTION:                                         *
   * If the Service status checkbox was checked in the Service    *
   * settings section of the Self Monitoring service,             *
   * service status updates were not being generated and          *
   * displayed in the Self Monitoring Log.                        *
   ****************************************************************
   * RECOMMENDATION:                                              *
   ****************************************************************
   Up until Impact 71 FP2,  service status updates were being
   generated by the addStateChangeSubscriber method in the
   Soap.....servicename....InterfaceSoap class for each service,
   for example, in CmdLineServiceInterface for CmdLineService.
   Following the move from SOAP to RESTUI in 71 FP2, this code was
   no longer being called and no service status updates were
   being generated.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The ServiceStatusUpdate class has been imported into the
   servicemanager class and
   notifyServiceStatus is called when a service is started or
   stopped.
   this replaces the old subscriber/publisher model.
   
   The fix for this APAR is contained in the following
   maintenance package:
   | Fix Pack | 7.1.0-TIV-NCI-FP0008
