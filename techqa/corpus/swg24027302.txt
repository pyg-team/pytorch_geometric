ABSTRACT
 This document describes how to download the installation images for the IBM® Tivoli® Storage Productivity Center Basic Edition V4.2 using the Passport Advantage® Online Web site. 

DOWNLOAD DESCRIPTION




This document describes how to download IBM Tivoli Storage Productivity Center Basic Edition V4.2 for AIX® installation images from the IBM Passport Advantage Online Web site. 

Tivoli Storage Productivity Center is a suite of storage infrastructure management software that can centralize, automate, and simplify complex and heterogeneous storage environments.

More information

To access a wide variety of technical resources for this product, see Support for Tivoli Storage Productivity Center [http://www.ibm.com/software/sysmgmt/products/support/IBMTotalStorageProductivityCenterBasicEdition.html].

Hardware requirements for Tivoli Storage Productivity Center servers:

For AIX® on IBM System i® and IBM System p®:

 * Machine: Minimum Dual POWER4™ or POWER5™ processor.
 * Minimum memory: 8 GB of RAM.
 * Disk space: Approximately 80 GB of hard disk space. For installation, you need you need 10 GB of available disk space, 500 MB in the /tmp directory, and 1.3 GB in the /opt directory.

Hardware requirements for Tivoli Storage Productivity Center Storage Resource agents:

For AIX on IBM System i and IBM System p:

 * Machine: Minimum 400 MHz processor or later.
 * Minimum memory and space requirements to run:  * 256 MB of RAM.
    * Approximately 100 MB of hard disk space. 
   
   

Downloading and assembling the product

 1.  Log on to Passport Advantage [http://www-01.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm]. 
 2.  Click Customer sign in. 
 3.  Enter your IBM ID and password, and click Sign in. 
 4.  Click Software download and media access. 
 5.  Click Download finder, and then click View all my downloads. 
 6.  Navigate to find the Tivoli Storage Productivity Center download parts. 
 7.  Select the Tivoli Storage Productivity Center parts that you require. The parts are described by platform in the tabbed sections of this document. 
 8.  Click I Agree followed by Download Now. 
 9.  When the download is complete, unpack Tivoli Storage Productivity Center Disk 1 images part 1, part 2, and part 3 to the same single temporary directory. Unpack all other parts into a separate temporary directory for each part. 
 10. Follow the instructions in the Quick Start Guide to install the product.
