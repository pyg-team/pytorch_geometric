APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  WSDL cache refreshes continue after disabling the domain.  This
   might cause WSDL fetch and compile errors to flood the logs.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  To minimize how often the refreshes occur, increase the WSDL
   cache refresh interval.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Affected is use of WSDL refresh.
   
   Refreshing WSDL's continues when objects are
   down.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Fix is available in 7.0.0.16, 7.1.0.13, 7.2.0.10, 7.5.0.4,
   7.5.1.3 and
   7.5.2.1
