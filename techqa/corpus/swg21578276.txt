Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I clean a failed resource from crm_mon ? 

ANSWER
You can use the crm_resource command to clear a failed resource. The syntax is as follows: 

 * 
 * #crm_resource -r <the-failed-resource> -C -H <hostname>


For example:
