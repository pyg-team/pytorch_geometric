Title: IBM Knowledge Collection: PureData System for Analytics Netezza Platform Software (NPS) Upgrades - United States

Text:
 RECOMMENDED RESOURCES

ABSTRACT
 A focused compilation of links to documents and resources to help you plan and perform your upgrade of IBM PureData System for Analytics Netezza Platform Software. 

CONTENT
The Netezza Platform Software(NPS) upgrade process is a robust, single command operation. IBM Support is pleased to announce that beginning July 1st 2016, every PureData System for Analytics customer, will now have the option to perform and manage NPS upgrades autonomously. This provides greater flexibility to our customers and improves customer's self sufficiency to perform NPS upgrades. 
Please note that IBM Support will continue to be available via PMR to perform any upgrade related activities including NPS only upgrades. This is purely an added value extended to our customers around NPS upgrades, providing an alternate option without wait time.

FDT, HPF and OS upgrades will continue to be performed by IBM support and the process for logging a ticket and booking a slot for those remains unchanged. 

You can refer to the below comprehensive step-by-step procedure for NPS upgrade alongside known issues, FAQs and the video guidance provided. If you experience issues you are unable to resolve with reference provided or using the IBM Support Portal [https://www-947.ibm.com/support/entry/myportal/product/information_management/puredata_system_for_analytics?productContext=-1708477406]or IBM Knowledge Center [http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSULQD/SSULQD_welcome.html] then please log a PMR with IBM support for further assistance.

Important Note: 

 * Perform Pre-Upgrade Checks 5-7 days before the actual upgrade. 
 * Fix any critical hardware issues prior to the upgrade. 
 * A NPS upgrade takes approximately 1 hour to complete if your catalog is 10GB or less. DO NOT INTERRUPT an upgrade in progress even is going over expected completion time. Let it run it's course. 
 * If NPS upgrade fails, please open a PMR with IBM PureData System for Analytics Support.
