[http://www.ibm.com/i/c.gif] INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
Important: 
Back up your systems and business-critical information before starting the installation process, especially if you are upgrading any software components.

1. Use the Planning and preparing for IBM FileNet P8 [http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/p8docs/v5r1m0/index.jsp?topic=/com.ibm.p8toc.doc/planning.htm] section in the IBM FileNet P8 information center to prepare for the FileNet P8 Platform and FileNet Content Manager installation.

2. Use the Installing or upgrading IBM FileNet P8 Platform [http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/p8docs/v5r1m0/index.jsp?topic=/com.ibm.p8toc.doc/installing.htm] section in the IBM FileNet P8 information center to install and configure the FileNet Content Manager and P8 Platform components, including Content Engine and Process Engine.

3. Use the Installing additional IBM FileNet P8 products [http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/p8docs/v5r1m0/index.jsp?topic=/com.ibm.p8.installingxt.doc/xt_insta.htm] section in the IBM FileNet P8 information center to install and configure IBM FileNet Workplace XT.

4. Use the IBM Production Imaging Edition Manuals [http://www-304.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27010422#xt] in the FileNet Workplace XT table for information about IBM Production Imaging Edition Viewer Pro. 

5. Use the IBM Datacap Taskmaster Capture installation guide [http://www-05.ibm.com/e-business/linkweb/publications/servlet/pbi.wss?CTY=US&FNC=SRX&PBL=GC19-3232-01] to prepare for installation and to install and configure
Datacap Taskmaster Capture.
