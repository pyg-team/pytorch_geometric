Title: IBM Does GENLINMIXED offer estimation using adaptive quadrature methods? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 I'm using the GENLINMIXED procedure in IBM SPSS Statistics 19, and would like to use adaptive quadrature methods to estimate parameters. Does GENLINMIXED have this capability? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
GENLINMIXED currently does not offer the option of using adaptive or other quadrature methods of estimation. An enhancement request has been submitted to SPSS Development
