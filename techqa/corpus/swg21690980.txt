Title: IBM DataPower Gateway Firmware Release 7.1 is now available - United States

Text:
 NEWS

ABSTRACT
 IBM DataPower Gateway Type 8436 appliance and firmware version 7.1 is now available. 

IBM DataPower Gateway is the new product name of a consolidated offering that uses an extensible and modular architecture to offer the functionality provided by the following three products in a single, consolidated product through software modules on both physical and virtual form factors.

WebSphere DataPower Service Gateway XG45
WebSphere DataPower Integration Appliance XI52
WebSphere DataPower B2B Appliance XB62
