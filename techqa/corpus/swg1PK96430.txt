   ****************************************************************
   * PROBLEM DESCRIPTION: A ConcurrentModificationException is    *
   *                      thrown from the MetaData Manager.       *

   * RECOMMENDATION:                                              *

   ConcurrentModificationException in
   MetaDataMgrImpl.addMetaDataToMap.
