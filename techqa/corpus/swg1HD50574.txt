   Incident Diagnosis
   The code has been rewritten to accept large data.
   ORACLE cannot support actually more than 1000
   items in a list ( select in (?,?,...,?,?) ) and more
   than 250 items in a complex list (select .. from ..
   where ((coid=? and compid=?) and ... and (coid=?
   and compid=?)))
   .
   The treatment is automatically switched to a "lot
   treatment".
