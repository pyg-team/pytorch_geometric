Title: IBM Out of Memory exceptions when running P8 Maintenance task - United States

Text:
icc; maintenance; p8 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 When running IBM Content Collector (ICC) P8 maintenance task to cleanup annotations , Why the maintenance task fails with Out of memory exception ?


CAUSE
This has been identified as a product defect under APAR HE12449.

ANSWER
Install or upgrade ICC version to 4.0.1.2-IBM-ICC-FP002 to resolve this issue.
