Title: IBM ReadMeFirst for IBM StoredIQ 7.6.0.3 - United States

 RELEASE NOTES

ABSTRACT
 IBM StoredIQ 7.6.0.3 is a fix-pack release that focuses on extending the capabilities of IBM StoredIQ through the addition of a new connector. There is also a new, automated workflow around change management and new user-interface components that provide deeper insight into and usability of the IBM StoredIQ solution. Read these notes prior to installing or using IBM StoredIQ 7.6.0.3.
