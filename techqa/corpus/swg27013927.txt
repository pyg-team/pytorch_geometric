ABSTRACT
 This WebSphere Support Technical Exchange is aimed at users of IBM Integration Bus (formerly WMB) who are coming into contact with the IBM WebSphere Transformation Extender node for the first time. It is a short but informative demonstration of how to activate WebSphere TX debugging and how to interpret the results.


Level of Difficulty: Intermediate

Presenter(s): Paul Brett

DATE: 19 NOVEMBER 2008
