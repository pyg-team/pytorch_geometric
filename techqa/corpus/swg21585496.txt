Title: IBM What is the equivalent in the CCDT for the DefRecon parameter in the Channels stanza in the mqclient.ini? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 WebSphere MQ 7.0.1 introduced the client configuration file or mqclient.ini and in the CHANNELS stanza there is an attribute to control default reconnection for client connections, for example:

CHANNELS:
DefRecon=YES
ServerConnectionParms=SVRCONN1/TCP/HOST1(1414)

You want to know what is the equivalent parameter in the Client Channel Definition Table (CCDT) for the DefRecon parameter in the Channels stanza in the mqclient.ini?


ANSWER
+ MQ 7.0.1: 
The DefRecon is not available in the DEFINE or ALTER statements for Client Connection Channels.

+ MQ 7.1 and later: 
The DEFRECON was made available in the DEFINE or ALTER statements for Client Connection Channels, and it is available in the MQ Explorer.
