PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
IBM DB2 Recovery Expert is a self-managing backup and recovery solution that offers the insurance you need to protect the mission-critical data that runs your business.

DB2 Recovery Expert recovers database objects safely, precisely and quickly without having to resort to full database recovery. It can help you avoid accidental data loss or corruption by providing a faster, less costly method of recovery when time is of the essence.

For more information,see Installation requirements and recommendations [http://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SS8QJD_5.1.0/contents/amacinstallationreq.html].
