ABSTRACT
 IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) and Tivoli Asset Discovery for Distributed (TAD4D) version 7.2.2 and 7.5 are past end-of-support. This presentation shows you the advantages of migrating to the latest release. The target audience is any customer who still is using ILMT or TAD4D v7.x. 

CONTENT
Les Popow and his Level 2 Support colleagues explained the pros of version 9 and the cons of staying on version 7. 




This Open Mic session was the second 2016 webcast provided by ILMT Technical Support.
