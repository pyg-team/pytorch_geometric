Title: IBM Mobile Connect is not vulnerable to the OpenSSL "Heartbleed" bug (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
CVE-2014-0160; Heartbleed; OpenSSL; Heartbleed Vulnerability; heart bleed; IMC TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
Is IBM Mobile Connect vulnerable to the OpenSSL "Heartbleed" bug (CVE-2014-0160)?

ANSWER
No, the Heartbleed vulnerability does not affect IBM Mobile Connect. IBM Mobile Connect uses the IBM Global Security Kit (GSKit) for SSL functions and features; it does not use OpenSSL. Therefore, there is no remediation or other action required to protect IBM Mobile Connect from this vulnerability. 

For more information on the Heartbleed bug, including a Q&A, go to http://www.heartbleed.com [http://www.heartbleed.com/].
