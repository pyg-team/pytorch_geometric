IBM Intelligent Operations Center version 1.6.0.3 interim fix PO05111 addresses multiple security vulnerabilities. This interim fix supports both the standard and high availability IBM Intelligent Operations Center environments. The following product updates are included in this interim fix:

 * IBM Cognos Business Intelligence 10.2.1 Fix Pack 8 
 * IBM DB2 10.1.0.5

 * IBM WebSphere 7.0 PI31622
