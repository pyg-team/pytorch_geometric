Title: IBM How to flush DNS cache on ClearCase servers - United States

Text:
flush; DNS; DNS cache; IP address; mount vob TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can you clear DNS cache on IBM Rational ClearCase servers? 

CAUSE

Stale DNS cache was suspected after IP address was changed on client server and it was moved to a different subnet. Client was unable to mount VOBs.


ANSWER
 

On Windows:
ipconfig /flushdns

On Solaris:
#ps -ef | grep nscd
#pkill <pid found in above command>

#ps -ef | grep nscd (to make sure it went away)
#/usr/sbin/nscd (restart it)

On linux: 
#/etc/init.d/nscd restart
