QUESTION
 Where can I find the end of support (EOS) for all versions of DB2? 

ANSWER
Official end of support (EOS) dates are listed in IBM product announcement letters. See the DB2 for z/OS software support lifecycle [http://www.ibm.com/software/data/db2/zos/support/plc/] page for announced EOS dates for DB2 for z/OS, and links to the announcement letters. 

For support dates for other IBM Software products, and a link to the IBM Software Support Lifecycle policy, see the IBM Software Support Lifecycle [http://www-01.ibm.com/software/support/lifecycle/index.html] overview page.
