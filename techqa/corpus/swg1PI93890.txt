APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  IBM Developer for z systems 14.1.1 can't download copybooks when
   Bidi setting is enable and the Property Group have a COBOL
   SYSLIB defined with more than 1 PDSE.
   
   Steps to recreate:
   1) Windows->Preferences->Bidirectional Development->Enable
   Bidirectional development capabilities
   2) Define a Property Group and a COBOL Syslib with 2 PDSE
   3) Associate the Property group to a Cobol member defined
   with copybooks
   4) Open the cobol member
   The copybooks are not downloading.
   From the bottom task bar , Downloading copybook for program:
   (0%) is shown and never progress.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  COBOL copybooks fail to download when BIDI options are enabled
   and the Property Group contains a COBOL SYSLIB with two or more
   PDSEs.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  BIDI option processing for copybooks has been updated in the
   client
