ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When using a Linux thin client with a Citrix XenApp client,
   time stamps in chats are 4 hours off.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  None. Sametime has limited support for Citrix.
   We only support pushing Windows sessions down to a
   Windows-based client; not Linux.
