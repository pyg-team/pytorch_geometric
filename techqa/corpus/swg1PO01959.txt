ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  In IBM Curam Income Support-Affordable Care Act (IS-ACA)
   Application script the question regarding
   Unemployment/Underemployment is incorrectly phrased.
   The question currently reads 'Are any of the members listed
   below unemployed or underemployed by working less than 100
   hours per week?'
   Instead it should read 'Are any of the members listed below
   unemployed or underemployed by working less than 100 hours per
   month?'
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  n/a
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Resolved in 6.0.5.2 iFix2
   PO01959, HCR-3334 - Unemployed/underemployed question is wrong
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The text from "Are any of the members listed below unemployed
   or underemployed by working less than 100 hours per week?" has
   been changed to "Are any of the members listed below unemployed
   or underemployed by working less than 100 hours per month? " in
   this UnemploymentUnderEmploymentPage.properties file for both
   HealthCarePortal and CaseWorker script.
