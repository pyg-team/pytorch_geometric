Title: IBM Useful TWS v8.4 through v9.3 WebSphere logs and their location - United States

Text:
bobcat; embedded; where; how; locate; find TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Many of the warning, informational, and error messages received in Tivoli Workload Scheduler (TWS) versions v8.4 through v9.x can be found in the WebSphere logs. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Listed below are the locations of the useful logs: 

TWS v8.4 

UNIX: 

<TWSHome>/appserver/profiles/twsprofile/logs/server1 

Windows: 

<TWSInstallPath>\appserver\profiles\twsprofile\logs\server1



TWS v8.5.x 

UNIX: 

<TWAHome>/eWAS/profiles/twaprofile/logs/<servername> 

Windows:

<TWAHome>\eWAS\profiles\twaprofile\logs\<servername> 

 

TWS v8.6 

UNIX: 

<TWAHome>/eWAS/profiles/TIPProfile/logs/server1 

Windows:

<TWAHome>\eWAS\profiles\TIPProfile\logs\server1 


TWS v9.x 

UNIX: 

<TWAHome>/WAS/TWSProfile/logs/server1 

Windows:

<TWAHome>\WAS\TWSProfile\logs\server1 

 

DWC v9.x 

UNIX:
<JazzSM_Home>/profile/logs/server1


Windows:

<JazzSM_Home>\profiles\logs\server1

In general the most useful file for troubleshooting issues is "SystemOut.log".
