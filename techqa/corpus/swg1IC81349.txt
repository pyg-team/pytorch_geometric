Title: IBM IC81349: NETWORK INSTABILITY THAT ABRUPTLY CLOSES OUT CONNECTIONS TO MQ MIGHT CAUSE AN APPLIANCE RESTART. - United States

Text:
 SUBSCRIBE
You can track all active APARs for this component.



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  TCP connections established between DataPower and MQ that are
   prematurely closed due to a network issue might cause an
   unexpected restart of the appliance.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Affected are customers who use an MQ QM object under stress test
   while the network is not stable.
   
   The appliance might restart after it establishes an MQ
   connection and encounters a network error.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The fix is available on 3.8.2.12, 4.0.1.10 and 4.0.2.6.
