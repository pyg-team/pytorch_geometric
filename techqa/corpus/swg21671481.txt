Title: IBM InfoSphere Optim Data Growth and Test Data Management Solutions for JD Edwards are not affected by the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 IBM® InfoSphere® Optim™ Data Growth and Test Data Management Solutions for JD Edwards in all editions and all platforms are NOT vulnerable to the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160). 

CONTENT
Optim Data Growth and Test Data Management Solutions for JD Edwards in all editions and all platforms are NOT vulnerable to the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160). While Optim Data Growth and Test Data Management Solutions for JD Edwards make use of the OpenSSL library for other functionality, they do not use OpenSSL for SSL/TLS. 

Remediation: No action required. 

Change History: 25 April 2014: original document published.
