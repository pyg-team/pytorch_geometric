Title: IBM Security Bulletin: IBM InfoSphere BigInsights products are not affected by the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160) - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 This alert contains a list of InfoSphere BigInsights products determined to NOT be vulnerable to the CVE-2014-0160 OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability. 

Vulnerability Details

The following list of products in all editions and all platforms are NOT vulnerable to the OpenSSL Heartbleed vulnerability (CVE-2014-0160), because they do not use OpenSSL. 

- InfoSphere BigInsights Enterprise Edition
- InfoSphere BigInsights Standard Edition
- Infosphere BigInsights Quick Start Edition
