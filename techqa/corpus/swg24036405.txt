Contents On a regular basis, IBM provides Fix Packs. You get these from Fix Central [https://www-933.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/options?selectionBean.selectedTab=find&selection=ibm%2fWebSphere%3bibm%2fWebSphere%2fWebSphere+MQ], and you apply them on top of your installed code.

 At IBM's discretion, a full refresh of the product at a given Fix Pack level is sometimes also made available. For each full refresh, there is a tabbed section within this document. Each tabbed section provides the following information:

 1. The part number and description of each eAssembly, followed by a list of the installation images available in the eAssembly.
 2. Instructions to download the relevant eAssembly images from the Passport Advantage website.
