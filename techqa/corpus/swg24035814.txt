It is recommended that you install the fix pack directly from the IBM update server; however, it is also available for download. You might want to download and install the refresh pack in the following situations: 


 * You have a slow or unstable Internet connection and want to use a download manager that can resume the download.

 * You have difficulty accessing the live IBM update servers from behind a firewall.
