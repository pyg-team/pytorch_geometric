Title: IBM Known limitation: You cannot add people to a matter request if there are more than 1000 people selected in the clipboard. - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 If you try to add people to a matter request when there are more than 1000 people in the clipboard, the following error will occur:
An unexpected error occurred.
Contact your system administrator.
ICWPA1001E 

CONTENT
To work around the limitation, when you add people from the clipboard, add fewer than 1000 people at one time.
