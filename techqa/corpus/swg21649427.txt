 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can you recover from a failed IBM Systems Director (ISD) version 6.3.2.1 upgrade under an IBM Smart Analytics System 7700 installation? 

CAUSE
During the upgrade of the ISD version 6.3 to version 6.3.2.1, under an IBM Smart Analytics System 7700 installation, a failure can occur that requires uninstalling ISD version 6.3 to retry the ISD 6.3.2.1 upgrade. 

ANSWER
The following instructions provide steps and links to ISD documentation that allows the uninstall of ISD version 6.3; reinstall of ISD version 6.3; and application of the ISD version 6.3.2.1 upgrade.
