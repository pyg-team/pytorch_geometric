Title: IBM Problem with MySQL datasource after migration to Netcool Impact 7.1 - United States

Text:
Netcool; impact; MySQL TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

THIS DOCUMENT APPLIES ONLY TO THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE VERSION(S):
 English 

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Error on the impactserver-errors.log: JDBC Connection Pool could not connect to datasource as: username. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The username needs to have the permissions to read, write and update to the MySQL db.
