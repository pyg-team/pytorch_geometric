Title: IBM Detailed hardware and software requirements for WebSphere Business Monitor V7.0 on AIX - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 This document contains system requirements for WebSphere Business Monitor V7.0 on IBM AIX. 

CONTENT
This document describes the minimum product levels that need to be installed before opening a problem report with the WebSphere Business Monitor Support team. Because other products frequently ship fixes, updates, and new releases, testing every configuration is not possible. In general and unless otherwise stated, you can install and run with updates to supported products if those updates are forward compatible.
