   Chinese encoding problems. We have modified the
   setting in the file "SmarTeamConnector.cfg" under
   the directory "C:\IBM\WebSphereServer"
   (snapshot "Tunbow_SmarTeam_For_WBISE_Final").
   In the setting, we have changed:
   1) From "ascii7" to "Big5"
   2) From "en_US" to "zh_CN"
   You can found the modified
   file "SmarTeamConnector.cfg"
