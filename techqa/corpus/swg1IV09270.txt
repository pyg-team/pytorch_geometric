Title: IBM IV09270: BMXAA6772W - GROUP MEMBERS COULD NOT BE RETRIEVED FROM THE LDAP CONTEXT - United States

APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When synchronizing with LDAPSYNC users within a OU that contain
   a "/" cannot be synchronized and receive the follow error.
   
   
   [ERROR] BMXAA6752E - Group
   synchronization could not be performed because of error
   BMXAA6772W - Group members could not be retrieved from the LDAP
   context.. The error count was 5.
   psdi.security.ldap.LdapSyncException: BMXAA6772W - Group members
   could not be retrieved from the LDAP context.
   
   It appears the slash is not being escaped
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Rename OU to not contain any slashes
