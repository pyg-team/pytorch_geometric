ABSTRACT
 You should review the Preventive Service Planning (PSP) bucket information for a product when planning for an upgrade or applying maintenance. Often, you might not know the UPGRADE or SUBSET name of the PSPBUCKET. This document gives the links directly to the PSP buckets for supported releases of z/OS Connect Enterprise Edition (z/OS Connect EE). You can also find any PSP bucket on the Web for any product. 

CONTENT
Select from one of the following products to be taken to the corresponding table containing links to the PSP buckets:


 * z/OS Connect Enterprise Edition V3.0
 * z/OS Connect Enterprise Edition V2.0


Do the following to obtain the PSP Buckets for any IBM product: 

 1. Go to [https://techsupport.services.ibm.com/server/390.psp390] [http://www.ibm.com/software/integration/wmq/support/] [http://www-306.ibm.com/software/htp/cics/support/]http://www14.software.ibm.com/webapp/set2/psp/srchBroker [http://www14.software.ibm.com/webapp/set2/psp/srchBroker].
    
    
 2. Enter the name of the product in the "Search for" box. You can also enter an Upgrade Name, Subset Name, FMID, Component ID, Program ID, or CLC under "Other search criteria".
    
    
 3. Click on Search to the right of the "Search for" box.
