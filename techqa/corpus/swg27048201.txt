ABSTRACT
 Arthur Testa from the Support team and a panel of other subject matter experts explained how to use IBM X-Force Exchange and IBM Security Network Protection and the XGS appliance to protect your network. 

CONTENT
The presentation explained how to use the information in IBM X-Force Exchange to create a Network Access Policy based on an Intrusion Prevention Policy to protect your network.

 * Date: Thursday, 30 June 2016
