Title: IBM OP-00000 error occurs when finalizing a Reporting Period - United States

Text:
OP-00000; Finalize; reporting Period; Timeout TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 An "OP-00000: The requested operation cannot be completed" error occurs when Finalizing a Reporting Period. How can this be resolved? 

CAUSE
The System.out contained this error : 

-------------------

------------------------------------------

ANSWER
 

These steps should resolve the issue: 

Restart the OP services to see if the Finalized Period appears in the UI. 

Delete the finalized period and increase the timeout setting referenced in this TN:
http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21628887 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21628887] 

Increase the JTA timeout using this TN:
http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21591878 [https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=http-3A__http-3A__www-2D01.ibm.com_support_docview.wss-3Fuid-3Dswg21591878&d=CwMCaQ&c=Ezx-H5PgBrUMEEvxgJuTQRYrd-4D8l8I18qkZZzJQbo&r=o4RjlMSsQTrzZP6x5D_nS2G0w8U0rZdc5aET0xvkMYs&m=guzskkZ-lvXG27e1hCnMWGeNXbn0wmf_r5iETq3VDuM&s=qlwN9fqwM49hJhUDe0Kl4hstOs_ZOk8Tqplo1RobJ7U&e=] 

Creating a Finalize Reporting Period should succeed without errors afterwards.
