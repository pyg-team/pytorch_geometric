Title: IBM What is the meaning of the 'total activity' in the MC monitor? - United States

Text:
activity; monitor TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What is the meaning of the 'total activity' in the MC monitor? 

ANSWER
The Total Activity in the MC monitor for source and target is calculated as follows: 

The Source activity is the sum of the following three stats: 
- SOURCE_DATABASE_BYTES (log reader) 
- SOURCE_ENGINE_BYTES 
- SOURCE_COMM_BYTES 

The Target activity is the sum of the following stats: 
- TARGET_COMM_BYTES 
- TARGET_ENGINE_BYTES 
- TARGET_DATABASE_BYTES
