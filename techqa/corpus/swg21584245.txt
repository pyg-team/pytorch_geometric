Title: IBM TargetInvocationException error when copying charts - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When a chart from an Excel object is copied to the clipboard, FSR crashes when clicking a Word object with a "TargetInvocationException" error. 

SYMPTOM

CAUSE
This is a product issue.



ENVIRONMENT
FSR 6.6.0.454 up to 6.6.1.147



DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
Open an Excel object that contains a chart. Click the said chart and copy it (either right click and select "Copy", either by pressing Ctrl + C). Click a Word object. 



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The issue was confirmed by development and it will be fixed in a future release (the issue is present in FSR 6.6.1.147).
