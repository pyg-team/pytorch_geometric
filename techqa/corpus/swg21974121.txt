Title: IBM Session time out when trying to drag iWidget in Cognos Workspace Advanced - United States

Text:
Timeout; Session timeout; IE TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When dragging the iWidget in the Workspace Advanced, it popup error "Session Time Out"

This happens only in IE11 and works for Firefox. 

SYMPTOM
Session Time Out Error Message


CAUSE
This is caused if the Browser has settings to not allow the cookies.

ENVIRONMENT
Cognos 10.2.2

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
- In IE explorer click on Settings followed by Internet Options.
- Click on the privacy tab and Click Advanced.
- Click Override automatic cookie handling.
- Check the Always allow cookie session cookies option.
- Click OK and reopen the Cognos Workspace Advanced session in the browser and it works.
