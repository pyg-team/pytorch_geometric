Title: IBM How do I turn off  global hold reminder only for a specific hold notice ? - United States

Text:
atlas ; GHR TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I turn off global hold reminder in Atlas only for a specific hold notice ? 

CAUSE
Legal wants to make last minute changes to a hold notice and would like to turn off global hold reminder just for this hold notice.

ANSWER
You cannot turn off global hold reminder just for single hold notice. Any change in global hold reminder configuration is global and it will effect all hold notices.
