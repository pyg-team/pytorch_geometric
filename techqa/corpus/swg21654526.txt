Major Releases, Maintenance and Fixpack follow these Guidelines:  * Major Release levels are supported for 3 years 
 * Minor Release levels are supported for 2.5 years 
 * Maintenance releases are supported for a minimum of 18 months 
 * All fixpacks are cumulative of prior fixpack content 
 * Client Kits and Drivers are maintained for a given platform for as long as the operating system vendor provides standard support.


Major Releases, Maintenance and Fixpack conventions: 

 

Release Type Example Names Purpose Major releases 6.0, 7.0 Releases that contain large areas of new functionality and capability. Minor releases 7.1.0 Releases that contain small enhancements and fixes. Maintenance (Dot) releases 7.2.1 Includes operational and minor enhancements as well as bug fixes. Fixpacks 7.2.1.3 Released twice a year and contain approximately 25 - 50 fixes. Patches 7.2.1.3-P2 Released every two months, except when fixpacks are being released. Fixes will be rolled into the next quarterly fixpack.
