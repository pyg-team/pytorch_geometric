LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  The DataLoad utility imports business variables that are
   available for search into the LSW_BPD_INSTANCE_VARS_PIVOT table.
   Each string variable is truncated to 128 characters. If the
   variable content is longer, the value is truncated to the
   maximum column length. This issue happens with truncated strings
   containing accented characters because accented characters
   require more than one byte.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  A fix will be included in  IBM BPM V8.5.7 cumulative fix 2016.06
   that considers accented characters when calculating where to
   truncate the string.
   
   To determine whether the cumulative fix is available and
   download it if it is, complete the following steps on Fix
   Central:
   
   1. On the Select product tab, select WebSphere as the product
     group, IBM Business Process Manager with your edition from the
     WebSphere options, All as the installed version, and All as
     the platform, and then click Continue.
   
   2. In the Text field, enter "cumulative fix?, and click
     Continue.
