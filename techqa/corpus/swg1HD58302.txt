ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  COMPUTE ENDS WITH INTERNAL ERROR
   .
   Scenario:
   1. Load "Ausleger_R16.CATAnalysis"
   2. Delete Restrints
   3. Apply a "Clamp" to the point in the middle of the
   lower hole
   4. Apply a "Rigid Virtual Part" to the lower hole but
   forget to select the middle point as "Handler"
   5. Compute
   6. A warning message appears, select "OK"
   7. Exit the Computation window by selecting "no"
   8. Edit "Rigid Virtual Part" by selecting the middle
   point as "Handler"
   9. Compute again
   10. Error message appears which persists even
   after renewing all FEM Definitions
   .
