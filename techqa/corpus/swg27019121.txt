ABSTRACT
 The list below identifies the supported releases of IBM® WebSphere® eXtreme Scale from which you can select specific detailed system requirements. 

CONTENT








These pages describe the minimum product levels you should have installed before opening a problem report with the WebSphere eXtreme Scale support team. Because other products frequently ship fixes, updates, and new releases, we cannot test every possible configuration. In general, you can install and run with updates to supported products if those updates are forward-compatible. 

Note that your entitlement to support, if any, is dependent upon your license or maintenance agreements for WebSphere eXtreme Scale, and is limited to your use of the relevant prerequisite with a supported WebSphere eXtreme Scale product.
