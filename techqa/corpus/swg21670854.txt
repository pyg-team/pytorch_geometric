Title: IBM Security Bulletin:  Vulnerabilities in IBM Tivoli Monitoring agents with potential for privilege escalation (CVE-2014-0907) - United States

Text:
privilege CVE-2014-0907 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 Privilege escalation vulnerability in IBM Tivoli Monitoring (ITM) agents could be exploited by a local user to gain elevated privilege of another user. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID: CVE-2014-0907 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2014-0907]
CVSS Base Score: 6.9
CVSS Temporal Score: See https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/91869 [https://exchange.xforce.ibmcloud.com/vulnerabilities/91869] for the current score
CVSS Environmental Score*: Undefined
CVSS Vector: (AV:L/AC:M/Au:N/C:C/I:C/A:C)
