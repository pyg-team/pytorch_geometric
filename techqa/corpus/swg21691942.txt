Title: IBM AIX on Power8 (P8) support now available for IBM Business Monitor, IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) Advanced, IBM Business Process Manager Standard, and IBM Business Process Manager Advanced - Process Server - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is AIX on Power8 (P8) supported for IBM Business Monitor and IBM Business Process Manager products? 

ANSWER
AIX on Power8 (P8) support is now available for IBM Business Monitor, IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) Advanced, IBM Business Process Manager Standard, and IBM Business Process Manager Advanced - Process Server.
