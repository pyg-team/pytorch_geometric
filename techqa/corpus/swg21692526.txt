ABSTRACT
 Fix packs 8.0.1.6 and 8.0.0.13 for the IBM Rational ClearCase 8.0.1 and 8.0 releases are available.
Fix pack 7.1.2.16 for IBM Rational ClearCase 7.1.2 has also been released.
