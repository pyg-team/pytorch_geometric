Title: IBM Themes and Skins portlet is unavailable - United States

Text:
context; context root; modify-servlet-path-portlets; modify-servlet-path; admin portlet; unavailable; disable TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM
 In the IBM WebSphere® Portal Administration UI, the Themes and Skins portlet is shown as "This portlet is unavailable". What is the cause of this issue and how to resolve it?

SYMPTOM
 In the IBM WebSphere® Portal Administration UI, under "Portal User Interface", when the Portal administrator clicks on "Themes and Skins", the portlet is not loading. Instead, in the portlet menu bar the portlet title is displayed as "[Not available]", and the portlet window displays "This portlet is unavailable".

CAUSE
 In this case, the context root was changed from /wps to /internal. However, not all required steps as documented in the Portal documentation were performed, in particular the ConfigEngine task "modify-servlet-path-portlets" was not executed.


RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
 Run the ConfigEngine task "modify-servlet-path-portlets" and restart Portal to resolve the issue.
