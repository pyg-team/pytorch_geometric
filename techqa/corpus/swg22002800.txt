WHAT WILL REPLACE INFOSPHERE FEDERATION SERVER AFTER SEPTEMBER 30, 2017?
Although InfoSphere Federation Server will be withdrawn from service, as per the Software withdrawal and service discontinuance announcement [https://www.ibm.com/common/ssi/cgi-bin/ssialias?subtype=ca&infotype=an&appname=iSource&supplier=897&letternum=ENUS916-117#rprodnx], it has been replaced by either IBM DB2 Advanced Enterprise Server Edition or IBM DB2 Developer Edition 

WILL INFOSPHERE FEDERATION SERVER V10.5 CONTINUE TO BE SUPPORTED AFTER SEPT 30, 2017?
 Yes, InfoSphere Federation Server v10.5 will continue to be supported as it is included within IBM DB2 Advanced Enterprise Server Edition v10.5 or IBM DB2 Developer Edition 10.5. 

HOW LONG WILL INFOSPHERE FEDERATION SERVER V10.5 CONTINUE TO BE SUPPORTED ONCE I MOVE TO IBM DB2 ADVANCED ENTERPRISE SERVER EDITION V10.5 OR IBM DB2 DEVELOPER EDITION V10.5?
InfoSphere Federation Server v10.5 will continue to be supported for as long as IBM DB2 Advanced Enterprise Server Edition v10.5 or IBM DB2 Developer Edition v10.5 is in support. See DB2 Distributed end of support (EOS) dates [https://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21168270] for more details 

WHAT ARE MY SUPPORT OPTIONS FOR INFOSPHERE FEDERATION SERVER V9.7 OR V10.1?
 InfoSphere Federated Server v9.7 and v10.1 has been replaced by DB2 Advanced Enterprise Server Edition or DB2 Developer Edition v9.7 and v10.1 which will be going out of support on September 30, 2017. You have two choices for getting Support after September 30, 2017. 

 * Continuing Support (no defect fixes) - see DB2 LUW Continuing Support for DB2 9.7 and DB2 10.1 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg22002945] for a complete description of this option. For "full defect" support you must purchase Extended Support. 
 * Extended Support (including defect fixes) - Support extensions may be purchased for an additional fee. To extend your support of your DB2 V9.7 or V10.1 product until September 30, 2020, please contact your local IBM Sales Representative. For direct inquiries, US Customers may call IBM Sales at 1-888-426-4343, option 1 and ask for a Service Extension on DB2 LUW Version 9.7 or 10.1. Customers in other geographies should refer to the IBM directory of worldwide support contacts to find sales contact numbers in your region. Note that when you contact support they may inquire if you are on the latest fix pack for DB2 V9.7 or V10.1. 

 IBM directory of worldwide support contacts [http://www.ibm.com/planetwide/] 

DO I HAVE TO MAKE ANY CHANGES TO MY INSTALLATION IN ORDER TO BE SUPPORTED?
You do not need to make any changes to your existing environment in order to receive support and/or remain compliant with IBM licensing.
