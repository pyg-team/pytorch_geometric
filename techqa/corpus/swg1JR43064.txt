ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When a user logs out of Business Space, after having accessed
   a Cognos related widget (e. g. Cognos Report Viewer) during the
   session, a Cognos authentication dialog may appear, forcing the
   user to log back in to Cognos before the logout can complete.
