This document provides the eAssembly part numbers required for downloading WebSphere Lombardi Edition V7.2 for AIX from the Passport Advantage Web site [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm].

Downloading and assembling the product 

 1. Locate the WebSphere Lombardi Edition eAssembly part numbers for your platform. The eAssembly part numbers are listed by platform in the tabbed sections of this document.
    
    
 2. Navigate to the Passport Advantage Web site [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm] and download the parts. (For information about how to access Passport Advantage, see the Passport Advantage Quick Reference Guide in the Learn more section on the Passport Advantage Web site [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm].) 
    
    
 3. Unpack the parts onto your system. 
    
    
 4. Follow the installation instructions listed below.
