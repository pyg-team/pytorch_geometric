Title: IBM FSR error OleDbException - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When trying to login to Cognos Financial Statement Reporting you receive the following error:

Type: OleDbException
Message: The transaction log for database 'tempdb' is full. To find out why space in the log cannot be reused, see the log_reuse_wait_desc column in sys.databases

This error is caused by the fact that there is no available space on the drive where the tempdb resides and it can be fixed by freeing up some space.

ENVIRONMENT

DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM

The issue can be solved by increasing the size of the drive on which the database resides or by freeing up unnecessary used space.
