WHAT ARE THE END OF SUPPORT DATES FOR DB2 9.7 ?
End of support for DB2 V9.7 products is September 30, 2017. Extended support may be purchased to extend your support until September 30, 2020. 

"End of support" indicates the last date on which you are entitled to base support for your DB2 LUW product. You can extend the support period beyond the end of support date for an additional fee. 

"Extended support" indicates the final date in which IBM shall end all support for the DB2 LUW products.

HOW DO I PURCHASE SUPPORT AFTER END OF SUPPORT?
 You have two choices for getting Support after End of Support for DB2 9.7: 

 * Continuing Support (no defect fixes) - see DB2 LUW Continuing Support for DB2 9.7 and DB2 10.1 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg22002945] for a complete description of this option. For "full defect" support you must purchase Extended Support. 
 * Extended Support (including defect fixes) - Support extensions may be purchased for an additional fee. To extend your support of your DB2 V9.7 product until September 30, 2020, please contact your local IBM Sales Representative. For direct inquiries, US Customers may call IBM Sales at 1-888-426-4343, option 1 and ask for a Service Extension on DB2 LUW Version 9.7. Customers in other geographies should refer to the IBM directory of worldwide support contacts to find sales contact numbers in your region. Note that when you contact support they may inquire if you are on the latest fix pack for DB2 V9.7. 

 IBM directory of worldwide support contacts [http://www.ibm.com/planetwide/]

DB2 Connect Personal Edition 


 

WHEN THE SERVER PRODUCT GOES OUT OF SUPPORT, DOES THE CLIENT ALSO GO OUT OF SUPPORT?
 The DB2 V9.7 clients go out of support at the same time that the server does. 

HOW DO I KNOW IF MY DB2 UNIVERSAL JDBC DRIVER IS SUPPORTED?
 Effective September 30, 2017, only those drivers shipped with DB2 10.5 and beyond (currently DB2 11.1) will be supported. Use technote 1363866 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21363866] to check the version of the DB2 JCC driver installed with your application. 

WHAT IF I HAVE A SPECIAL BUILD OR AN OLDER FIX PACK ON DB2 9.7? AM I STILL SUPPORTED?
 All support for DB2 9.7 ends on September 30, 2017 unless: 

 * You continue paying Support & Subscription. This entitles you to non-defect Support (Continuing Support). See DB2 LUW Continuing Support for DB2 9.7 and DB2 10.1 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg22002945] for a complete description of this option. 
 * You purchase a support extension#EOS3. This entitles you to Support including defect fixes (Extended Support) 

The policy statement for DB2 for Linux, UNIX and Windows fix packs, special builds, Total Content Ownership agreements and IBM Software Premium Services is located in technote 180416 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21180416]. I AM USING DB2 V9.7 AS PART OF A BUNDLE, AM I STILL SUPPORTED?
 Support terms and conditions may differ for the use of DB2 with certain applications. Customers should check directly with their application vendor for the terms and duration of their support for DB2. Read technote 1051638 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21051638] for more information as it relates to SAP 

IS THE DOCUMENTATION FOR VERSION 9.7 STILL AVAILABLE?
 The documentation for DB2 Version 9.7 is still available. However, it will no longer be updated. For information about DB2 Version 9.7, see the DB2 database product documentation [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27023554] page. 

HOW DO I OBTAIN DB2 9.7 IF I STILL NEED IT?
 Q: I need to download and install the base product of DB2 9.7 for a migration test environment. How can I obtain it? 
A: The DB2 9.7 server editions are available for download on Passport Advantage if you previously purchased them. 

Q: What is the final fix pack for DB2 9.7 and how do I get it?
A: The final fix pack is DB2 Version 9.7 Fix Pack 11. All DB2 LUW product fix packs are available for download at this link [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?rs=71&uid=swg27007053].
