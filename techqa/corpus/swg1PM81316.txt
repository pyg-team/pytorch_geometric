APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When two libraries are being syndicated, moving an item from the
   1st library into the 2nd library causes the item to go missing
   completely on the subscriber after the update completes.
   
   1. Create a 2 empty libraries.
   2. Create an All Items syndication pair to syndicate both
   libraries.
   3. On the syndicator, create a top level site area item in the
   1st library.
   4. Wait until the syndication completes and verify item exists
   on the subscriber.
   5. On the syndicator, move the item into the root of the 2nd
   library.
   6. Wait until the syndication update completes.
   
   On the subscriber server, the item is not there in either
   libraries!
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Entries for preset folders are not created in eventlog table
   when creating libary
