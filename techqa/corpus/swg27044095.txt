ABSTRACT
 On 13 Nov, 2014, Elaine Richard discussed how to diagnose hung Information Server DataStage parallel jobs. 

CONTENT
This presentation covers known issues, it shows how to determine if a DataStage parallel job is hung and if so, it describes the environment variables that need to be set and what information to collect during the job hang. Learn what to look for when you think a DataStage parallel job is hung, what resources are available to you to search for known issues and what information to collect and provide to IBM support. 



Level of Difficulty: Intermediate 
Presenter: Elaine Richard
