Title: IBM Failed to open Pool to Centera device error when trying the Test Connection in FileNet Enterprise Manager - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 When attempting to configure a Centera fixed content device in FileNet Content Engine Enterprise Manager (FEM) a Failed to open Pool to Centera device error is encountered. 

CAUSE
The default port 3218 was only open for TCP.
