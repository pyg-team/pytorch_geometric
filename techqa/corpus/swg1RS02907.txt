PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  When opening Manage Subbranches and Baselines for a project in
   Decision Center we got sometimes unexpected error displayed on
   the UI, The exception IlrObjectNotFoundException occurs when
   trying to retrieve the ruleset from the deployment baseline.
   To avoid that we need to check if the ruleset is not deleted.
