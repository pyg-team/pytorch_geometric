Title: IBM "Add components to Content Collector cluster" option  when installing  IBM Content Collector 4 fix pack 3 on a secondary node - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can "Add components to Content Collector cluster" option be selected when installing IBM Content Collector 4 fix pack 3 on a secondary node?

. 

ANSWER
When installing IBM Content Collector 4 fix pack 3. The "Add components to Content Collector cluster" option can be selected for both nodes (primary and secondary) at the "Installation Mode" section of the installation wizard.
