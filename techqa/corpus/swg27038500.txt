ABSTRACT
 IBM Tivoli Composite Application Manager (ITCAM) for SOA, the ITCAM Agent for DataPower, and the WebSphere Appliance Management Center monitor and manage DataPower appliances. We will review the new product versions released in 2012, a few sample problems, and approaches to troubleshooting.
