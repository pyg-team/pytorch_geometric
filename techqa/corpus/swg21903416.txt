PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Q: Are there any reported issues against Leap Second events? 

A: No issues have been detected or reported with Cognos BI in relation to the Leap Second event

Q: Can Cognos products present the inserted leap time period correctly, if the database supports it?

A: Both @23:59:60 and @0:0:0 will be formatted to the same date/time ... Our products will convert the two different timestamps into one formatted value.

No action required - Leap Second will have no impact on Cognos BI
