ABSTRACT
 Members of the IBM License Metric Tool technical support team shared information on how to identify and perform troubleshooting on hardware files and scans. 

CONTENT
For this live session, the support team explained analysis of the Hardware Inventory report and hardware scan files in detail. The goal is for a user to be able to locate and distinguish the difference between computer ID and remote ID, and be able to locate the hardware files and use them. 




After the presentation, the panelists opened the call to take questions from the audience. It was an educational, lively session.
