Title: IBM Transport Layer Security encryption compatibility in IBM i2 iBase - United States

Text:
ibase;ssl;tls QUESTION/ANSWER

QUESTION
What versions of Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption are supported for use with an iBase SQL Server database?

ANSWER
As of version 8.9.12, TLS 1.0, 1.1 and 1.2 are supported in iBase.
