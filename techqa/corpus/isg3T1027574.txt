WHAT'S OUR SCHEDULE FOR DISCONTINUING TESTING OF PRODUCTS AND RELEASES?
 * Currently, we're planning to discontinue testing of the following:  * CICS TS 5.1 - discontinued testing at the end of June 2016
    * CICS/TS 5.2 - discontinued testing at the end of September 2017
    * DB2 V10 - discontinued testing at the end of July 2017
    * IMS V12 - discontinued testing at the end of December, 2015
    * IMS V13 - discontinuing testing at the end of December 2017
    * JAVA V7 - discontinued testing at the end of August 2017
    * WebSphere for MQ V7 - discontinued testing at the end of August 2017
    * WebSphere Application Server V8 - discontinuing service at the end of February, 2018
    * z/OS V1R13 - discontinued testing at the end of September, 2016
   
   

WHAT'S OUR SCHEDULE FOR TESTING NEW PRODUCTS AND RELEASES?
We'd like to include every single available possible product in our testing, but we have to maintain a stable environment, and can only bring in new products or releases at the start of a quarter so that they get a full test.

 * Currently, we're investigating and planning to install any new releases as they become available for products that participate in CST:  *
