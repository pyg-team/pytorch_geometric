LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  This problem is specific to z/OS platform.When an unsupported
   encoding is used, its expected to fall back to the default
   encoding of the platform.When "export LC_ALL=Ar_AA.IBM-425" is
   set, both file.encoding property and ibm.system.encoding
   property get set as "IBM-425".Fallback mechanism implementation
   sets only the file.encoding to Cp1047.Above fallback mechanism
   does not affect to com.ibm.jvm.io.ConsoleInputStream class.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  In the fix, we are trying to over come the problem bypassing the
   java.io.UnsupportedEncodingException exception.
