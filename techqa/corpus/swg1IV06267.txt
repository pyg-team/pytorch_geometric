ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The new eWAS distribution on ITM 6.2.3 are incompatible with
   the SDMS components on SOA 7.1.1
   
   SDMS configuration is referencing eWAS 6 specific jars that have
   been renamed in eWAS 7 (which is shipped with 623).
   
   The jars in question are:
   
   org.eclipse.emf.common_2.2.1.v200609210005.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.commonj.sdo_2.1.0.v200609210005.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore_2.2.1.v200609210005.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore.sdo_2.2.0.v200609210005.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore.ami_2.2.1.v200609210005.jar
   
   In eWAS 7, these jars are now named:
   
   org.eclipse.emf.common.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.commonj.sdo.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore.sdo.jar
   org.eclipse.emf.ecore.ami.jar
