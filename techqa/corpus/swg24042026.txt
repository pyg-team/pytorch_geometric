DOWNLOAD PACKAGE
To download the interim fix: 

 *  Access the Fix Central web site: http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/ [http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/] Sign in with your IBM ID and password. If you are not yet registered or you forgot your ID, follow the instructions on the sign-in page. For Product Group, select Enterprise Content Management. For Select from Enterprise Content Management, select Atlas eDiscovery. For Select from Atlas eDiscovery, accept the default, Atlas eDiscovery Process Management. For Installed Version, select 6.0.3.3. For Platform, select All, then click Continue. Accept the default option, Browse for fixes, and click Continue. Select the fix pack option and click Continue. Specify your preferred download options, then click Continue. View the terms and conditions, then click I agree. Review your selection and click the download link next to the file name.
