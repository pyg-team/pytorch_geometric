Title: IBM Custodians added as other person on a Atlas Hold Notice receive Confirmation Reminders after Global Hold Reminder is sent - United States

Text:
Confirmation Reminder Atlas Global Hold Reminder Other Person TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why do custodians added as other person on a Atlas Hold Notice receive Confirmation Reminders after Global Hold Reminder is sent 

CAUSE
Confirmation Reminders are being sent to custodians after the Global Hold Reminder is sent

ANSWER
The Custodians who are added as other person will only receive Confirmation Reminders until they respond and will not receive Global Hold Reminder
