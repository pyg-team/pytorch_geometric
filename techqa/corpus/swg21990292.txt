Title: IBM LDAP users unable to logon - United States

Text:
SIQLegal; authentication TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 After I have configured the StoredIQ for Legal environment to point to the company's LDAP and imported users in. Why some of the users are not able to logon ? 

CAUSE
Incorrect configuration of the LDAP server could cause this issue.

ANSWER
Change the Base DN in LDAP configuration of StoredIQ for Legal to its smallest OU level.
