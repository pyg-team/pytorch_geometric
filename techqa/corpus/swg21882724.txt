Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in IBM DB2 Enterprise Server Edition shipped with IBM Cloud Orchestrator and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator - United States

Text:
CVE-2016-0729; CVE-2016-4463; CVE-2016-5995; CVE-2016-0215; CVE-2015-4872; CVE-2015-4893; CVE-2015-4803; CVE-2016-0201; CVE-2015-7420; CVE-2015-7421; CVE-2015-0138; CVE-2015-0159; CVE-2014-6221; CVE-2014-8730; CVE-2015-2808; CVE-2015-1788; CVE-2015-4000. CVE-2015-0204; CVE-2015-1947; CVE-2016-0201; CVE-2015-7420; CVE-2015-7421 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 IBM DB2 Enterprise Server Edition is shipped as a component of IBM Cloud Orchestrator, IBM Cloud Orchestrator Enterprise, IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator, and IBM SmartCloud Orchestrator Enterprise.
