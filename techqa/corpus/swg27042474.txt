iPad related troubleshooting documents for Process Portal in IBM Business Process Manager 

A full screen button is not available for task completion in Process Portal for IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) on Apple iPad (1664795) [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21664795&acss=dakc] 
Versions: 8.0.1 and 8.5 
You use a web browser on a desktop or laptop device to display a coach in Process Portal during task completion. A full screen button is available in the upper right corner of the coach view, which enables you to expand the coach view to cover the entire browser window. On an Apple iPad device, however, the full screen button is not available. 

Some IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) tools do not work correctly when used with the Google Chrome or Apple iPad Safari browsers and the Simplified Chinese (GB18030) character set (1616902) [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21616902&acss=dakc] 
Versions: 8.0.1, 8.5 and 8.5.5 
If you are using Google Chrome or Apple iPad Safari browsers to run web-based IBM Business Process Manager tools (for example, Process Portal or Process Center), and if you are using the Simplified Chinese (GB18030) character set, you might have issues with corrupted or missing input text and windows. 

IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) portal and coaches do not render on some browsers or mobile devices (1649366) [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21649366&acss=dakc] 
Versions: 8.0.1 
Remote browsers cannot access the IBM Business Process Manager Portal or coaches using an IP address. 



iPad related limitations for Process Portal in IBM Business Process Manager 

Notifications that you can expect in Process Portal 
Versions: 8.5.0.1 
You can receive notifications directly in Process Portal when an action occurs that affects you and your work, for example, when a new task is assigned to you or your user group, or you are mentioned in a post on the process. You can use settings in your Process Portal preferences to control which notifications you see. But this function is not supported for Safari on iPad. 

HTML5 Video is not playing on iPad 
Versions: 8.0.1 
Customer trying to add a .MP4 video file with HTML5 in a coach in BPM. Customer was loading video as a managed asset and video was only working on desktop and not on iPad iOS 6. But actually BPM does not support to add video file as a managed asset in current release. 



iPad related APARs for Process Portal in IBM Business Process Manager 

JR47212: PROCESS PORTAL BEHAVES SLOW ON APPLE IPAD 
Versions: 8.0.1.1, 8.0.1.2, 8.5.0.1 and 8.5.5 
Loading BPM Portal on the iOS takes longer than 2 minutes to load Process Portal page. 

JR48600: USING THE IPAD, CUSTOMER EXPERIENCES ISSUES USING THE KEYBOARD [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg1JR48600] 
Versions: 8.0.1.1 (JR48656), 8.0.1.2 (JR48893), 8.5.0.1 (JR49642) and 8.5.5 
The customer is experiencing problems when loading the Coach on the iPad. They tried the replicate scenario with 2 different iPads (they both had different OS and specs) and both experienced the issue. Additionally, there were slight differences in the results. The iOS 7 shows the keyboard is shaking violently back and forth, whereas the iOS 6 shows the keyboard as non-responsive. It has been reported the customer is using the Safari browser. 

JR48145: PROCESS PORTAL DOES NOT HANDLE COACH RESIZING PROPERLY ON IPAD [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg1JR48145] 
Versions: 8.0.1.1, 8.0.1.2 and 8.5.5 
In Process Portal, on an iPad, an increase in the size of the coach (such as triggered by a change to the of the coach becoming inaccessible. 

JR49846: PROCESS PORTAL DOES NOT HANDLE RESIZING OF COACHES PROPERLY ON AN IPAD (ADDITIONAL CASE - EXTENSION OF APAR JR48145) [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg1JR49846] 
Versions: 8.0.1.2 and 8.5.5 
On an iPad, after a transition from a shorter coach to a taller coach, the taller coach is truncated at the bottom due to an incorrect height calculation.
