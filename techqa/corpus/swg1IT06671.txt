APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When attempting to create a checkpoint of a large domain the
   operation might time out.  The WebGUI shows "Permission denied"
   error.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customers creating exports of their large domains might fail to
   create the checkpoint when the domain is too large to complete
   within the export timeout.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Fix is available in 6.0.0.14, 6.0.1.10, 7.0.0.7 and 7.1.0.5.
