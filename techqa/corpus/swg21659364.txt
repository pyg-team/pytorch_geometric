Title: IBM Please be aware that DataPower SOA Appliances Hardware Machine Type (M/T) 9235  is no longer supported.  End of service date was March 31, 2014 unless there was a Service Extension in place - United States

Text:
 FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 The DataPower SOA Appliances Hardware Machine Type (M/T) 9235 reached the end of standard software support on March 31, 2014. 

CONTENT
IBM Software Maintenance for 9235 IBM WebSphere DataPower SOA Appliances clients, including those with an ELA, are required to purchase a Service Extension if they wish to be supported after the March 31, 2014 date. 

The Service Extension is a fee-based offering for customers extended support requirements. It has limited terms and conditions, such as requiring to be on DataPower V6.0 firmware before the end of Standard Software support (March 31, 2014) in order to qualify for extended support from March 31, 2014 to March 31, 2016.
