PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  SAP may have the HR recorded locked at the time
    the TIM SAP Adapter attempts to establish the HR linking. This
   may cause the linkage to fail, but this missing linkage will not
    be detected on TIM until the next Recon.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION

TEMPORARY FIX
 *  Beta version of the SAP Netweaver adapter has bee
   n provided.
   
   
    
   
   

COMMENTS
 *  The new architecture of the SAP Adapter, SAP Netweaver
    Adapter, will be available on Dec 8, 2008 and will not contain
   this limitation.
