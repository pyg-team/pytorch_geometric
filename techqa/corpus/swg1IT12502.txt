APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  WSRR automatic synchronization method may cause the appliance
   to restart unexpectedly.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Use manual synchronization method.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Affected is use of WSRR.
   
   Refreshing WSRR subscription by regularly scheduled WSRR queries
    may result in appliance restart while changing WSRR
   subscription configurations.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  Fix is available in 7.0.0.13, 7.1.0.10, 7.2.0.6 and 7.5.0.2
