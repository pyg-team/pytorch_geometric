Title: IBM listIU output for a TIP 2.2 install does not show the correct eWAS fix pack - United States

Text:
TIPL2; TIPL2INST; listIU; Tivoli Integrated Portal; 2.2; eWAS; fix pack TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why doesn't the listIU output for a Tivoli Integrated Portal 2.2 installation show the same eWAS fix pack version that is actually installed after a TIP 2.2 fix pack is applied? 

ANSWER
As of TIP 2.2, eWAS fix packs are no longer registered in the Deployment Engine registry.
