ABSTRACT
 Information on support for the WebSphere Cast Iron Cloud integration products including the Cloud, Appliance, and Virtual Appliance. 

CONTENT
Welcome to WebSphere Cast Iron Support. This technote provides links to more information that can help you avoid opening service requests with IBM Support. Use the documents referenced here to answer questions about your appliance or virtual appliance and shorten the time to resolution if you need to open a service request.
