Title: IBM Confidential Matters are not accessible for system administrator role users. - United States

Text:
atlas; confidential TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does Confidential Matters in Atlas are not accessible for system administrator role users ? 

CAUSE
Confidential Matters are created by legal for highly confidential legal matters. So, providing access to these matters are restricted .

ANSWER
By default System Administrator or a user with this role will not be to access Confidential Matters. This is expected behavior. 

In order for System Administrator and any user with that role to be part of this Confidential Matter Access then they must be added to Admin --> Confidential Matter Access.
