Title: IBM License error 4 when launching Modeler 13.0 via redundant license servers. - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 I have setup a redundant license for Modeler 13.0. When I attempt to launch Modeler, I will get the following error:

License Error: Miscellaneous ELAN-related error. Error sub-code: 4

If I enable the commuter licenses for all of my redundant license servers, I can check out a license with my Modeler 13.0 client and launch the program sucessfully. 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
This problem has been reported to SPSS Development and is fixed in Modeler 14.0.1. - Workaround Availabl
