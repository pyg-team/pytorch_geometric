Title: IBM Rational System Architect 11.4 floating license expiry - United States

Text:
Expired floating license key; fixed term license key TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does an IBM Rational System Architect 11.4 floating license expire? 

CAUSE
An expired license message occurred when attempting to use a Rational System Architect 11.4 floating license key.

ANSWER
If you purchased a Fixed Term floating license key then your licenses will have a start and end date. The end date is the expiration date.
