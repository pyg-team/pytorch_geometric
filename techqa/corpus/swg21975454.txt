Title: IBM Is WebSphere Transformation Extender 8.4.1.x compatible with AIX 7.2 - United States

Text:
AIX compatible 7.2 tested TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is WebSphere Transformation Extender 8.4.1.x compatible with AIX 7.2 

CAUSE
AIX 7.2 was released after the General availability date of WTX 8.4.1.x.

ANSWER
AIX 7.2 was released after the General availability date of WTX 8.4.1.x so WTX 8.4.1.x was not tested using AIX 7, however IBM expects no issues when using this configuration..

WTX 8.4.1.x was tested using AIX 7.1. 

Detailed system requirements for WTX 8.4.1.x on AIX can be found using the following link.
