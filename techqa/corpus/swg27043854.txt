Title: IBM Atlas Policy Suite V6.0.3 Fix Pack 2 Release Notes - United States

Text:
 RELEASE NOTES

ABSTRACT
 IBM Atlas Policy Suite Version V6.0.3 Fix Pack 2 is available. Compatibility, installation, and other getting-started issues are addressed in these release notes. 

CONTENT
IBM Atlas Policy Suite is part of IBM Information Lifecycle Governance solutions, which enable you to efficiently retain and archive information of value and effectively meet eDiscovery obligations across both structured and unstructured data. With other IBM Information Lifecycle Governance products, IBM Atlas Policy Suite enables the systematic and defensible disposal of unnecessary data, which reduces storage growth, reduces IT and legal costs, and lowers legal risk for organizations.


For brief descriptions of new features, see the topic "What's new in IBM Atlas Policy Suite V6.0.3 Fix Pack 2?" in Planning, Installing, and Upgrading IBM Atlas Policy Suite(Planning_Installing_and_Upgrading_IBM_Atlas_Policy_Suite.pdf)

Known issues 

Known issues are documented in separate technotes in the Support knowledge base at http://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/overview/software/enterprise_content_management/altas_ediscovery/atlas_ediscovery_support_(general) [http://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/overview/software/enterprise_content_management/altas_ediscovery/atlas_ediscovery_support_(general)]. The following known issues were discovered in the product before its release: 

 * Known issue: Uninstalling IBM Atlas Policy Suite V6.0.3 requires manual file deletion [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27043716] 
 * Known issue: Uninstalling the IBM Policy Atlas V6.0.3.2 fix pack also uninstalls any previously installed fix packs [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27044002] 
 * Known issue: Information for running the script to associate orgnames with person records in a DB2 environment is missing [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27044134] 
 * Known issue: In Internet Explorer, when adding recipients to a matter, the X icon next to the ninth recipient is not shown [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27044460] 
 * Known issue: Matters > List tab # Custodians on Hold value differs from the Who Is On Hold report Total Number of Records value for the same matter [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27044186] 
 * Preventing "unresponsive script" warnings and system errors that can occur when you view a large number of notes on the collection plan or preservation plan Notes tab [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27044173]
