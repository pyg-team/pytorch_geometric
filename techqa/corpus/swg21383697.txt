Title: IBM Why am I getting error OPT1790 in my RMC report - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 RMC runs but reports OPT1790 errors 

SYMPTOM
RMC report showing OPT1790 errors 

Joblog also has OPT1790 error messages


CAUSE
Operation not allowed or conflicts with another request.


DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
See the reason code supplied in the joblog for OPT1790. This will provide additional details as to why the operation was not allowed or conflicted with another request



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The cause of the conflict based on the reason code needs to be eliminated. Research may be needed to determine what other activities are running at the same time. DSPLOG is a good tool to use
