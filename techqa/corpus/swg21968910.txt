Title: IBM packagedowngrade.sh shows "semodule:  Failed!" massage when doing Red Hat downgrade on NEC Orca system - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 On NEC Orca ZA50 system, packagedowngrade.sh would show "semodule: Failed!" massage when doing Red Hat downgrade. (RHEL ver. 5.8 to 5.7 & 5.7 to 5.5) 

ANSWER
When doing Red Hat downgrade on NEC Orca system , please implement following workaround before taking action (RHEL ver. 5.8 to 5.7 & 5.7 to 5.5)
Workaround:
a) Before downgrade from RHEL 5.8 to 5.7 
a-1. rm -rf /etc/selinux/targeted/modules/active/modules/mcelog.pp 
a-2. rm -rf /etc/selinux/targeted/modules/active/modules/zarafa.pp 
a-3. semodule -B 

b) Before downgrade from RHEL 5.7 to 5.5 
b-1. rm -rf /etc/selinux/targeted/modules/active/modules/piranha.pp 
b-2. semodule -B
