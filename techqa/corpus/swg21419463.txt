RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
- Go to the Report Administration application. 

 - Open the Report record wotrack_costanalysis.rptdesign . 

 - Select Action / Import Report. 

 - Re-Import wotrack_costanalysis.rptdesign under <maximohome>\reports\birt\reports\WOTRACK . 

 - Once it completes normally, click on the "Generate Request Page" button.

The problem will be fixed.
