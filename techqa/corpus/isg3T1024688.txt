Title: IBM Enable Encryption in Symphony WEBGUI - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

THIS DOCUMENT APPLIES ONLY TO THE FOLLOWING LANGUAGE VERSION(S):
 US English 

QUESTION
 How to enable Encryption in Symphony WEBGUI? 

CAUSE
In order to enhance the security of WEBGUI service, you may need to enable encryption on its HTTPS connection.

ANSWER
Following is a procedure to enable 4 protocols: SSL3.0, TLS1.0, TLS1.1, and TLS1.2. 

1. Stop WEBGUI service 
-- egosh service stop WEBGUI 
-- egosh service list 

2. Modify kernel/conf/wsm.conf as below, and pay attention to the parameter of Dhttps.protocols 
"-Dcom.ibm.jsse2.disableSSLv3=false 
"JVM_ARGS="-Xmx4096m -Xshareclasses:nonpersistent 
-Dhttps.protocols=SSL_TLSv2 
-Dcom.ibm.jsse2.disableSSLv3=false 
-Djava.library.path=../3.3/${BINARY_TYPE}/lib/jni:../soam/7.1.1/${BINARY_TYPE}/lib64:../3.3/${BINARY_TYPE}/lib -DKC_HOME=3.3/kcci" 

3. Modify gui/conf/server_gui.xml as below 
<ssl id="defaultSSLSettings" keyStoreRef="defaultKeyStore" sslProtocol="${https.protocols}" /> 
<sslDefault sslRef="defaultSSLSettings"/> 

4. Modify jre/3.3/linux-x86_64/lib/security/java.security 
-- jdk.tls.disabledAlgorithms=RC4, DH keySize < 768 

5. Restart service WEBGUI 
-- egosh service list 
-- egosh service start WEBGUI 

6. Verfication step 
1) Use IE 11, "Internet options" > "Advanced" > Remove other ciphers, only select "SSL3.0". The browser can access the WEBGUI service. 
2) And one by one try with all of the other protocols: TLS 1.0, TLS 1.1, TLS 1.2. 


Note: following is a list of the value can be configured in step 2. 

1) TLSv1.2 Enables TLS v1.2 protocol only 
2) SSL_TLS Enables all SSL V3.0 and TLS 1.0 protocols 
3) SSL_TLSv2 Enables all SSL V3.0 and TLS 1.0, 1.1 and 1.2 protocols
