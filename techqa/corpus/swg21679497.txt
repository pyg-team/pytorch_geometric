Title: IBM WebSeal multiple OCSP requests - United States

Text:
GSKIT; CRL; OCSP ; CDP TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 WebSeal makes multiple OCSP requests when a client certificate has intermediate CA in its chain. Is this expected? 

ANSWER
Yes it is the expected . 

If certificate revocation is enabled and OCSP responder is configured in WebSeal configuration file or if AIA points are defined in the intermediate CA certs than WebSeal tries to verify revocation status for each certificate in the chain except for the Root CA
