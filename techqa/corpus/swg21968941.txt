 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I navigate the IBM SPSS License Key Center in order to create, manage or view my existing SPSS licenses? 

CAUSE
Using the IBM License Key Center

ANSWER
The license key center is a secure site. Only the Primary contact (generally the person who purchased your product), site technical contact, or their designated contacts are able to access it.


If you already have access navigate to: https://www.ibm.com/analytics/us/en/spss/spss-support/ [https://developer.ibm.com/predictiveanalytics/get-help/] 
- Select SPSS Statistics Version 25, 24, 23... Scroll down to Licensing section - Get your license key 


Click on Button: "Visit the License Key Center" or use the direct link below:
https://spss.subscribenet.com/control/ibmp/login [https://spss.subscribenet.com/control/ibmp/login]

If you continue to have issues, please review our SPSS licensing forum https://developer.ibm.com/answers/topics/spsslicensing.html [https://developer.ibm.com/answers/topics/spsslicensing.html]
