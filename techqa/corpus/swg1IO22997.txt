 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Field at a time interface does not allow populating multiple
   fields with a lookup.
   Putting a lookup on a field that should populate multiple fields
   does not work in the Field at a time interface.
