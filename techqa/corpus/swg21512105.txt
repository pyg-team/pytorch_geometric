Title: IBM OMEGAMON XE z/OS 1.13 product currency support PTFs - United States

Text:
z/OS 1.13 Currency OMEGAMON OMEGAMON_ZOS_PRODUCT_CURRENCY FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 OMEGAMON currency support for z/OS 1.13 is provided for Version 410, 420 and later releases of OMEGAMON XE z/OS products. This document will help you determine currency support requirements for IBM Tivoli OMEGAMON products that run on z/OS. 

CONTENT
OMEGAMON z/OS 1.13 currency support is provided for Versions 410, 420 and later releases of OMEGAMON XE z/OS products. The information in this posting will help you plan and determine currency support requirements for OMEGAMON products that run on z/OS. If an OMEGAMON XE z/OS product or FMID is not listed in the matrix below then no APAR or PTF is required for currency support of this z/OS release.
The APARs and PTFs listed in the table below are available today.
