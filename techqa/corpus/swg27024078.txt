Title: IBM Atlas Suite V6.0 Administration: Populating Atlas with Business Data - United States

Text:
 PRODUCT DOCUMENTATION

ABSTRACT
 These guides tell you how to populate IBM Atlas Suite with your business data -- organizations, employees, legal matters, retention policies, and so on. There are two ways to populate Atlas with your business data: through a CSV file and through the Atlas Extensions application.
