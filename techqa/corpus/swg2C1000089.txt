Title: IBM Android Client SDK not being activated from Android service - United States

Text:
api; android TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Can the IBM MobileFirst Platform Foundation / IBM Worklight APIs (application program interface) be activated from within an Android Service? 

ANSWER
No. 

The IBM MobileFirst Platform Foundation / IBM Worklight APIs can not be activated from within an Android Service.
