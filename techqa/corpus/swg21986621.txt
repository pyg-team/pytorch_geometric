Title: IBM What encryption algorithm is used to secure passwords in the process files in TCQ? - United States

Text:
sterling connect:direct; C:D; windows; unix; strong password encryption; SPE; passwords; password TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 The temporary files used to store process details in the TCQ contain encrypted password field. What encryption algorithm is used to secure these passwords in the process files in TCQ? 

ANSWER
IBM Sterling Connect:Direct uses Strong Password Encryption (SPE), which is enabled by default, to secure all passwords at rest. SPE uses AES256 to do this.
