ABSTRACT
 This presentation describes the firmware upgrade process for a WebSphere DataPower 7993/9003 SOA appliance from 3.6.1 to the newer 3.7.x and 3.8 firmware, which requires an intermediate firmware upgrade. The presentation also references migration tips to consider during migration and testing.


Level of Difficulty: Intermediate 

Presenter(s): Robert Bunn, Rolf Wittich & Colt Gustafson

DATE: 17 June 2009
