Title: IBM Security Bulletin: IBM WebSphere MQ 7.0.1 potential denial of service (CVE-2015-2013) - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 IBM WebSphere MQ 7.0.1 is susceptible to a client issuing an MQI call that could cause the channel agent to abend, causing a denial of service to other channels that are running under the same MCA process. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
CVEID: CVE-2015-2013 [http://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2015-2013]
DESCRIPTION: IBM WebSphere MQ could allow a client to crash the channel agent when sending a specially crafted selection string.
CVSS Base Score: 4.3
CVSS Temporal Score: See http://xforce.iss.net/xforce/xfdb/103924 [http://xforce.iss.net/xforce/xfdb/103924] for the current score
CVSS Environmental Score*: Undefined
CVSS Vector: (AV:N/AC:M/Au:N/C:N/I:N/A:P) 

AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
IBM WebSphere MQ 7.0.1 

IBM WebSphere MQ 7.0.1.12 and earlier maintenance levels

REMEDIATION/FIXES
Apply the fix for APAR IV73860, which is shipped in IBM WebSphere MQ 7.0.1.13 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21960691]
