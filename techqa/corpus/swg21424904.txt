Title: IBM How to regenerate a missing repository.xml for an offering location - United States

Text:
PU; repository.xml; missing TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
How do you use the Packaging Utility to regenerate a missing repository.xml in your offering repository location? 

ANSWER
 

 1. Download the attached offering - RegenerateRepoXML.zip - to your desktop.
    
    
 2. Launch Packaging Utility
    
    
 3. Click Copy Packages > Open the downloaded offering > Next
    
    [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21424904&aid=1] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21424904&aid=1]
    
    
 4. Add the offering to the repository location that does not have the repository.xml file
    
    
 5. Click Copy
    
    Ignore the error message: "The Copy operation completed with errors."
