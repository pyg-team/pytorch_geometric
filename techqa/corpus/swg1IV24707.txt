APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Interim Fix 6 for TCR 2.1.1 on all supported platforms which was
   publsihed initially had some regressions reported by one of the
   adopters where the TCR Template and the skinning on top of
   Cognos were lost after applying this Interim Fix. This was
   because of the loss of customizations TCR does on a Cognos
   configuration file, which was overwritten by Cognos during their
   fixpack (FP4) installation by TCR.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Republish the IFix6 package after fixing the issues.
