There are multiple vulnerabilities in IBM Runtime Environment Java Technology Edition, Version 6 that is used by IBM Content Collector. This Fix Pack addresses these vulnerabilities, which are CVE-2015-7575 (CVSS 4), CVE-2016-0448 (CVSS 4), CVE-2016-5597 (CVSS 5.9), CVE-2016-5542 (CVSS 3.1), and CVE-2016-3455
