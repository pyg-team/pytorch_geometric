Title: IBM Error message "Too many open files" - United States

Text:
STERLINGNFX TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Error message "Too many open files" in the Unix server were CEHTTP and TOMCAT is installed.
TOMCAT Log message:
java.io.FileNotFoundException: 
/centerprise/apache/apache-tomcat-5.5.25/work/Catalina/localhost/tomcat-docs/SESSIONS.ser (Too many open files)
SEVERE: IOException while saving persisted sessions: java.io.FileNotFoundException: 
/centerprise/apache/apache-tomcat-5.5.25/
work/Catalina/localhost/tomcat-docs/SESSIONS.ser (Too many open files) 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
It is not a Sterling Connect:Enterprise for UNIX problem, but an environment problem. 

This message is coming from TOMCAT. 

It means the exceeded number of open files that is configured to an user. 

It is limited by nofiles parameter. 

$ ulimit -an
time(seconds) unlimited
file(blocks) unlimited
data(kbytes) unlimited
stack(kbytes) 8192
coredump(blocks) unlimited
nofiles(descriptors) 256
memory(kbytes) unlimited
