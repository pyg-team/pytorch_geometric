Title: IBM JR43096: Migrated BPD Instances, which are stopped at a UCA, can not be executed after they are migrated from WLE 7.2.0.2 to BPM 7.5.1. - United States

Text:
  A FIX IS AVAILABLE
Version 7.5.1 Fix Pack 1 for the IBM Business Process Manager products [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24033036]


SUBSCRIBE
You can track all active APARs for this component.



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Migrated BPD instances, which are stopped at an UCA, can not be
   executed after they are migrated from WLE 7.2.0.2 to BPM 7.5.1.
   
   Before migration, there are some BPD instances stopped at an UCA
   (waiting for trigger condition to next activity).
   Post migration to 7.5.1, these BPD instances can not be
   executed any more.
