Title: IBM Windows 10 support on ICD 76 - United States

Text:
TPAESCCD; Windows 10; ICD76 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Does ICD 76 support Windows 10 on client machine? 

ANSWER
By default, Windows 10 comes with Microsoft Edge browser. This browser is supported with limitations on Maximo 7.6. 

It is recommended that you use IE11 on Windows 10 as this is supported on ICD 76.
