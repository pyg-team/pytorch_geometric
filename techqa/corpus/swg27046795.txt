CONTENT
IBM Business Process Manager is a comprehensive and consumable business process management platform that provides visibility and management of business processes. It includes tools and a runtime environment for process design, execution, and optimization. It is specially designed to enable process owners and business users to engage directly in the improvement of their business processes.
