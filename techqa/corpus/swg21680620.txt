Title: IBM Using WTX 8.4.1.1 with IIB 9.0 may result in ABEND S2C1 under load - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 An ABEND S2C1 may be experienced, resulting in an IIB Execution Group crash when the Broker is under load on the z/OS platform (64 bit) if the flows use IBM WebSphere TX nodes. 

SYMPTOM
ABEND S2C1 REASON=000000C4 is issued. On analysis of the ABEND it can be seen to be occurring in the 
__preFormat code. Register 10 is bad, as it's set to FFFFFFFF FF3D1370.


CAUSE
This has been identified as a product defect under APAR PI20801

ENVIRONMENT
Only occurs on z/OS platform(64 bit) when IIB 9.0 is using WTX 8.4.1.1 nodes

DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
Unwind the ABEND, to review the __preFormat code.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Obtain and apply fix for APAR PI20801
