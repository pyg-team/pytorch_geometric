APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When the DB server had failover, or stopped (then restarted),
   the following 2 types of symptoms occurred;
   A) Users were able to login, but the error appeared on the
   scanning screen. After they tried some times, the error
   disappeared and it worked well, without restarting Datacap
   service.
   B) Users were not able to login. After restarting Datacap
   service, they were able to login.
