Title: IBM Tivoli Monitoring 6.3.0 FP5 and  FP6 or later Post Installation Security Updates - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 This technote lists the additional updates to the Tivoli Enterprise Portal (TEP) and Tivoli Enterprise Portal Server (TEPS) that need to be installed to complete the remediation for the CVEs published at the time FP5 and FP6 were published. 

ENVIRONMENT
6.3.0 Fix Pack 6 or later Tivoli Enterprise Portal Server (TEPS) and Tivoli Enterprise Portal (TEP) 

6.3.0 Fix Pack 5 Tivoli Enterprise Portal Server (TEPS) and Tivoli Enterprise Portal (TEP)



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
 The updates are grouped by Fix Pack (e.g. ITM 6.30 FP5 and ITM 6.30 FP6 or later). Only apply the updates specific to the IBM Tivoli Monitoring Fix Pack which you have installed.
