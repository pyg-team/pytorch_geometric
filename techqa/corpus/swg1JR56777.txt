APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Following error can be seen in Process Center environment:
   CWSIA0067E: An exception was received during the call t
   o the method JmsMsgProducerImpl.sendMessage (#4):
   com.ibm.ws.sib.processor.exceptions.SIMPLimitExceededException:
   CWSIP0251E:
   The destination TWServerTopicDestination.SingleCluster on
   messaging engine SingleCluster.000-BPM.De1.Bus is not available
   because the high limit 50,000 for the number of messages for
   this destination has already been reached.
   Upon inspecting the topic contents one can see a lot of
   com.lombardisoftware.client.event.ClientActivitySummaryEvent
   messages.
   This may cause BPM to hang and make it unusable.
   
   PRODUCTS AFFECTED:
   IBM Business Process Manager (BPM) Advanced
   IBM BPM Standard
   IBM BPM Express
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Process Center restart may help temporarily.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  No additional information is available.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  A fix will be included in IBM BPM V8.5.7 cumulative fix 2016.12
   that prevents
   com.lombardisoftware.client.event.ClientActivitySummaryEvent to
   not stay in the queue forever.
   
   To determine whether the cumulative fix is available and
   download it if it is, complete the following steps on Fix
   Central:
   
   1. On the Select product tab, select WebSphere as the product
   group, IBM Business Process Manager with your edition from the
   WebSphere options, All as the installed version, and All as the
   platform, and then click Continue.
   2. In the Text field, enter "cumulative fix", and click
   Continue.
