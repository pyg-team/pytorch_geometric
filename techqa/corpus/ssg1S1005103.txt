Title: IBM Spectrum Accelerate: Sybase DB Dump running time may vary - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Sybase DB Dump backup running time may vary between different instances. 
For example, for the same system, it may take 1.5 hours at one time, and 8 hours on another. 

CAUSE
Sybase DB Dump checks how full a page is before dumping it.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Dump the whole database without determining which pages are allocated or not by execute the following command on Sybase ASE to achieve the faster backup.


sp_dumpoptimize "archive_space=maximum"
