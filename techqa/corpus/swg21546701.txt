STERLINGPRI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do I change a remote node from receive only to send and receive? (SCI38311) 

ANSWER
Change the adjacent.node in your network map to indicate send and receive.
ADJACENT.NODE=((nodename,1364,,TCP,INT,BOTH) This entry will allow both send and receives from the remote node.
