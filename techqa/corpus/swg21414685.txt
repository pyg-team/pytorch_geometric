Title: IBM Recommended fixes and settings for WebSphere Commerce V6.0 - United States

Text:
v16TOC PREVENTIVE SERVICE PLANNING

ABSTRACT
 A comprehensive list of recommended, generally available (GA) fixes for IBM WebSphere Commerce V6.0. 

CONTENT

Products that are applicable to this technote
This technote includes recommended fixes for WebSphere Commerce, and for WebSphere Application Server, WebSphere MQ and DB2 when used with WebSphere Commerce.

If you are an IBM i (iSeries) user, you should also review the PTF tables for WebSphere Commerce Version 6.0 on i5/OS V5R4, i5/OS V5R3, and System i V6.1 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21239904] technote.

Determining your current Fix Pack level
If you are unsure of the Fix Pack level you are running on, follow the technote Determining the Fix Pack level for WebSphere Commerce and its associated products [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21358358] for details.
