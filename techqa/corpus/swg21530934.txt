Title: IBM What is the maximum number of characters in the area for the dataset name in DMNOTIFY? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 What is the maximum number of characters in the area for the dataset name in DMNOTIFY? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The limitation is 44 characters unless the DMNOTIFY program from SAMPLIB is modified. In V5.1 and later the program is DGADTIFY in the SDGASAMP library.
