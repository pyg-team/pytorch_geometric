Title: IBM About the end of support announcement for Rational Developer for i for SOA Construction 7.5 and 8.0 - United States

Text:
rdi; soa; rbd; EOS NEWS

ABSTRACT
 This document highlights an announcement regarding IBM Rational Developer for i for SOA Construction 7.5.x and 8.0.x and documentation on upgrading your product to Rational Developer for i RPG and COBOL Tools + Modernization Tools, EGL Edition 9.0. 

CONTENT
An announcement has been made that extended support for Rational Developer for i for SOA Construction 7.5.x and 8.0.x will no longer be offered after April 30, 2015. You can find the announcement here: Software withdrawal and end of service: IBM Rational selected products [http://www.ibm.com/vrm/newsletter_10577_9002022_276979_email_DYN_1IN/am106425186] 

You can also find the status of the product versions in the Rational Software Support Lifecycle [http://www.ibm.com/software/rational/support/lifecycle/] site.
