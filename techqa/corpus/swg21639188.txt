Title: IBM Strange Characters Appear in P8 Health Page - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After launching Content Platform Engine (CPE) 5.2 and loading the P8 Health page, the page will display strange characters. 

SYMPTOM
Strange Characters Appear in P8 Health Page 

Example:
[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21639188&amp;aid=1]


CAUSE
Pseudo translation files used during testing have been built and shipped with CPE 5.2


DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
Open P8 Health page and look for strange characters like the ones in the screenshot above



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Contact IBM FileNet Support and open a PMR/CRM. 

Note that this problem only occurs for CPE 5.2 GA. This problem does not occur for subsequent CPE 5.2 Fixpacks
