IBM Business Process Manager is a comprehensive and consumable business process management platform that provides visibility and management of business processes. It includes tools and a runtime environment for process design, execution, monitoring, and optimization. It is specifically designed to enable process owners and business users to engage directly in the improvement of their business processes.

This document provides the eAssembly part numbers that are required to download the IBM Business Process Manager Advanced - Process Server Version 8.5 product from the Passport Advantage website [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm].

Downloading and assembling the product 

 1. Ensure that your workstation has sufficient space to store both the files that you must download from IBM Passport Advantage and the extracted installation image.
    
    
 2. Navigate to the Passport Advantage website [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm] and download all of the parts that you need. The parts are listed by platform in the tabbed sections of this document. 
    (For information about how to access Passport Advantage, see the Passport Advantage Quick reference guide in the Learn more section on the Passport Advantage website [http://www.ibm.com/software/howtobuy/passportadvantage/pao_customers.htm].) 
    
    
 3. Download all of the required parts for the product installation image from IBM Passport Advantage to a temporary directory.
    
    
 4. Extract the downloaded files to one directory. 
    
    
 5. Open the Quick Start Guide, which is located in the Quick Start image, to access the documentation to help with product installation.
