ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Turning logarchmeth off will cause log files not to be reused if
   at the time database was activated
   and it found that at least one of the tablespaces is in either

    - Restore pending
    - Recovery pending

    - restore in progress

    - Tablespace in progression in progress
