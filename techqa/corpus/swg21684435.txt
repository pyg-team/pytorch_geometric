abstract text
Arabic language support is available for BrassRing for: New UI, BrassRing Agency, and candidate-facing BrassRing Talent Gateways. Hebrew language support is available for Talent Gateways only. This document describes support exceptions and the screens that do not support right-to-left orientation.
