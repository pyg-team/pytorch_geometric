 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How can I obtain the BIOS password for my Guardium Appliance 

CAUSE
The Guardium Support Team keep a record of the passwords of all appliances that Guardium customers have so that they can perform certain tasks when needed, without compromising security.


You may deem it necessary that you have the BIOS password in order to rebuild an appliance or for some other reason.


ANSWER
 In order to obtain the BIOS password from the Guardium Support Team, as much of the following information should be supplied when logging a new Problem Management Record (PMR) 

 * 
 *  Brief reason for needing the BIOS password 
 * 
 * 
 * 
 *  Serial Number Service Tag MAC Address
