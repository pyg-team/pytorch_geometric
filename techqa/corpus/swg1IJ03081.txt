 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  PROBLEM: When client move a rotating asset from operating
   location from site A to inventory storeroom from site B using
   move /modify asset action GLCReditaccount is going null
   both in matrectrans and assettrans tables.
   
   PERFORMANCE ISSUE: N/A
   
   STEPS TO REPRODUCE:
   . GO TO >> ASSETS >> ASSETS
   . Create an asset in the BEDFORD site
   . Item is PUMP100
   . Location is BR200 (operating type)
   . Save the asset
   . SELECT ACTION >> MOVE/MODIFY ASSET
   . Expand the Asset record in Assets session
   . Change the site in "To Site" field to FLEET
   . Enter ATLANTA(storeroom type) in "To Location" field
   . Enter any GL Account in "To GL Account" field
   . Take a look at the GL Credit Account field and GL Debit
   Account are filled
   . Click OK and the successful move message displayed
   
   
   
   CURRENT ERRONEOUS RESULT: The GL Credit Account field it's not
   filled after Move/Modify Asset action.
   
   EXPECTED RESULT: Maximo have to copy the GL Credit Account when
   the rotating asset was being moved to a storeroom locations.
