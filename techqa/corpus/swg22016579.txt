Title: IBM Security Bulletin: Release of GDPR ready IBM Data Science Experience Local - United States

Text:
 SECURITY BULLETIN

SUMMARY
 IBM Data Science Experience Local new release is GDPR ready. 

VULNERABILITY DETAILS
For more information on GDPR and Data Science Experience Local, see IBM Data Science Experience Local considerations for GDPR readiness [https://content-dsxlocal.mybluemix.net/docs/content/local/gdpr.html]

AFFECTED PRODUCTS AND VERSIONS
 

Principal Product and Version(s) IBM Data Science Experience Local: 1.1.0, 1.1.1, 1.1.2, 1.1.3, 1.2 REMEDIATION/FIXES
Upgrade to the latest Data Science Experience Local 1.2.0.3 release to be GDPR ready. 

You can download the latest release from Passport Advantage [https://www-01.ibm.com/software/passportadvantage/pao_customer.html].
