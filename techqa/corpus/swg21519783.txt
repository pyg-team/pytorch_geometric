Title: IBM Usage of the Maxdelay value parameter on Connect:Direct for OS400 or i5/OS on the iSeries - United States

Text:
STERLINGFAQ TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is the Maxdelay value parameter able to be used on Connect:Direct for OS400 or i5/OS on the iSeries? 

ANSWER
The Maxdelay parameter is not valid for the OS400 or i5/OS on the iSeries systems.
