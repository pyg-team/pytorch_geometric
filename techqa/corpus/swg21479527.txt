PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 What is the maximum number of columns, or variables, in SPSS Statistics? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The limit is 2 billion which, for most purposes, is "unlimited" for SPSS Statistics.

The largest number of columns that can be stored is 2**(31)-1 (2 to the 31st power minus one), or 2,147,493,647
