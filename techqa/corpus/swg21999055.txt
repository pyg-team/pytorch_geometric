Title: IBM Unable to view any reports under StoredIQ for Legal Reports tab - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why are we not able to view any reports under the StoredIQ for Legal Reports tab? 

CAUSE
Access to the cross matter reports are restricted by specific privileges

ANSWER
 

 1. Login as ilgadmin 
 2. Click on Roles and Privileges under Users and Roles 
 3. Edit the role of the user who is unable to view the reports 
 4. Select view check box for limited-access reports privilege 
 5. Save the changes
