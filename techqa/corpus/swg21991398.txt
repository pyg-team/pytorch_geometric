Title: IBM Datacap Navigator Plugin shows clear-text passwords in the log. - United States

Text:
clear text password; Datacap Navigator TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 In a certain situation, such as connection error, Datacap Navigator may log password in clear text in the application server log (eg: SystemOut.log). 

CAUSE
This is a know issue with Datacap Navigator 9.0.0 FP3 and below. This issue has been fixed in Datacap Navigator 9.0.1 and above.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
To resolve the issue:




1) Upgrade to the latest Datacap Navigator version. 

2) If you cannot upgrade to the latest version of Datacap, you may configure ICN to exclude the com.ibm.ecm.extension.datacap.wtm.wrapper.* class by 

 * 
 * 
 * b) Click Settings->Logging tab. Check "Specific classes" under Exclude, then enter com.ibm.ecm.extension.datacap.wtm.wrapper.* into the text box
