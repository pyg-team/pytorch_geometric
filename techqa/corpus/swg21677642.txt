Title: IBM Logging in to multiple portals on the same server by using the same browser at the same time causes unexpected behaviour - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 If you are logged in to one portal and then switch to another portal, in the same browser and on the same server, without logging out, you might experience unexpected behavior. 

SYMPTOM
After logging in to multiple portals at same time, you experience unexpected behavior.


CAUSE
Browser session handling causes issues when your are logged in to multiple portals at the same time.

ENVIRONMENT
IBM API Management V3.0.0.1

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Sign out from all portals and then log back in to only one portal.
