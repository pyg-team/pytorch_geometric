APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  The WebSphere Connect JDBC driver (found in WebSphere
   Application Server V6.1 and V6.0.2) has an error where "index
   out of range" exception is incorrectly reported on various
   ResultSet.getXXX methods after having previously invoked
   getMoreResults on the PreparedStatement.  Here is an example
   of the exception stack,
   
   java.sql.SQLException: [IBM][SQLServer JDBC
   Driver]Column index 1 is out of range.
   at com.ibm.websphere.jdbc.base.BaseExceptions.
   createException(Unknown Source)
   at com.ibm.websphere.jdbc.base.BaseExceptions.
   getException(Unknown Source)
   at com.ibm.websphere.jdbc.base.BaseResultSet.
   throwBadOrdinal(Unknown Source)
   at com.ibm.websphere.jdbc.base.BaseResultSet.
   getInt(Unknown Source)
   at com.ibm.websphere.jdbcx.base.BaseResultSetWrapper.
   getInt(Unknown Source)
   
   The APAR fix corrects the error such that getMoreResults
   does not lead to the "index out of range" error.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
