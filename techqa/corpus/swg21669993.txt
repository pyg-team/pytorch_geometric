Title: IBM Steps to respond on behalf of another custodian. - United States

Text:
recipient ; Atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 What are the steps required to respond on behalf of another custodian ? 

ANSWER
 

 1.  Login to Atlas as administrator and go to Admin --> Components. 
 2.  Access a Matter that you are the Attorney or Paralegal for. 
 3.  Search for Component called "Allow Paralegal or Attorney Confirmation" and make sure "Allow Any User to Respond" is set to 'Yes" and "Allow Paralegal or Attorney to Record A Confirmation" is set to "NO". 
 4.  Go to the Hold Notice that was sent to the custodian. 
 5.  In the Notices Sent section click on the Recipients link for the Original Notice or last Auto-reminder for which the custodian needs to respond to. 
 6.  On the Notice Recipients Log List screen you will see a check box next to the custodians for whom you can confirm on behalf of. 
 7.  Click the check box next to the custodian who you are confirming for. 
 8.  Click on the Confirm button will be displayed in the top right. 
 9.  Enter the reason describing why you need to confirm on behalf of the custodian. 
 10. Click Apply to save the confirmation.
