Title: IBM PI48258: SYNDICATION WIZARD VALIDATES INVALID URL RESULTING IN THE CREATI ON OF A SYNDICATION PAIR WITH WRONG URL - United States

Fixes integrated in WebSphere Portal 8.5.0.0 Combined Cumulative Fixes [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24037786]



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Syndication wizard validation wasn't strong enough to pick up a
   
   type of bad url that works for the wizard but would fail for the
   
   actual syndication pair.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  Syndication URLs can always be edited after pair creation by
   editing the syndicator or subscriber item.
