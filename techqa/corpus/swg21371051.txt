 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Where can I find the Cognos 8 Administration and Security Guide? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
The Cognos 8 Administration and Security Guide can be found in either the IBM Cognos 8 Business Intelligence or IBM Cognos 8 Planning - Server documentation in the Cognos 8|Documentation folder. 

Steps:1. Choose Start|All Programs|Cognos 8|Documentation and select either Cognos 8 Business Intelligence or Cognos 8 Planning - Server.
2. Scroll down and you will find a link for the Cognos 8 Administration and Security Guide near the bottom next to the heading 'Administering Cognos 8'.
