Title: IBM PI14116: MOVING A ITEM TO ANOTHER LIBRARY IS NOT SYNDICATED ON SUBSCRIBER - United States

Text:
  A FIX IS AVAILABLE
Fixes integrated in WebSphere Portal 8.0.0.1 Combined Cumulative Fixes [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24034497]



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Under certain circumstances, moved items may fail to syndicate
   to the subscriber server.
   
   An example scenario:
   1. Create two libraries:
   Lib1 --> with default item
   Lib2 --> empty library with no default items
   2. Setup a syndication pair and include both libraries with the
   All Items scope
   3. Wait for the syndication complete and then disable the
   syndicator
   4. Create two top-level site areas under Lib2
   Lib2
   + S1
   + S2
   5. Move the Articles site area from Lib1 to the root of Lib2
   6. Move S1 into Articles
   7. Move Articles into to S2
   8. Enable the syndication. Wait for complete
   
   Expected: The structure of Lib2 on subscriber will be
   Lib2
   + S2
   ++ Articles
   +++ Sample Article
   +++ Sample Article 2
   +++ S1
   
   Actual:
   Some of the items are missing unless the syndication is rebuilt.
   There are also no failed items and no errors in the logs.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  None available
