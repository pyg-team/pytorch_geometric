Title: IBM Regression introduced by APAR IT06286 in WebSphere Message Broker and IBM Integration Bus - United States

Text:
WMB IIB FLASH (ALERT)

ABSTRACT
 APAR IT06286 introduces a regression in WebSphere Message Broker v8 and IBM Integration Bus v9 & v10. 

CONTENT
APAR IT06286 introduces a regression in WebSphere Message Broker v8 and IBM Integration Bus v9 & v10 and is fixed under APAR IT11038. 

If you have APAR IT06286 applied or you have IBM Integration Bus v9.0.0.4, v10.0.0.0 or v10.0.0.1 installed, refer to APAR IT11038 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg1IT11038] for a description of the problem and evaluate its potential impact to your environment. 

If you are impacted and have Fix Pack 9.0.0.4 with no additional fixes applied then IT11038 is available for download from Fix Central on all distributed platforms from the following link:
http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/selectFixes?parent=ibm~WebSphere&product=ibm/WebSphere/Integration+Bus&release=All&platform=All&function=aparId&apars=IT11038 [http://www.ibm.com/support/fixcentral/swg/selectFixes?parent=ibm~WebSphere&product=ibm/WebSphere/Integration+Bus&release=All&platform=All&function=aparId&apars=IT11038] 

If you are impacted and require a combined fix, or you require a fix on z/OS, submit a request for the APAR via IBM support.
