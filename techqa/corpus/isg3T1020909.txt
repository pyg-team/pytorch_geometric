Title: IBM What does the error message "host remains closed due to job limit" mean? - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 When you run badmin hopen <hostname> it prints the following message:
$: badmin hopen hostA
Open <hostA> ...... done : host remains closed due to job limit;

You want to know what the error message means and how to fix it. 

ANSWER
It means the host is full and all job slots have been taken up. Once the slots are free the host will be opened automatically.
