Title: IBM Content Navigator with a FileNet repository, PDF and Tiff files fail to load in the viewer when being streamed by the IBM Daeja viewer. - United States

Text:
Viewer; Daeja; Navigator; streamer; streaming; FileNet viewer; ICN. TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Attempting to view PDF or Tiff files with the Streamer function enabled. 

SYMPTOM
The following error is displayed: 

[/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21962240&aid=1] [/support/docview.wss?uid=swg21962240&aid=1]


The error in the streamer log is: WebContainer : 6 Jul 7, 2015 10:34:50 AM (000001984/000000000): ViewOnePlatform.navigator.streamer> Sending error Error: Invalid document ID specified to client <ip addreess>


CAUSE
IBM Content Navigator is missing parameters for the IBM Daeja viewer streaming.


ENVIRONMENT
IBM Content Navigator 2.0.3.



DIAGNOSING THE PROBLEM
Review the streamer log: <Content Navigator Install path>/ECMClient/config/streamer.log
Look for the error: 'Error: Invalid document ID specified to client'.



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
 

 1. Log into IBM Content Navigator Admin Desktop.
    
    
 2. Go to the 'Daeja ViewONE' node.
    
    
 3. Click on the tab 'Document Streaming'.
    
    
 4.  Under 'Additional settings' click new.
    
    
 5. Add the following parameters and values:
    
    docIdPrototype=${originalDocURL}
    modifiedDocumentCheckMethod=uniqueid
    
    
 6. Save and Close.
    
    
 7. Log out of Navigator and log back into Navigator to test.
    
    note: You may need to stop the navigator jvm and clear the application server temp files.
