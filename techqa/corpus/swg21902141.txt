Title: IBM Scheduled reports stay in "Executing" - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 Scheduled reports begin to run and will sometimes complete successfully, but other times will remain in "Executing" and never complete. 

ENVIRONMENT
A VPN Connection exists between Cognos and the Data Source DB.

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Disable keep-alive on both ends of the VPN tunnel.
