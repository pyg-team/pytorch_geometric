PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  This APAR is included in Release 6.2.0.2. Please refer the
   release notes under
   http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27048751 [http://www-01.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27048751]
   PO04281, WorkItem:102159 - Authorization fails when more than
   300 characters are entered for authorization comments
   Previously when authorizing an application case, authorization
   failed if more than 300 characters were entered into the
   authorization comments field. This issue has now been resolved
   by increasing the size of the field to cater to a maximum of
   1957 characters.
