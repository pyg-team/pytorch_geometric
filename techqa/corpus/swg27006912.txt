ABSTRACT
 This document describes the minimum product levels that must be installed before opening a problem report with the IBM WebSphere Enterprise Service Bus support team.

Note: Your entitlement to support, if any, depends on your license, your maintenance agreements, or both, for WebSphere Enterprise Service Bus. Support is limited to your use of the relevant prerequisite with a supported WebSphere Enterprise Service Bus product.
