ABSTRACT
 This WebSphere Support Technical Exchange is designed to help customers determine why transactions are being routed to a particular CICS region. The emphasis is on setting up and interpreting CICSPlex SM (CPSM) Workload Manager Level Traces. This presentation was created in 2007, but most of the information still applies; however things like offsets might have changed. 

Level of Difficulty: Intermediate

Presenter(s): Michael Barrett and Tony Bertolozzi

DATE: 06 Dec 2007
