Title: IBM Atlas table to identify trasmission time of a Hold Notice when it is sent - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do you identify transmission time of a Hold Notice when it is sent? 

CAUSE
Identify how long it took to send a Hold Notice to all recipients

ANSWER
You can review the Atlas table NOTICEREMINDERSENT and using the NOTICE_TRANSMISSION_ID and DATESENT, you can build a query to list the transmission time for a specific type of a Notice
