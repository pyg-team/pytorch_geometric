Title: IBM Adding Approvers to IBM Atlas Release Notice - United States

Text:
Atlas Release Approver TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 How do you add approvers to IBM Atlas Release Notice? 

CAUSE
Add an approver to a Release Notice

ANSWER

1. Make sure that the role of the user has read privilege to Matters.

2. Add the role to Matter security group of the Matter where you are creating the release notice.

3. Add the Role to Admin > components > LEGAL NOTICE APPROVER component.
