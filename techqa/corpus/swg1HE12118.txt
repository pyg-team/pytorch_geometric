ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  When a custodian is added to a Collection Plan, instead of
   setting the INUSEINCOLLECTIONPLAN flag, the application instead
   sets the NOHOLD flag.  This causes the custodian to remain as
   Removable but not Releasable, and the custodian will never be
   released from the matter.
