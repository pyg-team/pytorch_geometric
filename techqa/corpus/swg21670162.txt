Title: IBM Publish button does not appear in hold notice - United States

Text:
release ; atlas TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Why does the Publish button does not appear on hold notice even after approving the notice by approver? 

CAUSE
If the notice is release type and if there is no recipients added to the notice, Publish button does not appear.

ANSWER
Add the recipients to the release notice will make the Publish button to appear.
