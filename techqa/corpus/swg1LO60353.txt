Title: IBM LO60353: LOTUS TRAVELER PROBLEM TO SEND AS BCC - United States

Text:
 SUBSCRIBE
You can track all active APARs for this component.



APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  Customer reported the recipients in BCC fields didn't get email
   from sender.
   
   
    
   
   

LOCAL FIX
 *  At least put one recipient on CC field.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM SUMMARY
 *  Customer reported that if there was no CC recipients mail sent
   from Nokia device would not get delivered to the BCC recipients.
   
   
    
   
   

PROBLEM CONCLUSION
 *  The Lotus Traveler client for Nokia devices was updated to
   handle this situation.
   
   
    
   
   

TEMPORARY FIX
 *  This fix will be available in Lotus Traveler 8.5.2.3 and any
   later releases.
   
   http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24019529 [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg24019529]
