Title: IBM Unable to login and configure Work Centers when using IE11 - United States

Text:
TPAESYSTEMUI TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 I am able to login and configure Work Centers with Firefox but not IE11. 

ANSWER
IE11 is not supported with Work Center 7.6.0.5 & 7.6.0.6. At this time the only browsers that are supported is Firefox 45 Chrome 47 & Microsoft Edge 20.
