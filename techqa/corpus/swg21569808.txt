Title: IBM Getting error "Method 'get_CoordinatedTasks' in type 'ibm.ctms.p8connector.util.SynchronizedDocument' from assembly 'P84xConnectorService, Version=0.0.0.0, Culture=neutral, PublicKeyToken=null' does not have an implementation." - United States

Text:
ICC TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 After starting Content Collector, "Method 'get_CoordinatedTasks' in type 'ibm.ctms.p8connector.util.SynchronizedDocument' from assembly 'P84xConnectorService, Version=0.0.0.0, Culture=neutral, PublicKeyToken=null' does not have an implementation. " occurred in P8 connector. 

SYMPTOM
The following error message is seen in the Content Collector P8 connector log: 

"Method 'get_CoordinatedTasks' in type 'ibm.ctms.p8connector.util.SynchronizedDocument' from assembly 'P84xConnectorService, Version=0.0.0.0, Culture=neutral, PublicKeyToken=null' does not have an implementation. " 


CAUSE
To connect to FileNet P8 5.0 or higher, Content Collector must be at least 2.2.0.1. 


ENVIRONMENT
Using repository of FileNet P8 5.0 or higher and Content Collector of version 2.2.0.0 or lower



RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
Apply Content Collector version 2.2 Fixpack 1. 

 * See related URL for download link and more information about product prerequisites.
