Title: IBM StoredIQ for Legal 2.0.1 Release Notes - United States

Text:
 RELEASE NOTES

ABSTRACT
 IBM StoredIQ for Legal Version 2.0.1 is available. Compatibility, installation, and other getting-started issues are addressed in these release notes. 

CONTENT
Description


IBM StoredIQ for Legal enables you to automate your legal hold notification and compliance process, and to optimize your preservation and collection process.

Known issues 

Known issues are documented in separate technotes in the Support knowledge base at the [https://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/support?brandind=enterprise%20content%20management]IBM Support Portal [https://www.ibm.com/support/entry/portal/support?brandind=enterprise]. The following known issues were discovered in the product before its release. 

 

 * Known issue: Scroll bar not displayed properly for lists when using Mozilla Firefox web browser on Apple Mac OS [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27047282] 
 * Known issue: Notice and interview editor problems and workarounds [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27047283] 
 * Known issue: Message schedule dates displayed when adding a custodian to a hold notice can be off by up to one day [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27047362] 
 * Known issue: Cannot restrict request scope when you refine a data box request that was created without custodians [http://www.ibm.com/support/docview.wss?uid=swg27047364]
