Title: IBM The Truth about coprocessors, precompilers and translators for CICS and DB2 with COBOL compilers - United States

Text:
COBOL; DB2; coprocessor; precompiler; translator WHITE PAPER

ABSTRACT
 SHARE presentation material for session 1553. 

CONTENT
This session explains what will and won't work in DB2 Version 8 (and DB2 9), CICS TS V2 and V3, how to migrate to coprocessor from a DB2 precompiler or CICS translator, and how to manage CCSID issues with COBOL and DB2. 

Document includes Hot topics: 

 * Can you precompile old COBOL with DB2 Version 8? 
 * Is the coprocessor different from the DB2 precompiler? 
 * How do you deal with Unicode, COBOL and DB2? 
 * Should you use CICS translator option COBOL2 or COBOL3? When?
