APAR STATUS
 * CLOSED AS PROGRAM ERROR.
    
   
   

ERROR DESCRIPTION
 *  In a HA environment scheduled deployments are cached by each
   node individually. Some nodes can have stale version of the
   cache. Users can be confused since depending on the node they
   are looking at they could be seeing a recent and accurate or old
   version of the cache.
