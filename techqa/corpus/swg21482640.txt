Title: IBM I'm using SPSS 17 with Clementine 12 and having problems. - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (TROUBLESHOOTING)

PROBLEM(ABSTRACT)
 I've installed SPSS 17 and attempting to use with Clementine 12. I'm running into various issues. Why is this? 

RESOLVING THE PROBLEM
SPSS 17 and Clementine 12 are not supported.
SPSS 17 will be supported with Clementine 13. 


Tech Support website; Clementine Product Compatibility Matrix:

SPSS Integration with Clementine
To use the SPSS integration features in Clementine 12 (that is the SPSS Procedure, Output and Custom Tables nodes), an SPSS 16 installation is required. 
To use the SPSS integration features in Clementine 11 (that is the SPSS Procedure and Output nodes), an SPSS 15 installation is required.
