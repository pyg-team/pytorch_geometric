Title: IBM Leap Second Information for DS8000 - United States

Text:
 TECHNOTE (FAQ)

QUESTION
 Is the DS8000 affected by the upcoming leap second addition? 

ANSWER

The DS8000 family has no known exposures to leap second changes, and no issues reported due to previous leap second changes.

These changes usually occur on June 30th or December 31st at midnight UTC when a second is added to adjust time to compensate for the actual rotation of the earth,

The next scheduled leap second adjustment is on Dec 31st 2016.
